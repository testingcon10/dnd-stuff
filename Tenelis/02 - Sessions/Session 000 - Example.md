# Session 0 — The Tavern at Tenelis

**Date:** 2026-01-01
**Players Present:** Alice, Bob, Charlie, Diana, Eve

## Previously...

*This is the first session — our heroes have yet to meet!*

## Summary

The party arrived independently at **The Gilded Gryphon**, a tavern in the coastal town of Tenelis. After a bar brawl broke out between local sailors and a gang of thugs, the five strangers found themselves fighting side by side. The tavern keeper, **Marta Voss**, thanked them and mentioned that the town has been plagued by disappearances near the old lighthouse. The party agreed to investigate together at dawn.

## Key Events

1. Each character arrived at The Gilded Gryphon for their own reasons
2. A gang of thugs called the **Iron Tide** started harassing patrons and demanding "protection" money
3. When the thugs threatened Marta, the party intervened — leading to a tavern brawl
4. After the fight, Marta offered free rooms for the night and told the party about the disappearances
5. A mysterious hooded figure was seen watching from a corner booth, then vanished
