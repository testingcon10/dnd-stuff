# Session 5

**Date:** 2/24/2026
**Players Present:**

## Previously...

The party befriended [[Aya]] and [[Grig]] in the [[Drayik]] shopping district. They discovered [[The Golds]] frequent the [[Boar Tusk Brewery]] nightly for cards and drinks, and killed 2 Gold Thug members with the help of [[Grig]] and the barkeep of the [[Red Elk]].

## Summary

The party entered the [[Boar Tusk Brewery]] and confronted a group of [[The Golds]]. A card game went sideways, leading to a full fight. The party killed [[Kay'Dara]], a lieutenant of [[The Golds]]. Before finishing off her henchmen, the party interrogated them and learned critical intel about the [[Drayik]] criminal underworld - the existence of the [[Avian Brotherhood]], [[The Golds]], [[The Feints]], and the [[Elven Mafia]]. Most importantly, they learned that a large group of [[Kenku]] occupy the catacombs beneath [[Drayik]] and are arming the criminal groups in the city.

9. Found a key on [[Kay'Dara]]'s body, used it to open a lockbox containing the [[Gambling Operations Manifest]] - a record of [[The Golds]]' gambling operations in taverns across [[Drayik]]
10. [[Netanyahu D. Kirkuenly|Netanyahu]] showed [[Aya]] proof that [[The Golds]] had been dealt with, fulfilling his promise from Session 4
11. [[Cassius Bellona|Cassius]] brought [[Grig]]'s body and gold to [[Aya]], asking that she give him a proper burial
12. Received a sealed envelope from a [[Kenku]] outside the [[Boar Tusk Brewery]]
13. Approaching the western bridge at night, [[Old Shell]] identified [[Eileen Whitebeak]] - sister of [[Corso]], leader of the Senin militia
14. [[Eileen Whitebeak|Eileen]] revealed the church often sends men to the catacombs, but they never return. She asked for help and warned we will die upon entrance
15. [[Eileen Whitebeak|Eileen]] is against violence - after the attack on [[Delmind]], she believes it won't change opinions of her people. Not on good terms with [[Corso]] due to his radical approach
16. Reasoned with [[Eileen Whitebeak|Eileen]] - she offered protection and an escort into the catacombs. Party unsure if it's an ambush
17. [[Eileen Whitebeak|Eileen]] says trust must be built first. Revealed [[Corso]] is obsessed with an ancient Senin prophecy - the old prophecy led to the genocide of her people. She refuses to follow that path as it will lead to more of her people dying
18. Supplies are difficult to exchange between the surface and the catacombs. [[Eileen Whitebeak|Eileen]] says to find [[Dimitri]] - if she can do this, she will prove to her people she can be trusted. She does not know what will happen after that with her brother
19. The story of [[The Three Sisters]] was mentioned - 150 years ago, a Senin boy named Alex was rumored to be a weapon, a bringer of the [[Mana Virus]]. He and [[The Three Sisters]] sought to find a cure. The Church of the Daughter was founded by Father Ellen, who lost his daughter to the virus and became a prophet and miracle worker. The sick had visions of him and he would cure those afflicted. The Three Sisters died and the church lived on
20. [[Netanyahu D. Kirkuenly|Net]] learned the food cost for the catacombs is 30 gold per day - [[Corso]] has the numbers. Asked [[Eileen Whitebeak|Eileen]] about food routes and vendors
21. [[Netanyahu D. Kirkuenly|Net]] tried casting [[Suggestion]] on one of [[Eileen Whitebeak|Eileen]]'s henchmen - this revealed her necklace is a [[Moon Necklace of Absorption]], which blocks [[Suggestion]]. Her group became very aggressive
22. [[Cassius Bellona|Cassius]] interjected in [[Kenku]], stating that what happens with [[Corso]]'s livelihood is the party's decision
23. [[August]], one of [[Eileen Whitebeak|Eileen]]'s henchmen, revealed the food trade routes:
    - [[The Vorge Plains]] - [[Kenku]] farmers there supply food, but not a primary source. Hard for a [[Kenku]] farmer to make a living there
    - [[Little Hill]] - largest food source, but supply has stopped
    - Mountains of [[Delmind]] - some farmland, but not bountiful and unreliable
24. [[Booker Locke|Booker]] revealed he has a twin sister, [[Gwen Locke|Gwen]], who was kidnapped 6 years ago in [[Drayik]]. His parents were murdered by the Senin. He received a package in the mail stating the Senin has information on her
25. [[Booker Locke|Booker]] handed [[Eileen Whitebeak|Eileen]] the note about his sister. She says she does not know [[Gwen Locke|Gwen]]. The party declared nothing will happen until information about [[Gwen Locke|Gwen]] is provided
