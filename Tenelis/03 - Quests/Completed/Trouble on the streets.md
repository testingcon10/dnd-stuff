## Description
The once bustling shopping district now lies deserted. [[The Golds]] have taken over many of the shops and have put many out of business. Dealing with them may bring life back into the shopping district.

## Summary

The party tracked [[The Golds]] to the [[Boar Tusk Brewery]] with the help of [[Aya]] and [[Grig]]. A card game with the gang went sideways, leading to a full fight. The party killed [[Kay'Dara]], a lieutenant of [[The Golds]], and interrogated her henchmen before finishing them off. [[Grig]] was killed in the fight by a half-orc henchman. A key found on [[Kay'Dara]]'s body opened a lockbox containing the [[Gambling Operations Manifest]] - a record of [[The Golds]]' gambling operations across [[Drayik]]. [[Netanyahu D. Kirkuenly|Netanyahu]] showed [[Aya]] proof that [[The Golds]] had been dealt with, and [[Cassius Bellona|Cassius]] brought [[Grig]]'s body and gold to [[Aya]] for a proper burial.
