## Description

A [[Kenku]] approached the party outside the [[Boar Tusk Brewery]] and handed them a sealed envelope. The envelope led the party to the western bridge of [[Drayik]], where they met [[Eileen Whitebeak]] - sister of [[Corso]], leader of the Senin militia.

## Summary

The party met [[Eileen Whitebeak]] at the western bridge. She revealed the church often sends men into the catacombs beneath [[Drayik]], but none return. She offered the party protection and an escort into the catacombs, but first asked them to find [[Dimitri]] to prove her trustworthiness to her people. She shared intel on food trade routes ([[The Vorge Plains]], [[Little Hill]], mountains of [[Delmind]]) and the story of [[The Three Sisters]] and the [[Mana Virus]]. [[Netanyahu D. Kirkuenly|Net]] tried casting [[Suggestion]] on one of her henchmen, exposing her [[Moon Necklace of Absorption]]. [[Booker Locke|Booker]] revealed his twin sister [[Gwen Locke|Gwen]] was kidnapped by the Senin and demanded information. The party now has a tentative alliance with [[Eileen Whitebeak|Eileen]], contingent on finding [[Dimitri]] and getting answers about [[Gwen Locke|Gwen]].
