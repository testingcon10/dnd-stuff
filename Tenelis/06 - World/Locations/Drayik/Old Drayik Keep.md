## Description

A fortified keep in [[Drayik]], formerly the residence of the Knights of Drayik. Now controlled by the church. Heavily guarded with church military - guards, soldiers sparring and training. Civilians are not allowed entry.

## Notes

- Formerly home to the Knights of Drayik
- Now church-controlled
- Contains a records room (highly confidential, access denied)
- Contains a library (party granted access by [[Payton Hightower]])
- Heavily guarded - civilians not permitted
- May contain an entrance to the [[Catacombs]] - but entry has been impossible (possibly magic, curse, enchanted door). Immune to spells
- Was used as ground zero during the [[Mana Virus]] quarantine - served as a major hospital for treating the afflicted
- Those who spent enough time with the [[Mana Virus]] were sealed away in the keep, in the [[Catacombs]]
- Founded by [[Avo Red]], the founding knight of the Knights of Drayik
