## Description

A network of catacombs beneath [[Drayik]] occupied by a large group of [[Kenku]]. The [[Kenku]] are using the catacombs as a base to arm criminal groups across the city.

Church expeditions theorize that the [[Tri Rivers]] flow underground beneath [[Drayik]], creating the catacomb network. However, insufficient research/data exists due to lack of expeditions.

## Known Access

- [[Uzog]], [[Nath]], and [[Von]] have access
- [[Old Drayik Keep]] may contain an entrance - but getting in has been impossible (possibly magic, curse, or enchanted door). Immune to spells
- Coastal caves may provide an alternative lead - cave system outside [[Drayik]], rough location known but not pinpointed
- Human trafficking routes bypass the docks, suggesting an alternative entrance to the city that avoids detection
- Underground river tributary flows beneath the shopping district ([[Red Elk]]? [[Boar Tusk Brewery]]? - needs exploration)
- Multiple basements in [[Drayik]] have underground connections (noted by [[Netanyahu D. Kirkuenly|Net]])
