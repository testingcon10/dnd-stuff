# The Three Sisters

## The Story

Approximately 150 years ago, a Senin boy named Alex was rumored to be a weapon — a bringer of the Mana Virus. Alex and the Three Sisters sought to find a cure for the virus.

During this time, Father Ellen lost his daughter to the Mana Virus. He became a prophet and miracle worker — the sick would have visions of him and he would cure those afflicted with the virus. This led to the founding of the Church of the Daughter.

The Three Sisters died, and the church lived on.
