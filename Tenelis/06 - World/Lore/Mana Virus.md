# Mana Virus

A virus that afflicted people approximately 150 years ago. A Senin boy named Alex was rumored to be a bringer of the virus. Alex and the [[The Three Sisters|Three Sisters]] sought to find a cure.

Father Ellen, who lost his daughter to the virus, became a prophet and miracle worker who could cure those afflicted. This led to the founding of the Church of the Daughter.
