# Aura of Life

Life-preserving energy radiates from you in a 30-foot radius aura. Until the spell ends, the aura moves with you, centered on you. Each nonhostile creature in the aura (including you) has resistance to necrotic damage, and its hit point maximum can't be reduced. In addition, a nonhostile living creature regains 1 hit point when it starts its turn in the aura with 0 hit points.
