# Intellect Fortress

For the duration, you or one willing creature you can see within range has resistance to psychic damage, as well as advantage on Intelligence, Wisdom, and Charisma saving throws.

## At Higher Levels
When you cast this spell using a spell slot of [[4th Level|4th level]] or higher, you can target one additional creature for each slot level above 3rd.
