# Dispel Magic

Choose one creature, object, or magical effect within range. Any spell of 3rd level or lower on the target ends. For each spell of 4th level or higher, make an ability check using your spellcasting ability (DC = 10 + the spell's level). On a success, the spell ends.

## At Higher Levels
When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 4th level or higher, you automatically end effects of a spell on the target if the spell's level is equal to or less than your slot level.
