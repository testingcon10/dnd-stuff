# Holy Aura

Divine light washes out from you in a 30-foot radius, granting friendly creatures within the area advantage on all saving throws and imposing disadvantage on attack rolls against them. Additionally, if a fiend or undead hits an affected creature with a melee attack, the aura flashes with blinding light, forcing the attacker to succeed on a Constitution saving throw or be blinded until the spell ends.
