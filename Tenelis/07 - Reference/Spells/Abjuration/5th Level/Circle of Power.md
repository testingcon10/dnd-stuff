# Circle of Power

Divine energy radiates from you, distorting and diffusing magical energy within a 30-foot radius. Until the spell ends, the sphere moves with you, centered on you. Each friendly creature in the sphere, including you, has advantage on saving throws against spells and other magical effects. Additionally, when an affected creature succeeds on a saving throw made against a spell or magical effect that allows it to make a saving throw to take only half damage, it instead takes no damage.
