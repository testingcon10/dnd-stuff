# Greater Restoration

You imbue a creature you touch with positive energy to undo a debilitating effect. You can reduce the target's exhaustion level by one, or end one of the following effects: one effect that charmed or petrified the target, one curse (including attunement to a cursed item), any reduction to one ability score, or one effect reducing the target's hit point maximum.
