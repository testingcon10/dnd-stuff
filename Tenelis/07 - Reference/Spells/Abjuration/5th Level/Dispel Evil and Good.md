# Dispel Evil and Good

Shimmering energy surrounds and protects you from fey, undead, and creatures originating from beyond the Material Plane. For the duration, celestials, elementals, fey, fiends, and undead have disadvantage on attack rolls against you. You can end the spell early by using either of the following special functions: Break Enchantment (as your action, touch a creature charmed, frightened, or possessed by such a creature to end the effect) or Dismissal (as your action, make a melee spell attack against such a creature to drive it back to its home plane if it fails a Charisma save).
