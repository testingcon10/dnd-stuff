# Protection from Evil and Good

Until the spell ends, one willing creature you touch is protected against certain types of creatures: aberrations, celestials, elementals, fey, fiends, and undead. The protection grants several benefits: creatures of those types have disadvantage on attack rolls against the target, the target can't be charmed, frightened, or possessed by them, and if the target is already affected by one of those conditions, the target has advantage on any new saving throw against the relevant effect.
