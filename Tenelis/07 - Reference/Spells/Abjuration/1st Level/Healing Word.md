# Healing Word

A creature of your choice that you can see within range regains hit points equal to 1d4 + your spellcasting ability modifier. This spell has no effect on undead or constructs. Its bonus action casting time makes it ideal for mid-combat healing.

## At Higher Levels
When you cast this spell using a spell slot of [[2nd Level|2nd level]] or higher, the healing increases by 1d4 for each slot level above 1st.
