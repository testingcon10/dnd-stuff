# Snare

As you cast this spell, you use the rope to create a circle with a 5-foot radius on the ground or the floor. When you finish casting, the rope becomes nearly invisible. If a Small, Medium, or Large creature moves into the area, it is hoisted into the air, hanging upside down 3 feet above the surface, where it is restrained. The restrained creature can make a Dexterity saving throw at the end of each of its turns, ending the effect on itself on a success.
