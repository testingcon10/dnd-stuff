# Ceremony

You perform a special religious ceremony that is infused with magic. When you cast the spell, choose one of the following rites: Atonement, [[Bless]] Water, Coming of Age, Dedication, Funeral Rite, or Wedding. Each rite has a specific effect described in the spell's full text.
