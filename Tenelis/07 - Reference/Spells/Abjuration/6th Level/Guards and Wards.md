# Guards and Wards

You create a ward that protects up to 2,500 square feet of floor space. The warded area can be up to 20 feet tall. You can place the following magical effects within the warded area: corridors filled with fog, locked doors, stairs filled with webs, and a suggestion effect on a door or other feature. You can also place a [[Dancing Lights|dancing lights]] effect in four corridors and choose from additional effects such as [[Magic Mouth|magic mouth]] in two locations and [[Stinking Cloud|stinking cloud]] in two locations.
