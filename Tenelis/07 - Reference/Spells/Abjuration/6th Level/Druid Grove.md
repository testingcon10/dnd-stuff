# Druid Grove

You invoke the spirits of nature to protect an area outdoors or underground. The area can be as small as a 30-foot cube or as large as a 90-foot cube. Buildings and other structures are excluded from the affected area. Effects include: solid fog fills the area, grasping undergrowth makes the area difficult terrain, grove guardians (animated trees) protect the area, gusts of wind slow creatures, and additional spell effects of your choice within the area.
