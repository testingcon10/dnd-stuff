# Prismatic Wall

A shimmering, multicolored plane of light forms a vertical opaque wall up to 90 feet long, 30 feet high, and 1 inch thick, or a sphere up to 30 feet in diameter, centered on a point you choose within range. The wall has seven layers, each with a different color and effect, and a creature attempting to reach through or pass through must contend with each layer in turn. Each layer can be destroyed by a specific means, and when all layers are destroyed, the wall is dispelled.
