# Speak with Animals

You gain the ability to comprehend and verbally communicate with beasts for the duration. The knowledge and awareness of many beasts is limited by their intelligence, but at minimum they can give you information about nearby locations and monsters, including whatever they can perceive or have perceived within the past day. As a ritual spell, it can be cast without expending a spell slot when time permits.
