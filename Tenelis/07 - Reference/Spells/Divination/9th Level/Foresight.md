# Foresight

You touch a willing creature and bestow a limited ability to see into the immediate future. For the duration, the target can't be surprised, has advantage on attack rolls, ability checks, and saving throws, and other creatures have disadvantage on attack rolls against the target. The spell immediately ends if you cast it again before its duration expires.
