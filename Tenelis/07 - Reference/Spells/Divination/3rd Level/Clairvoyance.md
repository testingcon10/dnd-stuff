# Clairvoyance

You create an invisible sensor within range in a location familiar to you or in an obvious location that is unfamiliar to you. The sensor remains for the duration and can't be attacked or otherwise interacted with. When you cast the spell, you choose seeing or hearing, and you can use the chosen sense through the sensor as if you were in its space. As your action, you can switch between seeing and hearing.
