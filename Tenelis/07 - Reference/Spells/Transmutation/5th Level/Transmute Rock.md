# Transmute Rock

You choose an area of stone or mud that you can see that fits within a 40-foot cube and is within range, and choose one of the following effects. If you target an area of nonmagical rock, you can transmute it into an equal volume of mud. If you target an area of mud, you can transmute it into nonmagical rock. Transmute Rock to Mud: Creatures in the mud sink and are restrained. Transmute Mud to Rock: A creature in the mud when it is transformed to rock must make a Dexterity saving throw or become restrained.
