# Telekinesis

You gain the ability to move or manipulate creatures or objects by thought. When you cast the spell and as your action each round for the duration, you can exert your will on one creature or object that you can see within range. You can affect the same target round after round or choose a new one at any time. If you choose a creature, you can try to move it by making an ability check with your spellcasting ability contested by the target's Strength check; on success you move the creature up to 30 feet in any direction and restrain it. You can also exert fine control on objects, such as manipulating a simple tool or opening a container, or move an object weighing up to 1,000 pounds up to 30 feet.
