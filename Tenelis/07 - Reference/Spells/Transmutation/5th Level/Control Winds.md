# Control Winds

You take control of the air in a 100-foot cube that you can see within range. Choose one of the following effects when you cast the spell: Gusts (strong wind in a horizontal direction), Downdraft (strong wind pushing downward), or Updraft (rising wind lifts creatures). You can change the effect as an action on each of your turns.
