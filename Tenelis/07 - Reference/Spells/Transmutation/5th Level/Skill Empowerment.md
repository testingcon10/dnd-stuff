# Skill Empowerment

Your magic deepens a creature's understanding of its own talent. You touch one willing creature and give it expertise in one skill of your choice; the creature must already be proficient in that skill. Until the spell ends, the creature's proficiency bonus is doubled for any ability check it makes that uses the chosen skill.
