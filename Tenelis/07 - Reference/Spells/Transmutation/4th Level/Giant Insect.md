# Giant Insect

You transform up to ten centipedes, three spiders, five wasps, or one scorpion within range into giant versions of their natural forms for the duration. The creatures are friendly to you and your companions, obey your verbal commands, and act on their own turn in initiative. The DM has the statistics for these creatures and resolves their actions and movement. A creature transformed by this spell reverts to its normal size when it drops to 0 hit points or when the spell ends.
