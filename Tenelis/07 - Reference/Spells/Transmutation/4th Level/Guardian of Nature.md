# Guardian of Nature

A nature spirit answers your call and transforms you into a powerful guardian. The transformation lasts until the spell ends. You choose one of the following forms: Primal Beast (bestial fur, fangs; +10 ft. speed, darkvision 120 ft., advantage on Strength-based attacks, 1d6 extra force damage on melee hits) or Great Tree (bark-like skin, leaves; 10 temp HP, advantage on Constitution saves, advantage on Dex/Wis attacks for allies within 15 ft., difficult terrain around you).
