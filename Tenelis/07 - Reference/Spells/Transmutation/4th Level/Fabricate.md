# Fabricate

You convert raw materials into products of the same material. For example, you can fabricate a wooden bridge from a clump of trees, a rope from a patch of hemp, or clothes from flax or wool. You can fabricate a Large or smaller object (contained within a 10-foot cube or eight connected 5-foot cubes) given a sufficient quantity of raw material. If you are working with metal, stone, or another mineral substance, the fabricated object can be no larger than Medium.
