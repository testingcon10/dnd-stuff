# Polymorph

You transform a creature you can see within range into a new beast form. An unwilling creature must make a Wisdom saving throw. The target's game statistics (including mental ability scores) are replaced by the beast's statistics, but it retains its alignment and personality. The target's hit points change to those of the new form. The creature reverts when it drops to 0 HP or the spell ends. The new form's CR must equal or be less than the target's level (or CR).
