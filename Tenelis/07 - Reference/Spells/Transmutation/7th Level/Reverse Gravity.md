# Reverse Gravity

This spell reverses gravity in a 50-foot-radius, 100-foot high cylinder centered on a point within range. All creatures and objects that aren't somehow anchored to the ground in the area fall upward and reach the top of the area. A creature can make a Dexterity saving throw to grab onto a fixed object it can reach, thus avoiding the fall. When the spell ends, affected objects and creatures fall back down.
