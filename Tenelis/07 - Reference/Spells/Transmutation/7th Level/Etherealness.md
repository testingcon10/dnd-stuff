# Etherealness

You step into the border regions of the Ethereal Plane, in the area where it overlaps with your current plane. You remain in the Border Ethereal for the duration or until you use an action to dismiss the spell, and can move in any direction during this time. You can see and hear the plane you originated from, but everything appears gray and you can't see anything more than 60 feet away. You can only affect and be affected by other creatures on the Ethereal Plane.

## At Higher Levels
When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 8th level or higher, you can target up to three willing creatures (including you) for each slot level above 7th.
