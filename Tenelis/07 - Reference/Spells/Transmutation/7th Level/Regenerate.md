# Regenerate

You touch a creature and stimulate its natural healing ability. The target regains 4d8 + 15 hit points immediately upon casting. For the duration of the spell, the target regains 1 hit point at the start of each of its turns (10 hit points per minute). The target's severed body members (fingers, legs, tails, and so on), if any, are restored after 2 minutes.
