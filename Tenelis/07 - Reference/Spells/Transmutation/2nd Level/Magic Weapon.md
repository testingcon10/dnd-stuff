# Magic Weapon

You touch a nonmagical weapon. Until the spell ends, that weapon becomes a +1 magic weapon.

## At Higher Levels
When you cast this spell using a 4th-level spell slot, the bonus increases to +2. When you use a 6th-level spell slot, the bonus increases to +3.
