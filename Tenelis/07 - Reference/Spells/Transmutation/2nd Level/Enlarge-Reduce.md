# Enlarge/Reduce

You cause a creature or an object you can see within range to grow larger or smaller for the duration. Choose Enlarge or Reduce. Enlarge doubles the target's size in all dimensions, multiplies weight by eight, grants advantage on Strength checks and saving throws, and adds 1d4 to weapon damage. Reduce halves the target's size, divides weight by eight, grants disadvantage on Strength checks and saving throws, and reduces weapon damage by 1d4. An unwilling creature can make a Constitution saving throw to resist.
