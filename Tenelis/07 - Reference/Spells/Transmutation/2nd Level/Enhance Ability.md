# Enhance Ability

You touch a creature and bestow upon it a magical enhancement. Choose one of the following effects: Bear's Endurance, Bull's Strength, Cat's Grace, Eagle's Splendor, Fox's Cunning, or Owl's Wisdom. The target gains advantage on ability checks using the corresponding ability score for the duration. Bear's Endurance also grants 2d6 temporary hit points, which are lost when the spell ends.

## At Higher Levels
You can target one additional creature for each slot level above 2nd.
