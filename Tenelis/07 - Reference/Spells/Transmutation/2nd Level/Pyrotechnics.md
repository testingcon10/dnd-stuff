# Pyrotechnics

Choose an area of nonmagical flame that you can see and that fits within a 5-foot cube within range. You can extinguish the fire in that area, and you create either fireworks or smoke. Fireworks: Each creature within 10 feet of the fire must succeed on a Constitution saving throw or become blinded until the end of your next turn. Smoke: The area is heavily obscured for 1 minute or until dispersed by wind.
