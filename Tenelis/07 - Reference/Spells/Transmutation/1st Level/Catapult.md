# Catapult

Choose one object weighing 1 to 5 pounds within range that isn't being worn or carried. The object flies in a straight line up to 90 feet in a direction you choose before falling to the ground. When the object strikes something, it and what it strikes each take 3d8 bludgeoning damage. If it would strike a creature, that creature must make a Dexterity saving throw. On a hit, the object strikes the target and stops moving.

## At Higher Levels
When you cast this spell using a spell slot of [[2nd Level|2nd level]] or higher, the maximum weight of objects that you can target increases by 5 pounds, and the damage increases by 1d8 for each slot level above 1st.
