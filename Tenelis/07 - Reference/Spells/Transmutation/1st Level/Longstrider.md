# Longstrider

You touch a creature, and the target's speed increases by 10 feet until the spell ends. This non-concentration buff lasts a full hour, making it an excellent pre-combat or travel enhancement that stacks with other movement bonuses.

## At Higher Levels
When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 2nd level or higher, you can target one additional creature for each slot level above 1st.
