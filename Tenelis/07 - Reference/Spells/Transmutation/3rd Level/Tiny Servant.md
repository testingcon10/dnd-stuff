# Tiny Servant

You touch one Tiny, nonmagical object that isn't attached to another object or a surface and isn't being carried by another creature. The target animates and sprouts little arms and legs, becoming a creature under your control until the spell ends or the creature drops to 0 hit points. As a bonus action, you can mentally command the creature if it is within 120 feet of you, deciding what it does on its next turn.

## At Higher Levels
When you cast this spell using a spell slot of [[4th Level|4th level]] or higher, you can animate two additional objects for each slot level above 3rd.
