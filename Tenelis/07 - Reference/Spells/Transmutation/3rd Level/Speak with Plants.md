# Speak with Plants

You imbue plants within 30 feet of you with limited sentience and animation, giving them the ability to communicate with you and follow your simple commands. You can question plants about events in the spell's area within the past day, gaining information about creatures that have passed, weather, and other circumstances. You can also turn difficult terrain caused by [[Plant Growth|plant growth]] into ordinary terrain for the duration, or turn ordinary terrain where plants are present into difficult terrain for the duration.
