# Gaseous Form

You transform a willing creature you touch, along with everything it's wearing and carrying, into a misty cloud for the duration. The target's only method of movement is a flying speed of 10 feet, and it can enter and occupy the space of another creature. The target has resistance to nonmagical damage, advantage on Strength, Dexterity, and Constitution saving throws, and can pass through small holes, narrow openings, and even mere cracks, though it treats liquids as solid surfaces.
