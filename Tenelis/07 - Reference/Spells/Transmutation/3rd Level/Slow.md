# Slow

You alter time around up to six creatures of your choice in a 40-foot cube within range. Each target must succeed on a Wisdom saving throw or be affected for the duration. An affected target's speed is halved, it takes a -2 penalty to AC and Dexterity saving throws, and it can't use reactions. On its turn, it can use either an action or a bonus action, not both, and it can make only one attack regardless of its abilities or magic items. If it attempts to cast a spell with a casting time of 1 action, roll a d20; on an 11 or higher, the spell takes effect on the creature's next turn instead.
