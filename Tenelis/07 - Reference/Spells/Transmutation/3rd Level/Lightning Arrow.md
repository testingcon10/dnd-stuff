# Lightning Arrow

The next time you make a ranged weapon attack during the spell's duration, the weapon's ammunition or the weapon itself (if it's a thrown weapon) transforms into a bolt of lightning. Make the attack roll as normal; the target takes 4d8 lightning damage on a hit or half as much on a miss, instead of the weapon's normal damage. Whether you hit or miss, each creature within 10 feet of the target must make a Dexterity saving throw, taking 2d8 lightning damage on a failed save or half as much on a successful one.

## At Higher Levels
When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 4th level or higher, the damage for both effects of the spell increases by 1d8 for each slot level above 3rd.
