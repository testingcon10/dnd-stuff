# Create Homunculus

While speaking an intricate incantation, you cut yourself with a jewel-encrusted dagger, taking 2d4 piercing damage that can't be reduced in any way. You then drip your blood on the spell's other components and touch them, transforming them into a special construct known as a homunculus. You can have only one homunculus at a time. If you cast this spell while your homunculus lives, the spell fails.
