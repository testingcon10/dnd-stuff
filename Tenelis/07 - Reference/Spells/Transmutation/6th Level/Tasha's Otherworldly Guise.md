# Tasha's Otherworldly Guise

Uttering an incantation, you draw on the magic of the Lower Planes or Upper Planes (your choice) to transform yourself. You gain the following benefits until the spell ends: immunity to fire and poison damage (Lower Planes) or radiant and necrotic damage (Upper Planes), AC becomes 16 if lower, a +2 bonus to AC, immunity to the frightened or charmed condition, you sprout spectral wings with a 40-foot flying speed, and your weapon attacks are magical and use your spellcasting ability modifier for attack and damage rolls.
