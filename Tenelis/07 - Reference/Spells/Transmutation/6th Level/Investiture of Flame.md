# Investiture of Flame

Flames race across your body, shedding bright light in a 30-foot radius and dim light for an additional 30 feet for the spell's duration. The flames don't harm you. Until the spell ends, you gain immunity to fire damage and resistance to cold damage, any creature that moves within 5 feet of you or hits you with a melee attack takes 1d10 fire damage, and you can use your action to create a line of fire 15 feet long and 5 feet wide. Each creature in the line must make a Dexterity saving throw, taking 4d8 fire damage on a failed save, or half as much on a successful one.
