# Wind Walk

You and up to ten willing creatures you can see within range assume a [[Gaseous Form|gaseous form]] for the duration, appearing as wisps of cloud. While in this cloud form, a creature has a flying speed of 300 feet and has resistance to damage from nonmagical weapons. A creature in cloud form can't take actions other than the Dash action or revert to its normal form, and reverting takes 1 minute during which the creature is incapacitated.
