# Animal Shapes

You transform any number of willing creatures within range into beasts of CR 4 or lower. Each target assumes a beast form of your choice, and you can choose different forms for different targets. A target's game statistics are replaced by the statistics of the chosen beast, though it retains its alignment and Intelligence, Wisdom, and Charisma scores. Each target gains temporary hit points equal to the hit points of its beast form, and when those temporary hit points are reduced to 0, the creature reverts to its normal form. You can use your action to transform affected creatures into new forms on subsequent turns.
