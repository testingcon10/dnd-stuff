# Mass Polymorph

You transform up to ten creatures that you can see within range. An unwilling target must succeed on a Wisdom saving throw to resist the transformation. An unwilling shapechanger automatically succeeds on the save. Each target assumes a beast form of your choice; you can choose a different form for each target. The new form can be any beast whose challenge rating is equal to or less than the target's (or the target's level, if it doesn't have a challenge rating). The target's game statistics are replaced by the statistics of the chosen beast, but it retains its alignment and personality.
