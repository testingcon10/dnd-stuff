# Shape Water

You choose an area of water that you can see within range and that fits within a 5-foot cube. You manipulate it in one of several ways: you instantaneously move or change the flow of the water up to 5 feet, cause the water to form into simple shapes for 1 hour, change the color or opacity for 1 hour, or freeze the water for 1 hour.
