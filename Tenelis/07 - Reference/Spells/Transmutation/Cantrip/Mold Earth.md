# Mold Earth

You choose a portion of dirt or stone that you can see within range and that fits within a 5-foot cube. You manipulate it in one of several ways: you move it along the ground 5 feet, cause shapes or colors to appear on it for 1 hour, or cause it to become difficult or normal terrain for 1 hour.
