# Druidcraft

Whispering to the spirits of nature, you create one of several minor effects: you predict the weather for the next 24 hours, make a flower blossom or a seed pod open, create a harmless sensory effect, or instantly light or snuff out a small flame. You can have up to three of this spell's non-instantaneous effects active at a time.
