# Gust

You seize the air and compel it to create one of the following effects at a point you can see within range: one Medium or smaller creature must succeed on a Strength saving throw or be pushed up to 5 feet away from you, you create a small blast of air capable of moving one object that is neither held nor carried and weighs no more than 5 pounds, or you create a harmless sensory effect using air.
