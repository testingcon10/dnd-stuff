# Control Flames

You choose a nonmagical flame that you can see within range and that fits within a 5-foot cube. You affect it in one of several ways: you instantaneously expand it 5 feet in one direction, extinguish it, double or halve its area of bright and dim light, or cause simple shapes to appear within it for 1 hour.
