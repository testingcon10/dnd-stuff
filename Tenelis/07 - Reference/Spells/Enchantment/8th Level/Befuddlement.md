# Befuddlement

You blast the mind of one creature you can see within range. The target must make an Intelligence saving throw. On a failed save, the target takes 10d12 Psychic damage and can't cast spells or take the Magic action until the spell ends. At the end of every 30 days, the target repeats the save, ending the effect on a success. The effect can also be ended by the [[Greater Restoration]], [[Heal]], or Wish spell.
