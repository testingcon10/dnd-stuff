# Compelled Duel

You attempt to compel a creature into a duel. One creature that you can see within range must make a Wisdom saving throw. On a failed save, the creature is drawn to you, compelled by your divine demand. The target has disadvantage on attack rolls against creatures other than you, and must make a Wisdom saving throw each time it attempts to move more than 30 feet away from you.
