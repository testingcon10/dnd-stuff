# Illusory Dragon

By gathering threads of shadow material from the Shadowfell, you create a Huge shadowy dragon in an unoccupied space that you can see within range. The illusion lasts for the spell's duration and occupies its space, as if it were a creature. The dragon is 10 feet tall, 20 feet long, and has a frightful presence (WIS save or frightened). As a bonus action, you can move the dragon up to 60 feet. It can use its breath weapon (INT save or 7d6 damage of a type you choose) in a 60-foot cone.
