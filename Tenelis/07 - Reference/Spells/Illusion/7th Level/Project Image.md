# Project Image

You create an illusory copy of yourself that lasts for the duration and appears at any location within range that you have seen before. The illusion looks and sounds like you but is intangible. You can use your action to move this illusion up to twice your walking speed and make it gesture, speak, and behave in whatever way you choose. It mimics your mannerisms perfectly. You can see through its eyes and hear through its ears as if you were in its space, and you can switch between its senses and yours as a bonus action.
