# Disguise Self

You make yourself, including your clothing, armor, weapons, and other belongings on your person, look different until the spell ends or until you use your action to dismiss it. You can appear up to 1 foot shorter or taller and can appear thin, fat, or in between. The changes wrought by this spell fail to hold up to physical inspection, and a creature can use its action to make an Intelligence (Investigation) check against your spell save DC to discern the illusion.
