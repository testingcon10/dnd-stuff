# Dream

This spell shapes a creature's dreams. You choose a creature known to you as the target of this spell. The target must be on the same plane of existence as you. You, or a willing creature you touch, enters a trance state and acts as a messenger, appearing in the target's dreams to converse with it. The messenger can also shape the dream's environment, creating landscapes, objects, and other images. If the target is unaware that the messenger is present, the messenger can cause the target to make a Wisdom save or have a nightmare that deals 3d6 psychic damage and prevents it from gaining benefit from the rest.
