# Hallucinatory Terrain

You make natural terrain in a 150-foot cube in range look, sound, and smell like some other sort of natural terrain. Open fields or a road can be made to resemble a swamp, hill, crevasse, or some other difficult or impassable terrain. A pond can be made to seem like a grassy meadow, a precipice like a gentle slope, or a rock-strewn gully like a wide and smooth road. The spell doesn't disguise, conceal, or add manufactured structures, equipment, or creatures, and the illusion includes audible, visual, tactile, and olfactory elements.
