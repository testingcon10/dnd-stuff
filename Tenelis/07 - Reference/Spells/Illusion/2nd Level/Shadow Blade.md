# Shadow Blade

You weave together threads of shadow to create a sword of solidified gloom in your hand. This magic sword lasts until the spell ends. It counts as a simple melee weapon with the finesse, light, and thrown properties (range 20/60). On a hit, it deals 2d8 psychic damage. If you attack with it in dim light or darkness, you make the attack roll with advantage.

## At Higher Levels
When you cast this spell using a 3rd- or 4th-level spell slot, the damage increases to 3d8. When you use a 5th- or 6th-level spell slot, the damage increases to 4d8. When you use a spell slot of 7th level or higher, the damage increases to 5d8.
