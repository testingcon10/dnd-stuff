# Mirror Image

Three illusory duplicates of yourself appear in your space. Until the spell ends, the duplicates move with you and mimic your actions, shifting position so it's impossible to track which image is real. Each time a creature targets you with an attack during the spell's duration, roll a d20 to determine whether the attack instead targets one of your duplicates. A duplicate's AC equals 10 + your Dexterity modifier, and if an attack hits a duplicate, the duplicate is destroyed.
