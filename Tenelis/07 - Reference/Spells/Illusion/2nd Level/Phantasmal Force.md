# Phantasmal Force

You craft an illusion that takes root in the mind of a creature that you can see within range. The target must make an Intelligence saving throw. On a failed save, you create a phantasmal object, creature, or other visible phenomenon of your choice that is no larger than a 10-foot cube and that is perceivable only to the target for the duration. The target rationalizes any illogical outcomes from interacting with the phantasm and takes 1d6 psychic damage each turn if the phantasm is made to appear dangerous.
