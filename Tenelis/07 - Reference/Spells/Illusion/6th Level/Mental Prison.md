# Mental Prison

You attempt to bind a creature within an illusory cell that only it perceives. One creature you can see within range must make an Intelligence saving throw. The target automatically succeeds if it is immune to being charmed. On a failed save, the target is restrained for the spell's duration and perceives the area around it as dangerous (your choice). The target can't see or hear anything beyond its cell. If the target is moved out of the illusion or reaches through it, it takes 10d10 psychic damage, and the spell ends.
