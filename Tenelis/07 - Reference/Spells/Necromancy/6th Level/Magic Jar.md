# Magic Jar

Your body falls into a catatonic state as your soul leaves it and enters the container you used for the spell's material component. While your soul inhabits the container, you are aware of your surroundings as if you were in the container's space. You can attempt to possess any humanoid within 100 feet of the container; the target must make a Charisma saving throw or your soul moves into the target's body, and the target's soul becomes trapped in the container.
