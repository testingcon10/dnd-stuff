# Create Undead

You can cast this spell only at night. Choose up to three corpses of Medium or Small humanoids within range, and each one becomes a ghoul under your control. On each of your turns, you can use a bonus action to mentally command any creature you made with this spell if the creature is within 60 feet of you. You must reassert control over the undead by casting this spell again within 24 hours, or they become uncontrolled.

## At Higher Levels
When you cast this spell using a 7th-level spell slot, you can animate or reassert control over four ghouls. When you cast this spell using an 8th-level spell slot, you can animate or reassert control over five ghouls or two ghasts or wights. When you cast this spell using a 9th-level spell slot, you can animate or reassert control over six ghouls, three ghasts or wights, or two mummies.
