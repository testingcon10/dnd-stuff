# Soul Cage

This spell snatches the soul of a humanoid as it dies and traps it inside the tiny cage you use for the material component. While the soul is trapped, the dead humanoid can't be revived. You can exploit the trapped soul in several ways: steal life (regain 2d8 HP, up to 6 times), query soul (ask the spirit a question), borrow experience (gain advantage on one check), or use eyes of the dead (see through the dead body's eyes).
