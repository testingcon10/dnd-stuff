# True Resurrection

You touch a creature that has been dead for no longer than 200 years and that died for any reason except old age. The creature is restored to life with all its hit points, and all diseases, poisons, curses, and magical effects are removed. The spell can even provide a new body if the original no longer exists, in which case you must speak the creature's name, and the creature then appears in an unoccupied space you choose within 10 feet of you.
