# Blight

Necromantic energy washes over a creature of your choice that you can see within range, draining moisture and vitality from it. The target must make a Constitution saving throw, taking 8d8 necrotic damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one. This spell has no effect on undead or constructs; if the target is a plant creature or a magical plant, it makes the saving throw with disadvantage and takes maximum damage.

## At Higher Levels
When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 5th level or higher, the damage increases by 1d8 for each slot level above 4th.
