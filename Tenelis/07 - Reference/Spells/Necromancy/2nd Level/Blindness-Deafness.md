# Blindness/Deafness

You can blind or deafen a foe. Choose one creature that you can see within range to make a Constitution saving throw. If it fails, the target is either blinded or deafened (your choice) for the duration. At the end of each of its turns, the target can make a Constitution saving throw, ending the effect on itself on a success.

## At Higher Levels
You can target one additional creature for each slot level above 2nd.
