# Finger of Death

You send negative energy coursing through a creature that you can see within range, causing it searing pain. The target must make a Constitution saving throw, taking 7d8 + 30 necrotic damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one. A humanoid killed by this spell rises at the start of your next turn as a zombie that is permanently under your command.

## At Higher Levels
When you cast this spell using a spell slot of [[8th Level|8th level]] or higher, the damage increases by 2d8 for each slot level above 7th.
