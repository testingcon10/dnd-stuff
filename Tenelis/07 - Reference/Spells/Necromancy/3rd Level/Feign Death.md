# Feign Death

You touch a willing creature and put it into a cataleptic state that is indistinguishable from death. For the spell's duration, or until you use an action to touch the target and dismiss the spell, the target appears dead to all outward inspection and to spells used to determine the target's status. The target is blinded and incapacitated, its speed drops to 0, it has resistance to all damage except psychic damage, and diseases and poisons have no effect for the duration.
