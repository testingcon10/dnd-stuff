# Bestow Curse

You touch a creature, and that creature must succeed on a Wisdom saving throw or become cursed for the duration of the spell. When you cast this spell, choose one of four curse effects: disadvantage on checks and saves with one ability score; disadvantage on attack rolls against you; a Wisdom save each turn or waste the turn; or your attacks and spells deal an extra 1d8 necrotic damage to the target. A [[Remove Curse|remove curse]] spell ends this effect.

## At Higher Levels
When you cast this spell using a spell slot of [[4th Level|4th level]] or higher, the duration is concentration up to 10 minutes. With a 5th-level slot, the duration is 8 hours. With a 7th-level slot, the duration is 24 hours. With a 9th-level slot, the curse lasts until dispelled and doesn't require concentration.
