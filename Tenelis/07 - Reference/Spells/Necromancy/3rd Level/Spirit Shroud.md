# Spirit Shroud

You call forth spirits of the dead, which flit around you for the spell's duration. When you cast this spell, choose a damage type: cold, necrotic, or radiant. Any creature that is not undead or a construct that you hit with a melee attack during the spell's duration takes 1d8 extra damage of the chosen type. Additionally, any creature of your choice within 10 feet of you at the start of each of your turns has its speed reduced by 10 feet until the start of your next turn.

## At Higher Levels
When you cast this spell using a spell slot of [[4th Level|4th level]] or higher, the damage increases by 1d8 for every two slot levels above 3rd.
