# Inflict Wounds

Make a melee spell attack against a creature you can reach. On a hit, the target takes 3d10 necrotic damage. This is one of the highest single-target damage spells available at [[1st Level|1st level]], making it a powerful option for clerics willing to get into melee range.

## At Higher Levels
When you cast this spell using a spell slot of [[2nd Level|2nd level]] or higher, the damage increases by 1d10 for each slot level above 1st.
