# False Life

Bolstering yourself with a necromantic facsimile of life, you gain 1d4 + 4 temporary hit points for the duration. These temporary hit points provide a buffer against damage without requiring concentration, making it a reliable pre-combat defensive option.

## At Higher Levels
When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 2nd level or higher, you gain 5 additional temporary hit points for each slot level above 1st.
