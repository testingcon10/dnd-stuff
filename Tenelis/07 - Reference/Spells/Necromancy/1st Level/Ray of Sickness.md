# Ray of Sickness

A ray of sickening greenish energy lashes out toward a creature within range. Make a ranged spell attack against the target, dealing 2d8 poison damage on a hit. The target must also make a Constitution saving throw or be poisoned until the end of your next turn. The poisoned condition imposes disadvantage on attack rolls and ability checks.

## At Higher Levels
When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 2nd level or higher, the damage increases by 1d8 for each slot level above 1st.
