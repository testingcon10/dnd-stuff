# Giant Insect

You summon a giant centipede, spider, wasp, or scorpion, and it appears in an unoccupied space you can see within range. The creature is friendly to you and your companions. In combat, the creature acts on your initiative count. It obeys your verbal commands (no action required by you). The creature disappears when it drops to 0 Hit Points or when the spell ends.

## At Higher Levels
When you cast this spell using a spell slot of [[5th Level|5th level]] or higher, you summon a second creature. If you use a 6th-level slot, you summon a third creature.
