# Leomund's Secret Chest

You hide a chest, and all its contents, on the Ethereal Plane. You must touch the chest and the miniature replica that serves as a material component for the spell. The chest can contain up to 12 cubic feet of nonliving material. While the chest remains on the Ethereal Plane, you can use an action and touch the replica to recall the chest, which appears in an unoccupied space on the ground within 5 feet of you. After 60 days, there is a cumulative 5 percent chance per day that the spell's effect ends, causing the chest to be irretrievably lost.
