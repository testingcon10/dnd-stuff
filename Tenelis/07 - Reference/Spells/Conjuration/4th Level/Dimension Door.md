# Dimension Door

You teleport yourself from your current location to any other spot within range. You can bring along objects as long as their weight doesn't exceed what you can carry. You can also bring one willing creature of your size or smaller who is carrying gear up to its carrying capacity. If you would arrive in an occupied space, you and any accompanying creature each take 4d6 force damage and the teleportation fails.
