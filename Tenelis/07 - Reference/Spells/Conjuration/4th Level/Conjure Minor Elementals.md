# Conjure Minor Elementals

You summon elementals that appear in unoccupied spaces that you can see within range. You choose one of the following options for what appears: one elemental of challenge rating 2 or lower, two elementals of challenge rating 1 or lower, four elementals of challenge rating 1/2 or lower, or eight elementals of challenge rating 1/4 or lower. The summoned creatures are friendly to you and your companions, obey your verbal commands, and act on their own turn in initiative.

## At Higher Levels
When you cast this spell using certain higher-level spell slots, you choose one of the summoning options above, and more creatures appear: twice as many with a 6th-level slot and three times as many with an 8th-level slot.
