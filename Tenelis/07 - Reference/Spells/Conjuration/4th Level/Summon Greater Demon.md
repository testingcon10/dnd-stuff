# Summon Greater Demon

You utter foul words, summoning one demon from the chaos of the Abyss. You choose the demon's type, which must be one of challenge rating 5 or lower. The demon appears in an unoccupied space you can see within range, and it disappears when it drops to 0 hit points or when the spell ends. At the end of each of the demon's turns, it makes a Charisma saving throw. On a failure, the demon continues to obey you. On a success, it breaks free from your control.

## At Higher Levels
When you cast this spell using a spell slot of [[5th Level|5th level]] or higher, the challenge rating increases by 1 for each slot level above 4th.
