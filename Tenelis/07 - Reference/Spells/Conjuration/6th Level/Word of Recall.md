# Word of Recall

You and up to five willing creatures within 5 feet of you instantly teleport to a previously designated sanctuary. You and any creatures that teleport with you appear in the nearest unoccupied space to the spot you designated when you prepared your sanctuary. You must designate a sanctuary by casting this spell within a location, such as a temple, dedicated to or strongly linked to your deity.
