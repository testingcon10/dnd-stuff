# Planar Ally

You beseech an otherworldly entity for aid. The being must be known to you: a god, a primordial, a demon prince, or some other being of cosmic power. That entity sends a celestial, an elemental, or a fiend loyal to it to aid you, appearing in an unoccupied space within range. The creature is under no compulsion to behave in any particular way and you must negotiate payment for its services, which can include a task, a monetary payment, or some other form of compensation.
