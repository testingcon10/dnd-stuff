# Wall of Thorns

You create a wall of tough, pliable, tangled brush bristling with needle-sharp thorns. The wall appears within range on a solid surface and can be up to 60 feet long, 10 feet high, and 5 feet thick, or a circle of 20-foot diameter, 20 feet high, and 5 feet thick. When the wall appears, each creature in its area must make a Dexterity saving throw, taking 7d8 piercing damage on a failed save or half as much on a success. A creature can move through the wall, albeit slowly and painfully, using 4 feet of movement for every 1 foot and taking 7d8 slashing damage per turn while in the wall.

## At Higher Levels
When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 7th level or higher, both types of damage increase by 1d8 for each slot level above 6th.
