# Spirit Guardians

You call forth spirits to protect you. They flit around you to a distance of 15 feet for the duration. When a hostile creature enters the area for the first time on a turn or starts its turn there, it must make a Wisdom saving throw, taking 3d8 radiant (or necrotic for evil casters) damage on a failed save, or half as much on a success. Affected creatures' speed is halved in the area.

## At Higher Levels
Damage increases by 1d8 for each slot level above 3rd.
