# Hunger of Hadar

You open a gateway to the dark between the stars, a region infested with unknown horrors. A 20-foot-radius sphere of blackness and bitter cold appears centered on a point within range, filled with a cacophony of soft whispers and slurping noises. No light, magical or otherwise, can illuminate the area, and creatures fully within the area are blinded. Any creature that starts its turn in the area takes 2d6 cold damage, and any creature that ends its turn in the area must make a Dexterity saving throw or take 2d6 acid damage.
