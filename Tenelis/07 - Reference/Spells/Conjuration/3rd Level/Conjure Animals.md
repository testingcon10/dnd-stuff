# Conjure Animals

You summon fey spirits that take the form of beasts and appear in unoccupied spaces that you can see within range. Choose one of the following options: one beast of challenge rating 2 or lower, two beasts of challenge rating 1 or lower, four beasts of challenge rating 1/2 or lower, or eight beasts of challenge rating 1/4 or lower. Each beast is also considered fey and disappears when it drops to 0 hit points or when the spell ends. The summoned creatures are friendly to you and your companions, obey your verbal commands, and roll their own initiative.

## At Higher Levels
When you cast this spell using certain higher-level spell slots, you choose one of the summoning options above, and more creatures appear: twice as many with a 5th-level slot, three times as many with a 7th-level slot, and four times as many with a 9th-level slot.
