# Blade of Disaster

You create a blade-shaped planar rift about 3 feet long in an unoccupied space you can see within range. The blade lasts for the duration. When you create the blade, you make up to two melee spell attacks with it, each one against a creature, loose object, or structure within 5 feet of the blade. On a hit, the target takes 4d12 force damage. On a critical hit (18-20), the blade deals an extra 8d12 force damage. As a bonus action on your turn, you can move the blade up to 30 feet and repeat the attacks.
