# Infestation

You cause a cloud of mites, fleas, and other parasites to appear momentarily on one creature you can see within range. The target must succeed on a Constitution saving throw or take 1d6 poison damage and move 5 feet in a random direction.

The spell's damage increases by 1d6 when you reach [[5th Level|5th level]] (2d6), 11th level (3d6), and 17th level (4d6).
