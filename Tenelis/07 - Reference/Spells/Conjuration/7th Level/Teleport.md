# Teleport

This spell instantly transports you and up to eight willing creatures of your choice that you can see within range, or a single object that you can see within range, to a destination you select. The accuracy of the teleportation depends on your familiarity with the destination, ranging from perfectly accurate for a permanent circle to potentially off-target or a mishap for destinations you've only heard described. The DM rolls d100 and consults the Teleportation table to determine what happens.
