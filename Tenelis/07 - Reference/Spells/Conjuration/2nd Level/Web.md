# Web

You conjure a mass of thick, sticky webbing at a point of your choice within range. The webs fill a 20-foot cube from that point for the duration and are difficult terrain and lightly obscure the area. Each creature that starts its turn in the webs or enters them during its turn must make a Dexterity saving throw or be restrained. A restrained creature can use its action to make a Strength check against your spell save DC, freeing itself on a success. The webs are flammable and any 5-foot cube exposed to fire burns away in 1 round, dealing 2d4 fire damage to any creature that starts its turn in the fire.
