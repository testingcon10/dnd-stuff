# Tree Stride

You gain the ability to enter a tree and move from inside it to inside another tree of the same kind within 500 feet. Both trees must be living and at least the same size as you. You must use 5 feet of movement to step into the tree, and you instantly know the location of all other trees of the same kind within 500 feet. You can either pass into one of those trees or step out of the tree you're in, using 5 feet of movement. You can use this transportation ability once per round for the duration.
