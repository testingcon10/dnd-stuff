# Tsunami

A wall of water springs into existence at a point you choose within range. You can make the wall up to 300 feet long, 300 feet high, and 50 feet thick, and it lasts for the duration. Each creature in the wall's area must make a Strength saving throw, taking 6d10 bludgeoning damage on a failed save or half as much on a success. At the start of each of your turns, the wall moves 50 feet away from you, and each creature in its space or whose space it enters must make the saving throw again, with the damage decreasing by 1d10 per round.
