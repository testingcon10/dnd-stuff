# Grease

Nonflammable grease covers the ground in a 10-foot square centered on a point within range and turns it into Difficult Terrain for the duration. When the grease appears, each creature standing in its area must succeed on a Dexterity saving throw or have the [[Prone]] condition. A creature that enters the area or ends its turn there must also succeed on a Dexterity saving throw or have the [[Prone]] condition.
