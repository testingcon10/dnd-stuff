# Tenser's Floating Disk

This spell creates a circular, horizontal plane of force, 3 feet in diameter and 1 inch thick, that floats 3 feet above the ground in an unoccupied space of your choice within range. The disk can hold up to 500 pounds and follows you at a distance of 20 feet, moving to remain within that distance. If you move more than 100 feet from it, the spell ends.
