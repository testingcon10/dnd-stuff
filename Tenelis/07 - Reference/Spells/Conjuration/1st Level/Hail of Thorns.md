# Hail of Thorns

The next time you hit a creature with a ranged weapon attack before the spell ends, this spell creates a rain of thorns that sprouts from your ranged weapon or ammunition. In addition to the normal effect of the attack, the target and each creature within 5 feet of it must make a Dexterity saving throw, taking 1d10 piercing damage on a failed save, or half as much on a success.

## At Higher Levels
When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 2nd level or higher, the damage increases by 1d10 for each slot level above 1st (to a maximum of 6d10).
