# Fog Cloud

You create a 20-foot-radius sphere of fog centered on a point within range. The sphere spreads around corners, and its area is heavily obscured. A wind of moderate or greater speed (at least 10 miles per hour) can disperse it. This spell is effective for breaking line of sight, disrupting ranged attackers, and enabling escapes.

## At Higher Levels
When you cast this spell using a spell slot of [[2nd Level|2nd level]] or higher, the radius of the fog increases by 20 feet for each slot level above 1st.
