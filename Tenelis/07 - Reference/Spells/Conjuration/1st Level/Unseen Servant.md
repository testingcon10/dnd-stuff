# Unseen Servant

This spell creates an invisible, mindless, shapeless force that performs simple tasks at your command until the spell ends. The servant has AC 10, 1 hit point, a Strength of 2, and can't attack. It can perform simple tasks that a human servant could do, such as fetching things, cleaning, mending, folding clothes, lighting fires, and serving food. Once given a command, it continues to follow it until the task is completed.
