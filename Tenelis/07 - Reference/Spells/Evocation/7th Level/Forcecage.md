# Forcecage

An immobile, invisible, cube-shaped prison composed of magical force springs into existence around an area you choose within range. The prison can be a cage (up to 20 feet on a side with bars of force spaced half an inch apart) or a box (up to 10 feet on a side, creating a solid barrier). A creature inside the cage that is too large for the area is pushed to the outside. Any creature inside the prison can't leave it by nonmagical means, and a creature that attempts to use teleportation or interplanar travel to leave must first make a Charisma saving throw or the attempt fails.
