# Delayed Blast Fireball

A beam of yellow light flashes from your pointing finger and condenses at a chosen point within range as a glowing bead for the duration. When the spell ends, or if you use an action to dismiss it, the bead blossoms into a 20-foot-radius sphere of fire, dealing 12d6 fire damage on a failed Dexterity saving throw, or half as much on a success. For each round the bead remains without detonating, the damage increases by 1d6, to a maximum of 22d6.

## At Higher Levels
When you cast this spell using a spell slot of [[8th Level|8th level]] or higher, the base damage increases by 1d6 for each slot level above 7th.
