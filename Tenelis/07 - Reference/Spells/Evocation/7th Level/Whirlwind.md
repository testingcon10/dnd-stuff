# Whirlwind

A whirlwind howls down to a point that you can see on the ground within range. The whirlwind is a 10-foot-radius, 30-foot-high cylinder centered on that point. Until the spell ends, you can use your action to move the whirlwind up to 30 feet in any direction along the ground. Each creature in the whirlwind's area must make a Dexterity saving throw. On a failed save, a creature takes 10d6 bludgeoning damage and is flung up to 20 feet upward. A creature that is flung upward takes falling damage. On a successful save, the target takes half as much damage and isn't flung.
