# Wall of Water

You create a wall of water on the ground at a point you can see within range. You can make the wall up to 30 feet long, 10 feet high, and 1 foot thick, or you can make a ringed wall up to 20 feet in diameter, 20 feet high, and 1 foot thick. The wall vanishes when the spell ends. The wall's space is difficult terrain. Ranged weapon attacks that pass through the wall's space have disadvantage, and fire damage is halved if the attack or effect passes through the wall.
