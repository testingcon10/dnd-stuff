# Melf's Minute Meteors

You create six tiny meteors in your space. They float in the air and orbit you for the spell's duration. As a bonus action on each of your turns, you can expend one or two of the meteors, sending each one to a point within 120 feet of you. Once a meteor reaches its destination, it explodes. Each creature within 5 feet of the point must make a Dexterity saving throw, taking 2d6 fire damage on a failed save, or half as much on a successful one.

## At Higher Levels
When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 4th level or higher, the number of meteors created increases by two for each slot level above 3rd.
