# Blade Barrier

You create a vertical wall of whirling, razor-sharp blades made of magical energy. The wall can be up to 100 feet long, 20 feet high, and 5 feet thick, or a ringed wall up to 60 feet in diameter, 20 feet high, and 5 feet thick. The wall provides three-quarters cover and its space is difficult terrain. A creature that enters the wall's area for the first time on a turn or starts its turn there must make a Dexterity saving throw, taking 6d10 slashing damage on a failed save or half as much on a success.
