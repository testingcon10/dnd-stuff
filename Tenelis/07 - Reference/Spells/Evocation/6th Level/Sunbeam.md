# Sunbeam

A beam of brilliant light flashes out from your hand in a 5-foot-wide, 60-foot-long line. Each creature in the line must make a Constitution saving throw, taking 6d8 radiant damage on a failed save or half as much on a success. A creature that fails is also blinded until your next turn. Undead and oozes have disadvantage on this saving throw. For the duration, you can use your action on subsequent turns to create a new line of radiance.
