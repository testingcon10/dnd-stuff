# Fire Bolt

You hurl a mote of fire at a creature or object within range. Make a ranged spell attack. On a hit, the target takes 1d10 fire damage. A flammable object hit by this spell ignites if it isn't being worn or carried.

## At Higher Levels
The spell's damage increases by 1d10 at 5th level (2d10), 11th level (3d10), and 17th level (4d10).
