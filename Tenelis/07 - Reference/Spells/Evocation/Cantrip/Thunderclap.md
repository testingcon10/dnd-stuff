# Thunderclap

You create a burst of thunderous sound that can be heard up to 100 feet away. Each creature within 5 feet of you (other than you) must succeed on a Constitution saving throw or take 1d6 thunder damage.

The spell's damage increases by 1d6 when you reach [[5th Level|5th level]] (2d6), 11th level (3d6), and 17th level (4d6).
