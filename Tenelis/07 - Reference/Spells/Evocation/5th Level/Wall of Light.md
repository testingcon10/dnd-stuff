# Wall of Light

A shimmering wall of bright light appears at a point you choose within range. The wall appears in any orientation you choose: horizontally, vertically, or diagonally. It can be up to 60 feet long, 10 feet high, and 5 feet thick. When the wall appears, each creature in its area must make a Constitution saving throw, taking 4d8 radiant damage on a failed save, or half as much on a successful one. A creature blinded by the wall can make a Constitution saving throw at the end of each of its turns. On a success, it is no longer blinded.
