# Wrath of Nature

You call out to the spirits of nature to rouse them against your enemies. Choose a point you can see within range. The spirits cause trees, rocks, and grasses in a 60-foot cube centered on that point to become animated until the spell ends. Trees throw rocks at enemies (3d8+STR bludgeoning), grasses and undergrowth become difficult terrain, and roots and vines restrain creatures that start their turn or enter the area (STR save or restrained).
