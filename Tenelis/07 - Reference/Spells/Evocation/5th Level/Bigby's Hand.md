# Bigby's Hand

You create a Large hand of shimmering, translucent force in an unoccupied space that you can see within range. The hand lasts for the spell's duration, moves at your command, and mimics the movements of your own hand. The hand is an object with AC 20 and hit points equal to your hit point maximum. You can use the hand to perform one of four actions as a bonus action: Clenched Fist (melee spell attack for 4d8 force), Forceful Hand (push a creature 5 feet plus 5 feet per 5 you beat the check), Grasping Hand (grapple), or Interposing Hand (half cover and resistance to its damage for a chosen creature).

## At Higher Levels
When you cast this spell using a spell slot of [[6th Level|6th level]] or higher, the damage from the clenched fist option increases by 2d8 and the damage from the grasping hand increases by 2d6 for each slot level above 5th.
