# Hallow

You touch a point and infuse an area around it with holy or unholy power. The area can have a radius up to 60 feet, and the spell fails if the radius includes an area already under the effect of a hallow spell. Celestials, elementals, fey, fiends, and undead can't enter the area, nor can such creatures charm, frighten, or possess creatures within it. You can bind an extra effect to the area, choosing from: Courage, [[Darkness]], [[Daylight]], Energy Protection, Energy Vulnerability, Everlasting Rest, Extradimensional Interference, [[Fear]], [[Silence]], or [[Tongues]].
