# Wrathful Smite

Your attack deals an extra 1d6 Psychic damage. Additionally, if the target is a creature, it must succeed on a Wisdom saving throw or have the Frightened condition until the spell ends. At the end of each of its turns, the Frightened creature repeats the save, ending the effect on itself on a success.
