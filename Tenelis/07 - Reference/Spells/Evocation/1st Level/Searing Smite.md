# Searing Smite

As you hit the target, it takes an extra 1d6 Fire damage from the attack. At the start of each of its turns, the target must make a Constitution saving throw. On a failed save, it takes 1d6 Fire damage. On a successful save, the ongoing effect ends.

## At Higher Levels
When you cast this spell using a spell slot of [[2nd Level|2nd level]] or higher, all the damage increases by 1d6 for each slot level above 1st.
