# Guiding Bolt

A flash of light streaks toward a creature of your choice within range. Make a ranged spell attack. On a hit, the target takes 4d6 radiant damage, and the next attack roll made against this target before the end of your next turn has advantage.

## At Higher Levels
When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 2nd level or higher, the damage increases by 1d6 for each slot level above 1st.
