# Warding Wind

A strong wind (20 miles per hour) blows around you in a 10-foot radius and moves with you, remaining centered on you. The wind lasts for the spell's duration. The wind has the following effects: it deafens you and other creatures in its area, it extinguishes unprotected flames in its area, it is difficult terrain for other creatures, the attack rolls of ranged weapon attacks passing through the wind have disadvantage, and it hedges out vapor, gas, and fog that can be dispersed by strong wind.
