# Spiritual Weapon

You create a floating, spectral weapon within range that lasts for the duration. When you cast the spell, you can make a melee spell attack against a creature within 5 feet of the weapon, dealing 1d8 + spellcasting ability modifier force damage on a hit. As a bonus action on subsequent turns, you can move the weapon up to 20 feet and repeat the attack. Notably does not require concentration.

## At Higher Levels
Damage increases by 1d8 for every two slot levels above 2nd.
