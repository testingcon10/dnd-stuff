# Shatter

A sudden loud ringing noise painfully intense erupts from a point of your choice within range. Each creature in a 10-foot-radius sphere must make a Constitution saving throw, taking 3d8 thunder damage on a failed save or half as much on a success. Creatures made of inorganic material have disadvantage on the saving throw.

## At Higher Levels
Damage increases by 1d8 for each slot level above 2nd.
