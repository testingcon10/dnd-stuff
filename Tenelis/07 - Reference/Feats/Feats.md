# Feats

Feats are special abilities chosen at character creation or when you gain the Ability Score Improvement feature. They are organized into four categories:

| Category | Available | Description |
|----------|-----------|-------------|
| [[Origin]] | Level 1 (character creation) | Background-related feats chosen during character creation |
| [[General]] | Level 4+ (ASI) | The main feat pool, chosen when your class grants Ability Score Improvement |
| [[Fighting Style]] | Level 4+ (ASI) | Combat specialization feats for martial characters |
| [[Epic Boon]] | Level 19+ (ASI) | Powerful feats available only at the highest levels |
