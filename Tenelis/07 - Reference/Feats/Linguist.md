# Linguist

You have studied languages and codes extensively. You increase your Intelligence by 1 (to a maximum of 20). You learn three languages of your choice. You can ably create written ciphers; others can't decipher a code you create unless you teach them, they succeed on an Intelligence check (DC equal to your Intelligence score + your proficiency bonus), or they use magic to decipher it.
