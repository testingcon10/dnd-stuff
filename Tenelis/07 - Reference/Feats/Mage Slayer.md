# Mage Slayer

When a creature within 5 feet of you casts a spell, you can use your reaction to make a melee weapon attack against that creature. When you damage a creature concentrating on a spell, that creature has disadvantage on the saving throw to maintain concentration. You have advantage on saving throws against spells cast by creatures within 5 feet of you.
