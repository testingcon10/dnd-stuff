# Origin

*Origin Feat (Prerequisite: None)*

Origin feats are chosen at level 1 during character creation, based on your character's background. Every background grants one Origin feat.

| Feat | Benefit |
|------|---------|
| [[Alert]] | Add proficiency bonus to initiative; swap initiative with a willing ally |
| [[Crafter]] | Proficiency with three [[Artisan's Tools|artisan's tools]]; 20% discount on nonmagical items |
| [[Healer]] | Use [[Healer's Kit]] to restore hit points plus proficiency bonus as an action |
| [[Lucky]] | Spend Luck Points to gain advantage on d20 tests or impose disadvantage on attacks against you |
| [[Magic Initiate]] | Learn two cantrips and one 1st-level spell from [[Cleric]], [[Druid]], or [[Wizard]] lists |
| [[Musician]] | Proficiency with three instruments; grant Heroic Inspiration to allies after a rest |
| [[Savage Attacker]] | Roll weapon damage dice twice once per turn and use the better result |
| [[Skilled]] | Gain proficiency in any combination of three skills or tools |
| [[Tavern Brawler]] | Enhanced unarmed strikes (1d4 + modifier), reroll damage 1s, wield furniture as weapons |
| [[Tough]] | Increase max HP by 2 per level (current and future) |
