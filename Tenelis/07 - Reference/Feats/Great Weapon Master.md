# Great Weapon Master

On your turn, when you score a critical hit or reduce a creature to 0 HP with a melee weapon, you can make one melee weapon attack as a bonus action. Before you make a melee attack with a heavy weapon you are proficient with, you can choose to take a -5 penalty to the attack roll. If the attack hits, you add +10 to the attack's damage.
