# Crossbow Expert

You ignore the loading quality of crossbows you are proficient with. Being within 5 feet of a hostile creature doesn't impose disadvantage on your ranged attack rolls. When you use the Attack action with a one-handed weapon, you can use a bonus action to attack with a [[Hand Crossbow|hand crossbow]] you are holding.
