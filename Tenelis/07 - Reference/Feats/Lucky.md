# Lucky

You have 3 luck points. Whenever you make an attack roll, ability check, or saving throw, you can spend one luck point to roll an additional d20 and choose which to use. You can also spend a luck point when an attack roll is made against you, rolling a d20 and choosing which roll the attacker uses. Luck points are regained after a long rest.
