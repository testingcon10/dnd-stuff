# Shield Master

*General Feat (Prerequisite: Shield proficiency)*

You gain the following benefits.

**Ability Score Increase.** Increase your Strength score by 1, to a maximum of 20.

**Shield Bash.** If you attack a creature within 5 feet of you as part of the Attack action and hit with a Melee weapon, you can immediately make a Shove using your Shield against that creature as a Bonus Action. You can use this benefit only once on each of your turns.

**Interpose Shield.** If you're subjected to an effect that allows you to make a Dexterity saving throw to take only half damage, you can take a Reaction to take no damage if you succeed on the saving throw, interposing your Shield between yourself and the source of the effect.
