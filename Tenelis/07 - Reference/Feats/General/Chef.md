# Chef

*General Feat (Prerequisite: Constitution or Wisdom 13+)*

You gain the following benefits.

**Ability Score Increase.** Increase your Constitution or Wisdom score by 1, to a maximum of 20.

**Cook's Utensils.** You gain proficiency with Cook's Utensils if you don't already have it.

**Replenishing Meal.** As part of a Short Rest, you can cook special food if you have Cook's Utensils and ingredients on hand. You can prepare enough for a number of creatures equal to 4 + your Proficiency Bonus. At the end of the Short Rest, any creature who eats the food regains an extra 1d8 Hit Points.

**Bolstering Treats.** With 1 hour of work or when you finish a Long Rest, you can cook a number of treats equal to your Proficiency Bonus if you have Cook's Utensils on hand. These special treats last 8 hours after being made. A creature can eat one of these treats as a Bonus Action, gaining Temporary Hit Points equal to your Proficiency Bonus.
