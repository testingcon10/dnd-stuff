# Gunner

*General Feat*

You have a quick hand and keen eye when employing firearms, granting you the following benefits:

**Ability Score Increase.** Increase your Dexterity score by 1, to a maximum of 20.

**Firearms Proficiency.** You gain proficiency with firearms.

**Quick Draw.** Being within 5 feet of a hostile creature doesn't impose disadvantage on your ranged attack rolls.
