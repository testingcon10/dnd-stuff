# Athlete

*General Feat (Prerequisite: Strength, Dexterity, or Constitution 13+)*

You gain the following benefits.

**Ability Score Increase.** Increase your Strength, Dexterity, or Constitution score by 1, to a maximum of 20.

**Climb Speed.** You gain a Climb Speed equal to your Speed.

**Hop Up.** When you have the [[Prone]] condition, you can right yourself with only 5 feet of movement.

**Jumping.** Your jump distances are determined by your Strength or Dexterity score (your choice) instead of only Strength.
