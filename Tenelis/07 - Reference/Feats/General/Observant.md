# Observant

*General Feat (Prerequisite: Intelligence or Wisdom 13+)*

You gain the following benefits.

**Ability Score Increase.** Increase your Intelligence or Wisdom score by 1, to a maximum of 20.

**Keen Observer.** Choose one of the following skills: Insight, Investigation, or Perception. If you lack proficiency in the chosen skill, you gain proficiency in it, and if you already have proficiency in it, you gain Expertise in it.

**Quick Search.** You can take the Search action as a Bonus Action.
