# Polearm Master

When you take the Attack action with a glaive, halberd, quarterstaff, or spear, you can use a bonus action to make a melee attack with the opposite end of the weapon (1d4 bludgeoning). While wielding these weapons, other creatures provoke an opportunity attack from you when they enter your reach.
