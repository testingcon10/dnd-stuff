# Sharpshooter

Attacking at long range doesn't impose disadvantage on your ranged weapon attack rolls. Your ranged weapon attacks ignore half cover and three-quarters cover. Before making a ranged weapon attack with a proficient weapon, you can take a -5 penalty to the attack roll. If the attack hits, you add +10 to the damage.
