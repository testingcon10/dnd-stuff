# Grappler

You've developed the skills necessary to hold your own in close-quarters grappling. You have advantage on attack rolls against a creature you are grappling. You can use your action to try to pin a creature grappled by you; to do so, make another grapple check. If you succeed, you and the creature are both restrained until the grapple ends.
