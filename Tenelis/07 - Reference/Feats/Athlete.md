# Athlete

You have undergone extensive physical training. You increase your Strength or Dexterity by 1 (to a maximum of 20). When you are prone, standing up uses only 5 feet of your movement. Climbing doesn't cost you extra movement. You can make a running long jump or a running high jump after moving only 5 feet on foot, rather than 10 feet.
