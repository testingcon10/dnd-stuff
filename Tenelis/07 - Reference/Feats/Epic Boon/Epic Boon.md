# Epic Boon

*Epic Boon Feat (Prerequisite: Level 19+)*

Epic Boons are powerful feats available only at level 19 or higher when your class grants an Ability Score Improvement. Each boon also increases one ability score by 1.

| Feat | Benefit |
|------|---------|
| [[Boon of Combat Prowess]] | Turn one missed attack into a hit, once per turn |
| [[Boon of Dimensional Travel]] | [[Teleport]] up to 30 feet after taking the Attack or Magic action |
| [[Boon of Energy Resistance]] | Resistance to two [[Damage Types|damage types]]; redirect absorbed damage to nearby enemies |
| [[Boon of Fate]] | Add or subtract 2d4 from any nearby creature's d20 test |
| [[Boon of Fortitude]] | +40 max HP; gain bonus HP whenever you receive healing |
| [[Boon of Irresistible Offense]] | Ignore resistance to bludgeoning, piercing, and slashing; extra crit damage |
| [[Boon of Recovery]] | Survive lethal damage once per long rest; spend Hit Dice as a bonus action |
| [[Boon of Skill]] | Proficiency in all skills and expertise in five of them |
| [[Boon of Speed]] | +30 feet speed; advantage against opportunity attacks |
| [[Boon of Spell Recall]] | Chance to cast level 1-4 spells without expending a spell slot |
| [[Boon of the Night Spirit]] | 300-foot darkvision; teleport between shadows and hide as a bonus action |
| [[Boon of Truesight]] | 60-foot Truesight to see through illusions, invisibility, and darkness |
