# Exhaustion

Exhaustion is measured in six levels. Effects are cumulative: Level 1 - disadvantage on ability checks. Level 2 - speed halved. Level 3 - disadvantage on attack rolls and saving throws. Level 4 - hit point maximum halved. Level 5 - speed reduced to 0. Level 6 - death. A long rest reduces exhaustion by one level (if food/water available).
