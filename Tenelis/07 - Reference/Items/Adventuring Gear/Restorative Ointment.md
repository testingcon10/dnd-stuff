# Restorative Ointment

This glass jar, 3 inches in diameter, contains 1d4 + 1 doses of a thick mixture that smells faintly of aloe. As an action, one dose can be swallowed or applied to the skin to restore 2d8 + 2 hit points, and the creature ceases to be poisoned and is cured of any disease.
