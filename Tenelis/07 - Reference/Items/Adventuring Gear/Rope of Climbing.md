# Rope of Climbing

This 60-foot length of silk rope weighs 3 pounds and can hold up to 3,000 pounds. If you hold one end of the rope and use an action to speak the command word, the rope animates and moves at your direction, climbing along surfaces and tying itself to objects. It has AC 20, 20 hit points, and repairs itself in 1 minute.
