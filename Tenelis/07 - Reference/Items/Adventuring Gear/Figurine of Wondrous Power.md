# Figurine of Wondrous Power

A figurine of wondrous power is a statuette of a beast small enough to fit in a pocket. If you use an action to speak the command word and throw the figurine to a point on the ground within 60 feet of you, it becomes a living creature. Varieties include the bronze griffon, ebony fly, golden lions, ivory goats, marble elephant, obsidian steed, onyx dog, serpentine owl, and silver raven.
