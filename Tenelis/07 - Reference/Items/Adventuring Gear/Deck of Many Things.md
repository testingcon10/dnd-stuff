# Deck of Many Things

Usually found in a box or pouch, this deck contains a number of cards made of ivory or vellum. Before you draw, you must declare how many cards you intend to draw and then draw them randomly. Any cards drawn in excess of this number have no effect. Each card's magic takes effect immediately, with dramatic and often permanent consequences.
