# Sovereign Glue

This viscous, milky-white substance can form a permanent adhesive bond between any two objects. It must be stored in a jar or flask that has been coated inside with [[Oil of Slipperiness|oil of slipperiness]]. One ounce of the glue can cover a 1-foot square surface and takes 1 minute to set. Once set, the bond it creates can be broken only by the application of [[Universal Solvent|universal solvent]] or [[Oil of Etherealness|oil of etherealness]].
