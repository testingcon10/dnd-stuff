# Dust of Dryness

This small packet contains 1d6 + 4 pinches of dust. You can use an action to sprinkle a pinch over water, transforming a 15-foot cube of water into a marble-sized pellet. The pellet can be smashed against a hard surface to release the water. If a pinch of dust is sprinkled on a water elemental, it must make a DC 13 Constitution saving throw or take 10d6 necrotic damage.
