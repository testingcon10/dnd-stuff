# Well of Many Worlds

This fine black cloth, soft as silk, is folded up to the dimensions of a handkerchief. It unfolds into a circular sheet 6 feet in diameter. You can use an action to unfold and place the well on a solid surface, where it creates a two-way portal to another world or plane of existence chosen by the DM. The portal remains open for as long as the well is left in place.
