# Sphere of Annihilation

This 2-foot-diameter black sphere is a hole in the multiverse, hovering in space and stabilized by a magical field. Any matter that touches it is instantly obliterated. A creature within 5 feet of it must succeed on a DC 13 Dexterity saving throw or be sucked in and obliterated. You can control the sphere's movement with a DC 25 Arcana check.
