# Rod of Lordly Might

This rod has a flanged head, and it functions as a magic mace that grants a +3 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with it. The rod has six buttons along its haft, each of which causes the rod to transform in a different way, such as becoming a [[Flame Tongue|flame tongue]] longsword, a battleaxe, or a battering ram.
