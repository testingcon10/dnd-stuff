# Rod of Security

While holding this rod, you can use an action to activate it, transporting you and up to 199 other willing creatures you can see to a paradise that exists in an extraplanar space. You choose the form the paradise takes, and creatures regain hit points while resting there. The rod can't be used again for 10 days.
