# Rod of Rulership

You can use an action to present the rod and command obedience from each creature of your choice that you can see within 120 feet of you. Each target must succeed on a DC 15 Wisdom saving throw or be charmed by you for 8 hours. A charmed target regards you as its trusted leader. The rod can't be used again until the next dawn.
