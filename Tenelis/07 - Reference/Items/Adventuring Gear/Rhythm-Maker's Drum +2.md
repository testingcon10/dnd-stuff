# Rhythm-Maker's Drum, +2

While holding this drum, you gain a +2 bonus to spell attack rolls and to the saving throw DCs of your bard spells. As a bonus action, you can play the drum to regain one use of your Bardic Inspiration feature. This property can't be used again until the next dawn.
