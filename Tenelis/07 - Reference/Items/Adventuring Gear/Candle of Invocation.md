# Candle of Invocation

This slender taper is dedicated to a deity and shares that deity's alignment. Lighting the candle sheds dim light in a 30-foot radius for 4 hours. While lit, creatures within its light that share its alignment have advantage on attack rolls, saving throws, and ability checks. The candle can also be used to cast the gate spell.
