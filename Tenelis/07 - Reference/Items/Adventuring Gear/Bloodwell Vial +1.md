# Bloodwell Vial, +1

This glass vial contains a drop of blood and is attached to a necklace. While wearing it, you gain a +1 bonus to spell attack rolls and to the saving throw DCs of your sorcerer spells. In addition, when you roll any Hit Dice to recover hit points while carrying this vial, you can regain 5 sorcery points.
