# Wind Fan

While holding this fan, you can use an action to cast the [[Gust of Wind|gust of wind]] spell (save DC 13) from it. Once used, the fan shouldn't be used again until the next dawn. Each time it is used again before then, there is a cumulative 20 percent chance that the fan is destroyed and crumbles into useless, nonmagical tatters.
