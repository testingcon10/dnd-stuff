# Eversmoking Bottle

Smoke leaks from the lead-stoppered mouth of this brass bottle, which weighs 1 pound. When you use an action to remove the stopper, a cloud of thick smoke pours out in a 60-foot radius, and the cloud's radius increases by 10 feet each minute until it reaches 120 feet. The area within the cloud is heavily obscured.
