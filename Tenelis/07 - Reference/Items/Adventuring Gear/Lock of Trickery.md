# Lock of Trickery

This lock appears to be an ordinary lock and comes with a single key. A creature proficient with thieves' tools can pick this lock with a successful DC 15 Dexterity check. The DC to open it with the knock spell or similar magic is 20. The lock magically hides its keyhole, which can be found with a DC 15 Intelligence (Investigation) check.
