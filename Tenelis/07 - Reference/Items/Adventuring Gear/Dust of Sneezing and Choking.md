# Dust of Sneezing and Choking

Found in a small container, this powder resembles very fine sand. It appears to be [[Dust of Disappearance|dust of disappearance]], and an identify spell confirms this. When you use an action to throw a handful into the air, you and each creature that needs to breathe within 30 feet must succeed on a DC 15 Constitution saving throw or become unable to breathe while sneezing uncontrollably.
