# Sending Stones

Sending stones come in pairs, with each smooth stone carved to match the other so the pairing is easily recognized. While you touch one stone, you can use an action to cast the sending spell from it, targeting the bearer of the other stone. Once the sending spell is cast through the stones, they can't be used again until the next dawn.
