# Decanter of Endless Water

You can use an action to remove the stopper and speak one of three command words, causing fresh or salt water to pour out in varying amounts. "Stream" produces 1 gallon per round, "Fountain" produces 5 gallons per round, and "Geyser" produces 30 gallons per round in a 30-foot-long geyser.
