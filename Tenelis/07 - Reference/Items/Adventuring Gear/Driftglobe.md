# Driftglobe

This small sphere of thick glass weighs 1 pound. If you are within 60 feet of it, you can speak its command word and cause it to emanate the light or daylight spell. Once the daylight spell has been used, it can't be used again until the next dawn. The globe hovers in place and follows you at a distance of 5 feet.
