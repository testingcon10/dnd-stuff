# Ioun Stone of Reserve

This vibrant purple prism orbits your head while attuned and stores spells cast into it, holding them until you use them. The stone can store up to 3 levels worth of spells at a time. When found, it contains 1d4 - 1 levels of stored spells chosen by the DM.
