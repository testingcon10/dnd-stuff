# Ioun Stone of Greater Absorption

This marbled lavender and green ellipsoid orbits your head while attuned. You can use your reaction to cancel a spell of [[8th Level|8th level]] or lower cast by a creature you can see targeting only you. Once the stone has canceled 50 levels of spells, it burns out and turns dull gray, losing its magic.
