# Daern's Instant Fortress

You can use an action to place this 1-inch metal cube on the ground and speak its command word, causing it to rapidly grow into a fortress. The tower is 20 feet square and 30 feet high, with arrow slits on all sides and a battlement atop it. Creatures in the area when it appears must make a DC 15 Dexterity saving throw or take 10d10 bludgeoning damage.
