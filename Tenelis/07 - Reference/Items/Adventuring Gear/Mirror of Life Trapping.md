# Mirror of Life Trapping

When this 4-foot-tall mirror is viewed indirectly, its surface shows faint images of creatures. The mirror has up to twelve extradimensional cells that can trap creatures. Any creature other than you that sees its reflection in the mirror must succeed on a DC 15 Charisma saving throw or be trapped in one of the cells.
