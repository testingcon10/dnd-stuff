# Rope of Entanglement

This rope is 30 feet long and weighs 3 pounds. If you hold one end of the rope and use an action to speak its command word, the other end darts forward to entangle a creature you can see within 20 feet. The target must succeed on a DC 15 Dexterity saving throw or become restrained. You can release the creature by speaking a second command word.
