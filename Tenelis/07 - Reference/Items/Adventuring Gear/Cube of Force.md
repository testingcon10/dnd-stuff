# Cube of Force

This cube is about an inch across and has 36 charges. You can use an action to press one of the cube's six faces, expending charges to create a barrier of force centered on you. Each face creates a different wall effect that blocks specific types of matter or energy. The cube regains 1d36 expended charges daily at dawn.
