# Deck of Illusions

This box contains a set of parchment cards. A full deck has 34 cards. When you use an action to draw a card at random and throw it on the ground, an illusion of one or more creatures forms over the thrown card and remains until dispelled. The apparent creature is determined by the card drawn.
