# Perfume of Bewitching

This tiny vial contains magic perfume, enough for one use. You can use an action to apply the perfume to yourself, gaining advantage on all Charisma checks directed at humanoids of challenge rating 1 or lower for the next hour. Those subjected to the perfume's effect are not combative toward you unless you threaten or harm them.
