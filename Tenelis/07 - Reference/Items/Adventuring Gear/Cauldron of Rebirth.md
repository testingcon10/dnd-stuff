# Cauldron of Rebirth

This Tiny cauldron bears engravings of various creatures. You can use it as a spellcasting focus and it has 3 charges, regaining 1d3 charges daily at dawn. You can expend a charge to cast the revivify spell. You can also fill the cauldron with water and perform a 1-hour ritual to create a [[Potion of Greater Healing|potion of greater healing]].
