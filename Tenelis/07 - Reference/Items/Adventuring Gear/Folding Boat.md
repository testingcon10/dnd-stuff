# Folding Boat

This object appears as a wooden box that measures 12 inches long, 6 inches wide, and 6 inches deep. It can be commanded to unfold into a boat 10 feet long or a ship 24 feet long. The boat can hold four Medium creatures, while the ship can hold fifteen and has a sailing speed.
