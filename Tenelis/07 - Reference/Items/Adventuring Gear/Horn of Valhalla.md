# Horn of Valhalla

You can use an action to blow this horn, summoning warrior spirits from the plane of Ysgard to fight for you. They arrive within 60 feet of you and use the statistics of a berserker. They return to Ysgard after 1 hour or when they drop to 0 hit points. The horn comes in silver, brass, bronze, and iron varieties with increasing power.
