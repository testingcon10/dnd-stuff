# Wand of Binding

This wand has 7 charges and regains 1d6 + 1 expended charges daily at dawn. While holding it, you can use an action to expend charges to cast the [[Hold Monster|hold monster]] spell (5 charges) or the [[Hold Person|hold person]] spell (2 charges) from it, using your spell save DC.
