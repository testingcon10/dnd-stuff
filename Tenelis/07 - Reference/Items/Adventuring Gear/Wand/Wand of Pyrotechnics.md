# Wand of Pyrotechnics

This wand has 7 charges and regains 1d6 + 1 expended charges daily at dawn. While holding it, you can use an action to expend 1 charge and create a harmless burst of multicolored light at a point you can see up to 60 feet away, accompanied by a crackling noise heard 300 feet away.
