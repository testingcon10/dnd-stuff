# Wand of Fireballs

This wand has 7 charges and regains 1d6 + 1 expended charges daily at dawn. While holding it, you can use an action to expend 1 or more charges to cast the fireball spell (save DC 15) from it. For 1 charge, you cast the 3rd-level version, and the spell level increases by one for each additional charge expended.
