# Wand of Enemy Detection

This wand has 7 charges and regains 1d6 + 1 expended charges daily at dawn. While holding it, you can use an action and expend 1 charge to speak its command word, learning the direction of the nearest creature hostile to you within 60 feet, though not its distance or identity.
