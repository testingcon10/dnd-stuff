# Wand of Magic Missiles

This wand has 7 charges and regains 1d6 + 1 expended charges daily at dawn. While holding it, you can use an action to expend 1 or more charges to cast the [[Magic Missile|magic missile]] spell from it. For 1 charge, you cast the 1st-level version, and the spell level increases by one for each additional charge expended.
