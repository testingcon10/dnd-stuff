# Wand of Paralysis

This wand has 7 charges and regains 1d6 + 1 expended charges daily at dawn. While holding it, you can use an action to expend 1 charge to cause a thin blue ray to streak from the tip toward a creature within 60 feet. The target must succeed on a DC 15 Constitution saving throw or be paralyzed for 1 minute.
