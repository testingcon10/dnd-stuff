# Wand of Fear

This wand has 7 charges and regains 1d6 + 1 expended charges daily at dawn. While holding the wand, you can use an action to expend 1 charge to issue a command that causes all creatures within 60 feet to become frightened, or expend 2 charges to cast the fear spell (save DC 15).
