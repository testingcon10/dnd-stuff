# Wand of Scowls

This wand has 3 charges. While holding it, you can use an action to expend 1 charge and target a humanoid you can see within 30 feet. The target must succeed on a DC 10 Charisma saving throw or be forced to scowl for 1 minute. The wand regains all expended charges daily at dawn.
