# Wand of Wonder

This wand has 7 charges and regains 1d6 + 1 expended charges daily at dawn. While holding it, you can use an action to expend 1 charge and choose a target within 120 feet. The target can be a creature, object, or point in space. Roll d100 and consult the table to determine the wand's random magical effect.
