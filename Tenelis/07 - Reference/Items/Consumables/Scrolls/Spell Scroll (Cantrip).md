# Spell Scroll (Cantrip)

A spell scroll bears the words of a single cantrip, written in a mystical cipher. If the spell is on your class's spell list, you can read the scroll and cast the spell without providing any material components. The spell save DC is 13 and the attack bonus is +5. Once the spell is cast, the scroll crumbles to dust.
