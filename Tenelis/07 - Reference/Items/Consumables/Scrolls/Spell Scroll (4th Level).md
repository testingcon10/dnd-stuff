# Spell Scroll (4th Level)

A spell scroll bears the words of a single 4th-level spell, written in a mystical cipher. If the spell is on your class's spell list, you can read the scroll and cast the spell at [[4th Level|4th level]] without providing any material components. The spell save DC is 15 and the attack bonus is +7. If the spell is not on your class's spell list, you must make a DC 14 ability check using your spellcasting ability or the scroll is wasted.
