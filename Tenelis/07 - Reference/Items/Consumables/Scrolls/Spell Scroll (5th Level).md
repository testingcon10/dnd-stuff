# Spell Scroll (5th Level)

A spell scroll bears the words of a single 5th-level spell, written in a mystical cipher. If the spell is on your class's spell list, you can read the scroll and cast the spell at [[5th Level|5th level]] without providing any material components. The spell save DC is 17 and the attack bonus is +9. If the spell is not on your class's spell list, you must make a DC 15 ability check using your spellcasting ability or the scroll is wasted.
