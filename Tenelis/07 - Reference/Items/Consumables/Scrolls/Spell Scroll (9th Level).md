# Spell Scroll (9th Level)

A spell scroll bears the words of a single 9th-level spell, written in a mystical cipher. If the spell is on your class's spell list, you can read the scroll and cast the spell at [[9th Level|9th level]] without providing any material components. The spell save DC is 19 and the attack bonus is +11. If the spell is not on your class's spell list, you must make a DC 19 ability check using your spellcasting ability or the scroll is wasted.
