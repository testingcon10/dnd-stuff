# Potion of Diminution

When you drink this potion, you gain the "reduce" effect of the [[Enlarge-Reduce|Enlarge/Reduce]] spell for 1d4 hours (no concentration required). The red in the potion's liquid continuously contracts to a tiny bead and then expands to color the clear liquid around it.
