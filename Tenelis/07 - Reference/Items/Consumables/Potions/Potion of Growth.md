# Potion of Growth

When you drink this potion, you gain the "enlarge" effect of the [[Enlarge-Reduce|Enlarge/Reduce]] spell for 1d4 hours (no concentration required). The red in the potion's liquid continuously expands from a tiny bead to color the clear liquid around it and then contracts.
