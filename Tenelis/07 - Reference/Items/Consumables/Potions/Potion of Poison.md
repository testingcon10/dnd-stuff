# Potion of Poison

This concoction looks, smells, and tastes like a [[Potion of Healing]] or other beneficial potion. However, it is actually poison masked by illusion magic. If you drink it, you take 3d6 poison damage and must succeed on a DC 13 Constitution saving throw or be poisoned, taking 3d6 poison damage at the start of each of your turns for the duration. The poisoned condition lasts until the damage is reduced to 0.
