# Oil of Slipperiness

This sticky black unguent is thick and heavy in the container, but it flows quickly when poured. The oil can cover a Medium or smaller creature along with its equipment, requiring 10 minutes to apply. The affected creature then gains the effect of a [[Freedom of Movement]] spell for 8 hours. Alternatively, the oil can be poured on the ground as an action, duplicating the [[Grease]] spell in a 10-foot square for 8 hours.
