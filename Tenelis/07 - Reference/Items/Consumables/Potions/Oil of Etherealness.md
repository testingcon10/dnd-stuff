# Oil of Etherealness

Beads of this cloudy gray oil form on the outside of its container and quickly evaporate. The oil can cover a Medium or smaller creature along with its equipment. Applying the oil takes 10 minutes, and the affected creature then gains the effect of the [[Etherealness]] spell for 1 hour.
