# Bead of Force

This small black sphere measures 3/4 of an inch in diameter and weighs an ounce. You can use an action to throw the bead up to 60 feet, where it explodes on impact dealing 5d4 force damage in a 10-foot radius. A sphere of transparent force then encloses the area for 1 minute.
