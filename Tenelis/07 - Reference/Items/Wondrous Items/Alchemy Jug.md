# Alchemy Jug

This ceramic jug appears to be able to hold a gallon of liquid and weighs 12 pounds whether full or empty. You can use an action and name one liquid from the jug's table to cause the jug to produce the chosen liquid. The jug can produce a variety of liquids including acid, [[Basic Poison|basic poison]], beer, honey, mayonnaise, oil, vinegar, water, and wine.
