# Cubic Gate

This cube is 3 inches across and radiates palpable magical energy. Each of the six sides is keyed to a different plane of existence. You can use an action to press one side of the cube to cast the gate spell, opening a portal to the plane keyed to that side, or use two charges to cast the [[Plane Shift|plane shift]] spell.
