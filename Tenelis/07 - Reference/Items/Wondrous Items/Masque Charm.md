# Masque Charm

A masque charm is a small silver pin. While you are wearing it, you can use an action to cast the [[Disguise Self]] spell (DC 13 to discern the disguise). Once the spell is cast, it can't be cast from the charm again until the next dawn. When the spell ends, the charm crumbles to dust.
