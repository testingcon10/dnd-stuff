# Quiver of Ehlonna

Each of the quiver's three compartments connects to an extradimensional space. The shortest compartment holds up to sixty arrows or similar objects, the midsize compartment holds up to eighteen javelins or similar objects, and the longest compartment holds up to six long objects such as bows or quarterstaves. The quiver weighs 2 pounds regardless of its contents.
