# Horseshoes of a Zephyr

These iron horseshoes come in a set of four. While all four shoes are affixed to the hooves of a horse or similar creature, they allow the creature to move normally while floating 4 inches above the ground. The creature leaves no tracks and ignores difficult terrain. It can also cross or stand above liquid surfaces.
