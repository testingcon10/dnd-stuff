# Devotee's Censer

This censer can be wielded as a flail that deals an extra 1d8 radiant damage on a hit. When you use it as a [[Holy Symbol|holy symbol]] to cast a spell that restores hit points, the target of the spell regains 1d4 additional hit points. As a bonus action, you can speak the censer's command word to fill it with holy incense, creating a 10-foot radius of healing smoke.
