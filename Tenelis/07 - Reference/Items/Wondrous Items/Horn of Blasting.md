# Horn of Blasting

You can use an action to speak the horn's command word and then blow the horn, which emits a thunderous blast in a 30-foot cone. Each creature in the cone must make a DC 15 Constitution saving throw, taking 5d6 thunder damage on a failed save or half on a success. Glass and crystal objects in the cone take 10d6 thunder damage.
