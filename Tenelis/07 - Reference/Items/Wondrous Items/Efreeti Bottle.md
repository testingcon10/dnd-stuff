# Efreeti Bottle

When you use an action to remove the stopper of this painted brass bottle, a cloud of thick smoke flows out. At the end of your turn, the smoke disappears with a flash of harmless fire, and an efreeti appears. The DM rolls to determine the efreeti's attitude and the result, which can range from the efreeti attacking you to serving you for a period of time.
