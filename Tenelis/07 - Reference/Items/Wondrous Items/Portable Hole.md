# Portable Hole

This fine black cloth, soft as silk, is folded up to the dimensions of a handkerchief. It unfolds into a circular sheet 6 feet in diameter. You can use an action to unfold it and place it on a solid surface, creating a 10-foot-deep extradimensional hole. Placing the hole inside an extradimensional space such as a [[Bag of Holding|bag of holding]] creates a rift to the Astral Plane.
