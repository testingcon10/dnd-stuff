# Bag of Tricks

This ordinary bag, made from gray, rust, or tan cloth, appears empty. Reaching inside the bag reveals a small, fuzzy object. You can use an action to pull the fuzzy object from the bag and throw it up to 20 feet, where it transforms into a random creature determined by rolling on the bag's table. The creature is friendly to you and vanishes at the next dawn.
