# Heward's Handy Haversack

This backpack has a central pouch and two side pouches, each of which is an extradimensional space. The central pouch can hold up to 8 cubic feet or 80 pounds of gear, while each side pouch can hold up to 20 pounds. The backpack always weighs 5 pounds regardless of its contents, and retrieving an item from it requires only a bonus action.
