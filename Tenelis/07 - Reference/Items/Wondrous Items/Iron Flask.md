# Iron Flask

This iron bottle has a brass stopper. You can use an action to speak the flask's command word, targeting a creature you can see within 60 feet that originated on another plane. The target must succeed on a DC 17 Wisdom saving throw or be trapped inside the flask. A trapped creature can be released and forced to obey your commands for 1 hour.
