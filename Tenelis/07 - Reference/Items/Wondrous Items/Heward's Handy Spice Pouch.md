# Heward's Handy Spice Pouch

This belt pouch appears empty and has 10 charges. While holding the pouch, you can use an action to expend 1 charge, speak the name of any nonmagical food seasoning, and remove a pinch of the desired seasoning from the pouch. The pouch regains 1d6 + 4 charges daily at dawn.
