# Instrument of the Bards

An instrument of the bards is an exquisite musical instrument that bestows magical abilities on its attuned bard. A creature that attempts to play an instrument of the bards without being attuned takes psychic damage. Each instrument allows a bard to cast certain spells using the instrument, with seven types of increasing rarity from Fochlucan to Ollamh.
