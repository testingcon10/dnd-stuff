# Iron Bands of Bilarro

This rusty iron sphere measures 3 inches in diameter and weighs 1 pound. You can use an action to speak the command word and throw the sphere at a Huge or smaller creature within 60 feet. On a successful ranged attack, the sphere transforms into bands of iron that restrain the target until you use a bonus action to release it or the target breaks free with a DC 20 Strength check.
