# Bottle of Boundless Coffee

This bottle comes with a stopper and appears to be empty. However, while the stopper is removed, you can use an action to pour a serving of delicious, piping hot coffee from the bottle. The bottle never runs out of coffee, which is always hot. A creature that drinks coffee from the bottle has advantage on saving throws against the exhaustion condition for the next hour. The coffee loses its magical properties if it isn't consumed within 1 hour of being poured.
