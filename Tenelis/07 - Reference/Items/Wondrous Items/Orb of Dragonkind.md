# Orb of Dragonkind

Ages past, elves and humans waged war against evil dragons and created five Orbs of Dragonkind to help them in their fight. While attuned to an orb, you can use an action to peer into the orb's depths and speak its command word, summoning a dragon of the orb's type from within 40 miles. The orb also grants powerful abilities but carries a significant curse.
