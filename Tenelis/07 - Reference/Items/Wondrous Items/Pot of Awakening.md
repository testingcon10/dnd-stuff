# Pot of Awakening

If you plant an ordinary shrub in this 10-pound clay pot and let it grow for 30 days, the shrub magically transforms into an awakened shrub at the end of that period. When the shrub awakens, its roots break the pot, destroying it. The awakened shrub is friendly toward you and follows your verbal commands.
