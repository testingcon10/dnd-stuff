# Brazier of Commanding Fire Elementals

While a fire burns in this brass brazier, you can use an action to speak the brazier's command word and summon a fire elemental, as if you had cast the [[Conjure Elemental|conjure elemental]] spell. The brazier can't be used this way again until the next dawn.
