# Nolzur's Marvelous Pigments

Typically found in 1d4 pots inside a fine wooden box with a brush, these pigments allow you to create three-dimensional objects by painting them in two dimensions. The paint flows from the brush to form the desired object as you concentrate on its image. Each pot of paint is sufficient to cover 1,000 square feet, and the painted objects become real.
