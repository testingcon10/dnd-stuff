# Gem of Brightness

This prism has 50 charges. While you are holding it, you can use an action to speak one of three command words to cause one of three effects: emit bright light in a 30-foot radius, fire a beam of light at a creature within 60 feet that must succeed on a DC 15 Constitution saving throw or be blinded, or cause the gem to flare with blinding light in a 30-foot cone.
