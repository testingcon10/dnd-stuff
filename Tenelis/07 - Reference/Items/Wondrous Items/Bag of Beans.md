# Bag of Beans

Inside this heavy cloth bag are 3d4 dry beans. When you dump the bag's contents on the ground, they explode in a 10-foot radius dealing 5d4 fire damage. If you plant a bean in dirt or sand and then water it, a random magical effect occurs 1 minute later from the spot where it was planted.
