# Net

A mesh of rope used to entangle creatures. A Large or smaller creature hit by a net is restrained until it is freed. A creature can use its action to make a DC 10 Strength check to free itself or another creature. Properties: special, thrown (range 5/15). Weight: 3 lb. Cost: 1 gp.
