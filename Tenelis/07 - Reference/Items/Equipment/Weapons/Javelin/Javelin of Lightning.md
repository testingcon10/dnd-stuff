# Javelin of Lightning

This javelin is a [[Magic Weapon|magic weapon]]. When you hurl it and speak its command word, it transforms into a bolt of lightning, forming a line 5 feet wide that extends out from you to a target within 120 feet. Each creature in the line excluding you and the target must make a DC 13 Dexterity saving throw, taking 4d6 lightning damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one. The javelin then reverts to its normal form and its property can't be used again until the next dawn.
