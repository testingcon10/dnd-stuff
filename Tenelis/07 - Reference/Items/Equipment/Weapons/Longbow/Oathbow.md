# Oathbow

When you nock an arrow on this bow, it whispers in Elvish, "Swift defeat to my enemies." When you use this weapon to make a ranged attack and say the command phrase "Swift death to you who have wronged me," the target of that attack becomes your sworn enemy until it dies or until dawn seven days later. You have advantage on attack rolls against your sworn enemy, your attacks deal an extra 3d6 piercing damage against it, and it gains no benefit from cover other than total cover. You can have only one sworn enemy at a time, and you have disadvantage on attack rolls with all other weapons while your sworn enemy lives.
