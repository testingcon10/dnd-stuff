# Moon Sickle, +3

You gain a +3 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this [[Magic Weapon|magic weapon]]. While holding this silver-bladed sickle under moonlight, you gain a +3 bonus to spell attack rolls and to the saving throw DCs of your druid and ranger spells. In addition, you can use the sickle as a spellcasting focus for your druid and ranger spells, and when you cast a spell that restores hit points, you can roll a d4 and add the result to the total hit points restored.
