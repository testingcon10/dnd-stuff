# Defender

You gain a +3 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this [[Magic Weapon|magic weapon]]. The first time you attack with the sword on each of your turns, you can transfer some or all of the sword's bonus to your Armor Class instead of using the bonus on any attacks that turn. The adjusted bonuses remain in effect until the start of your next turn.
