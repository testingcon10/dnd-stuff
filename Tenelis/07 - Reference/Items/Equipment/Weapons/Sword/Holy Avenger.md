# Holy Avenger

You gain a +3 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this [[Magic Weapon|magic weapon]]. When you hit a fiend or an undead with it, that creature takes an extra 2d10 radiant damage. While you hold the drawn sword, it creates an aura in a 10-foot radius around you that grants you and all friendly creatures in the aura advantage on saving throws against spells and other magical effects.
