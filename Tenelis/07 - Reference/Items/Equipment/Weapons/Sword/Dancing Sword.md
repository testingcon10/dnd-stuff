# Dancing Sword

You can use a bonus action to toss this magic sword into the air and speak the command word. When you do so, the sword begins to hover, flies up to 30 feet, and attacks one creature of your choice within 5 feet of it using your attack bonus and ability modifier for damage. On each of your turns, you can use a bonus action to cause the sword to fly up to 30 feet and repeat the attack against a target within 5 feet of it. After the sword attacks for the fourth time, it flies up to 30 feet and tries to return to your hand.
