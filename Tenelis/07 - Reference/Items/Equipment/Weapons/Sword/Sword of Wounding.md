# Sword of Wounding

Hit points lost to this weapon's damage can be regained only through a short or long rest, rather than by regeneration, magic, or any other means. Once per turn, when you hit a creature with an attack using this [[Magic Weapon|magic weapon]], you can wound the target. The wounded target takes 1d4 necrotic damage at the start of each of its turns for each time you've wounded it. The target or a creature within 5 feet can use an action to make a DC 15 Wisdom ([[Medicine]]) check, ending the wound on a success.
