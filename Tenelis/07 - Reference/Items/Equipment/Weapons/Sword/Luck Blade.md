# Luck Blade

You gain a +1 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this [[Magic Weapon|magic weapon]]. While the sword is on your person, you also gain a +1 bonus to saving throws. The sword has 1d4 - 1 charges. While holding it, you can use an action to expend 1 charge to cast the wish spell from it. Once all charges are expended, the sword loses this property.
