# Giant Slayer

You gain a +1 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this [[Magic Weapon|magic weapon]]. When you hit a Giant with it, the giant takes an extra 2d6 damage of the weapon's type and must succeed on a DC 15 Strength saving throw or fall prone.
