# Berserker Axe

You gain a +1 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this [[Magic Weapon|magic weapon]]. In addition, while you are attuned to this weapon, your hit point maximum increases by 1 for each level you have attained. When you are damaged by a hostile creature, you must succeed on a DC 15 Wisdom saving throw or go berserk, using your action each round to attack the nearest creature with this weapon until no creatures are within 60 feet that you can see or hear.
