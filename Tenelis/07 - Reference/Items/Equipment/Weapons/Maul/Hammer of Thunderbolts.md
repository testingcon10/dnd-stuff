# Hammer of Thunderbolts

You gain a +1 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this [[Magic Weapon|magic weapon]]. While you wear a belt of giant strength and [[Gauntlets of Ogre Power|gauntlets of ogre power]], the bonus increases to +4, you gain immunity to giant-thrown boulder attacks, and when you hit a giant with this weapon, it must succeed on a DC 17 Constitution saving throw or die. You can also use the weapon to hurl a thunderbolt at a point within 120 feet, requiring each creature within 30 feet to make a DC 17 Dexterity saving throw, taking 5d6 thunder and 5d6 lightning damage on a failure.
