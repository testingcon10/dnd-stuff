# Staff of the Magi

This staff has 50 charges and can be wielded as a magic quarterstaff that grants a +2 bonus to attack and damage rolls and a +2 bonus to spell attack rolls. While holding it, you have advantage on saving throws against spells, and you can use an action to expend charges to cast a wide variety of spells. When the last charge is expended, you can break the staff in a retributive strike, creating a devastating explosion. The staff regains 4d6 + 2 expended charges daily at dawn.
