# Staff of Fire

You have resistance to fire damage while you hold this staff. The staff has 10 charges and regains 1d6 + 4 expended charges daily at dawn. If you expend the last charge, roll a d20; on a 1, the staff blackens and crumbles to ash. While holding it, you can use an action to expend charges to cast [[Burning Hands|burning hands]] (1 charge), fireball (3 charges), or [[Wall of Fire|wall of fire]] (4 charges) from it, using your spell save DC.
