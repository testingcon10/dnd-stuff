# Staff of Thunder and Lightning

This staff can be wielded as a magic quarterstaff that grants a +2 bonus to attack and damage rolls. It also has several special abilities that can each be used once per day: Lightning (5d6 lightning damage on a hit), Thunder (DC 17 Constitution save or stunned for 1 turn on a hit), Lightning Strike (8d6 [[Lightning Bolt|lightning bolt]]), [[Thunderclap]] (DC 17 Constitution save or deafened/stunned in a 60-foot radius), and Thunder and Lightning (combines Lightning and Thunder on a single hit).
