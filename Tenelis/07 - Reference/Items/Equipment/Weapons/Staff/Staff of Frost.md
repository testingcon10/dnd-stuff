# Staff of Frost

You have resistance to cold damage while you hold this staff. The staff has 10 charges and regains 1d6 + 4 expended charges daily at dawn. If you expend the last charge, roll a d20; on a 1, the staff turns to water and is destroyed. While holding it, you can use an action to expend charges to cast [[Cone of Cold|cone of cold]] (5 charges), [[Fog Cloud|fog cloud]] (1 charge), [[Ice Storm|ice storm]] (4 charges), or [[Wall of Ice|wall of ice]] (4 charges) from it, using your spell save DC.
