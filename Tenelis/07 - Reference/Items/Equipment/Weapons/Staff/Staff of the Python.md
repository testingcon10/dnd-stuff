# Staff of the Python

You can use an action to speak this staff's command word and throw it on the ground within 10 feet of you. The staff becomes a giant constrictor snake under your control. The snake acts on your turn and reverts to staff form when it drops to 0 hit points, when you use a bonus action to revert it, or when you die. If the snake is reduced to 0 hit points, the staff is destroyed.
