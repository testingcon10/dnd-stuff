# Staff of Swarming Insects

This staff has 10 charges and regains 1d6 + 4 expended charges daily at dawn. If you expend the last charge, roll a d20; on a 1, a swarm of insects consumes the staff and destroys it. While holding the staff, you can use an action to expend charges to cast [[Giant Insect|giant insect]] (4 charges) or [[Insect Plague|insect plague]] (5 charges) from it, using your spell save DC. You can also use an action to expend 1 charge to cause a swarm of harmless flying insects to spread out in a 30-foot radius from you, creating heavily obscured area for 10 minutes.
