# Staff of Power

This staff can be wielded as a magic quarterstaff that grants a +2 bonus to attack and damage rolls and to AC, spell attack rolls, and saving throw DCs. It has 20 charges for spellcasting ([[Cone of Cold|cone of cold]], fireball, [[Globe of Invulnerability|globe of invulnerability]], [[Hold Monster|hold monster]], levitate, [[Lightning Bolt|lightning bolt]], [[Magic Missile|magic missile]], [[Ray of Enfeeblement|ray of enfeeblement]], [[Wall of Force|wall of force]]). It regains 2d8 + 4 charges daily at dawn. If you expend the last charge, roll a d20 — on a 1, the staff triggers a retributive strike.
