# Staff of Birdcalls

This wooden staff is decorated with bird carvings. It has 10 charges. While holding it, you can use an action to expend 1 charge to create one of the following sounds: a finch's chirp, a raven's caw, a loon's call, a goose's honk, an owl's hoot, or a random bird sound. The staff regains 1d6 + 4 expended charges daily at dawn. If you expend the last charge, roll a d20; on a 1, the staff explodes in a harmless cloud of feathers.
