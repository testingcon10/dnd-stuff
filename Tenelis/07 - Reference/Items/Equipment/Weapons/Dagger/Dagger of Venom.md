# Dagger of Venom

You gain a +1 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this [[Magic Weapon|magic weapon]]. You can use an action to cause thick, black poison to coat the blade, which lasts for 1 minute or until an attack using this weapon hits a creature. That creature must succeed on a DC 15 Constitution saving throw or take 2d10 poison damage and become poisoned for 1 minute. The dagger can't be used this way again until the next dawn.
