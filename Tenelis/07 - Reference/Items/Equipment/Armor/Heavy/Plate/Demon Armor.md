# Demon Armor

While wearing this armor, you gain a +1 bonus to AC, and you can understand and speak Abyssal. The armor's clawed gauntlets turn unarmed strikes into magic weapons that deal 1d8 slashing damage. The armor has a curse: once you don it, you can't doff it unless you are targeted by a [[Remove Curse|remove curse]] spell or similar magic, and you have disadvantage on attack rolls and saving throws against demons.
