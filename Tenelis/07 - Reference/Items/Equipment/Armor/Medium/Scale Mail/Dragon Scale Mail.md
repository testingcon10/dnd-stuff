# Dragon Scale Mail

Dragon [[Scale Mail|scale mail]] is made of the scales of one kind of dragon. You gain a +1 bonus to AC, advantage on saving throws against the Frightful Presence and breath weapons of dragons, and resistance to one damage type determined by the kind of dragon that provided the scales. Additionally, you can use an action to magically discern the distance and direction to the closest dragon of that kind within 30 miles of you.
