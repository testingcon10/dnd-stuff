# Shield of Missile Attraction

While holding this shield, you have resistance to damage from ranged weapon attacks. The shield is cursed: attuning to it curses you until you are targeted by a [[Remove Curse|remove curse]] spell or similar magic. Whenever a ranged weapon attack is made against a target within 10 feet of you, the curse causes you to become the target instead.
