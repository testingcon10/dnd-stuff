# Medallion of Thoughts

This medallion has 3 charges. While wearing it, you can use an action and expend 1 charge to cast the [[Detect Thoughts|detect thoughts]] spell (save DC 13) from it. The medallion regains 1d3 expended charges daily at dawn.
