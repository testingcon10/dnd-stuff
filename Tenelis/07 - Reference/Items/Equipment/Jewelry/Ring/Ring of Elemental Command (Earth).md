# Ring of Elemental Command (Earth)

While wearing this ring, you have advantage on attack rolls against elementals from the Elemental Plane of Earth, and they have disadvantage on attack rolls against you. You can also speak and understand Terran. You have access to properties that include casting [[Stone Shape|stone shape]], stoneskin, [[Wall of Stone|wall of stone]], [[Dominate Monster|dominate monster]] on earth elementals, and [[Move Earth|move earth]], along with resistance to acid damage.
