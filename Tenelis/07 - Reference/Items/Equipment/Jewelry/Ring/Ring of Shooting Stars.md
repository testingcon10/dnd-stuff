# Ring of Shooting Stars

While wearing this ring in dim light or darkness, you can cast [[Dancing Lights|dancing lights]] and light from the ring at will. The ring has 6 charges and regains 1d6 charges daily at dawn. You can expend charges to use the [[Faerie Fire]], Ball Lightning, or Shooting Stars properties.
