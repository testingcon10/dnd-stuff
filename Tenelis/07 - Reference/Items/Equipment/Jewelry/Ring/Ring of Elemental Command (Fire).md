# Ring of Elemental Command (Fire)

While wearing this ring, you have advantage on attack rolls against elementals from the Elemental Plane of Fire, and they have disadvantage on attack rolls against you. You can also speak and understand Ignan. You have access to properties that include casting [[Burning Hands|burning hands]], fireball, [[Wall of Fire|wall of fire]], [[Dominate Monster|dominate monster]] on fire elementals, and [[Fire Shield|fire shield]], along with immunity to fire damage.
