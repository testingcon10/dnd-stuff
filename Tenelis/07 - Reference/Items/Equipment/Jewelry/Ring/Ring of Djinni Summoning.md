# Ring of Djinni Summoning

While wearing this ring, you can speak its command word as an action to summon a particular djinni from the Elemental Plane of Air. The djinni appears in an unoccupied space you choose within 120 feet of you and remains as long as you concentrate, up to 1 hour. After the djinni departs, it can't be summoned again for 24 hours, and the ring becomes nonmagical if the djinni dies.
