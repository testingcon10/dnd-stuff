# Ring of Elemental Command (Air)

While wearing this ring, you have advantage on attack rolls against elementals from the Elemental Plane of Air, and they have disadvantage on attack rolls against you. You can also speak and understand Auran. You have access to properties that include casting [[Wind Wall|wind wall]], [[Dominate Monster|dominate monster]] on air elementals, [[Chain Lightning|chain lightning]], and [[Gust of Wind|gust of wind]] at will, along with resistance to lightning damage and the ability to fly.
