# Ring of the Ram

This ring has 3 charges and regains 1d3 expended charges daily at dawn. While wearing the ring, you can use an action to expend 1 to 3 of its charges to make a ranged spell attack against one creature you can see within 60 feet of you. The ring produces a spectral ram's head that deals 2d10 force damage per charge spent and can push the target 5 feet away per charge.
