# Ring of Elemental Command (Water)

While wearing this ring, you have advantage on attack rolls against elementals from the Elemental Plane of Water, and they have disadvantage on attack rolls against you. You can also speak and understand Aquan. You have access to properties that include casting [[Create or Destroy Water|create or destroy water]], [[Control Water|control water]], [[Ice Storm|ice storm]], [[Wall of Ice|wall of ice]], and [[Dominate Monster|dominate monster]] on water elementals, along with the ability to breathe underwater and swim.
