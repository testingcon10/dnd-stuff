# Ring of Animal Influence

This ring has 3 charges and regains 1d3 expended charges daily at dawn. While wearing the ring, you can use an action to expend 1 charge to cast [[Animal Friendship|animal friendship]] (save DC 13), fear (save DC 13) targeting only beasts, or [[Speak with Animals|speak with animals]] from the ring.
