# Necklace of Fireballs

This necklace has 1d6 + 3 beads hanging from it. You can use an action to detach a bead and throw it up to 60 feet away, where it detonates as a 3rd-level fireball spell (save DC 15). You can hurl multiple beads as part of the same action, increasing the level of the fireball by 1 for each bead beyond the first.
