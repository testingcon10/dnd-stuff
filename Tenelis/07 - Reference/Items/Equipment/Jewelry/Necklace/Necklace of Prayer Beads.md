# Necklace of Prayer Beads

This necklace has 1d4 + 2 magic beads made from aquamarine, black pearl, or topaz. Each bead has a specific magical property that can be activated as a bonus action, allowing you to cast spells such as bless, [[Cure Wounds|cure wounds]], [[Lesser Restoration|lesser restoration]], [[Greater Restoration|greater restoration]], [[Branding Smite|branding smite]], [[Planar Ally|planar ally]], or [[Wind Walk|wind walk]] depending on the bead type.
