# Amulet of the Planes

While wearing this amulet, you can use an action to name a location that you are familiar with on another plane of existence, then make a DC 15 Intelligence check. On a success, you cast the [[Plane Shift|plane shift]] spell. On a failure, you and each creature and object within 15 feet of you travel to a random destination.
