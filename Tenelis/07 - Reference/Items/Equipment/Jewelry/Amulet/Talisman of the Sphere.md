# Talisman of the Sphere

When you make an Intelligence (Arcana) check to control a [[Sphere of Annihilation|sphere of annihilation]], you do so with double your proficiency bonus on the check. In addition, when you start your turn with control over a [[Sphere of Annihilation|sphere of annihilation]], you can use an action to levitate it 10 feet plus a number of additional feet equal to 10 times your Intelligence modifier.
