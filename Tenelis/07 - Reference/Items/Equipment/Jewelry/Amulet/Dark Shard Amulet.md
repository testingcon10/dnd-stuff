# Dark Shard Amulet

This amulet is fashioned from a single shard of resilient extraplanar material originating from the realm of your warlock patron. While you are wearing it, you can use it as a spellcasting focus for your warlock spells, and you can try to cast a cantrip that you don't know by succeeding on a DC 10 Intelligence ([[Arcana]]) check.
