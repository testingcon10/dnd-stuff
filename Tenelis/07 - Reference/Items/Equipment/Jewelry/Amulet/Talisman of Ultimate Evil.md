# Talisman of Ultimate Evil

This item symbolizes unrepentant evil. A creature that is neither good nor evil in alignment takes 6d6 necrotic damage upon touching the talisman. The talisman has 6 charges. As an action, you can expend 1 charge and target one creature on the ground that you can see within 120 feet of you, opening a flaming fissure beneath it. The target must succeed on a DC 20 Dexterity saving throw or fall into the fissure and be destroyed, leaving no remains.
