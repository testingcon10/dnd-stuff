# Clockwork Amulet

This copper amulet contains tiny interlocking gears and is powered by magic from Mechanus. When you make an attack roll while wearing the amulet, you can forgo rolling the d20 to get a 10 on the die. Once used, this property can't be used again until the next dawn.
