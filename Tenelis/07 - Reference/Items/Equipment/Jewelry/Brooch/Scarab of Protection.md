# Scarab of Protection

This scarab-shaped brooch has 12 charges and gives you advantage on saving throws against spells. If you succeed on a saving throw against a necromancy spell or a harmful effect originating from an undead creature, you can use your reaction to expend 1 charge to turn the effect back on the caster, who must make its own saving throw against the spell or effect.
