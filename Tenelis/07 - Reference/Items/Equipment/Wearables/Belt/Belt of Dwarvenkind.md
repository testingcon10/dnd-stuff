# Belt of Dwarvenkind

While wearing this belt, you gain a +2 bonus to your Constitution score (to a maximum of 20), advantage on Charisma (Persuasion) checks made to interact with dwarves, darkvision out to 60 feet, the ability to speak, read, and write Dwarvish, and resistance to poison damage. Each day at dawn, there is a 50 percent chance you grow a full beard or your existing beard becomes visibly thicker.
