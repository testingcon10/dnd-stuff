# Belt of Hill Giant Strength

While wearing this belt, your Strength score changes to 21. The belt has no effect if your Strength is already 21 or higher. Other belt variants exist for stronger giant types (Stone: 23, Frost: 23, Fire: 25, Cloud: 27, Storm: 29).
