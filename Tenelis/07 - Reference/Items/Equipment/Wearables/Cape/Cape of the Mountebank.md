# Cape of the Mountebank

This cape smells faintly of brimstone. While wearing it, you can use it to cast the [[Dimension Door]] spell as an action. This property of the cape can't be used again until the next dawn. When you disappear, you leave behind a cloud of smoke, and you appear in a similar cloud of smoke at your destination, briefly obscured.
