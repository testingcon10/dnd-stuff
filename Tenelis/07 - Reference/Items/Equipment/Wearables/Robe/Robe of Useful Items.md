# Robe of Useful Items

This robe has cloth patches of various shapes and colors covering it. While wearing the robe, you can use an action to detach one of the patches, causing it to become the object or creature it represents. The robe comes with two each of dagger, bullseye lantern, steel mirror, 10-foot pole, hempen rope, and sack patches, along with 4d4 additional random patches determined by rolling on the robe's table.
