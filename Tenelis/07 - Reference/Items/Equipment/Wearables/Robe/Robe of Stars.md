# Robe of Stars

This black or dark blue robe is embroidered with small white or silver stars and grants you a +1 bonus to saving throws while you wear it. The robe has 6 stars that can be removed and thrown as [[Magic Missile|magic missile]] darts (5th-level), and you can use an action to enter the Astral Plane along with everything you are wearing and carrying. The robe regains 1d6 removed stars daily at dawn.
