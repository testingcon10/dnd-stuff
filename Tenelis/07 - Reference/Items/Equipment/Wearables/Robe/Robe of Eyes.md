# Robe of Eyes

This robe is adorned with eyelike patterns. While you wear the robe, you gain darkvision out to 120 feet, can see invisible creatures and objects, and can see into the Ethereal Plane out to 120 feet. The eyes on the robe can't be closed or averted, making you vulnerable to the blinded condition from effects like the light or daylight spell if you fail a Constitution saving throw.
