# Robe of Scintillating Colors

This robe has 3 charges and regains 1d3 expended charges daily at dawn. While you wear it, you can use an action and expend 1 charge to cause the garment to display a shifting pattern of dazzling hues. During this time, the robe sheds bright light in a 30-foot radius and dim light for an additional 30 feet. Each creature within 30 feet that can see you must succeed on a DC 15 Wisdom saving throw or become stunned until the effect ends.
