# Robe of the Archmagi

This elegant garment is made from exquisite cloth and set with numerous tiny stars. While wearing this robe, your base AC is 15 + your Dexterity modifier if you aren't wearing armor. You also gain advantage on saving throws against spells and other magical effects, and your spell save DC and spell attack bonus each increase by 2.
