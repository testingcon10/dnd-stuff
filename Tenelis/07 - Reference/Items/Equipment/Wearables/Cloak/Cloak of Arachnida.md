# Cloak of Arachnida

This fine garment is made of black silk interwoven with faint silvery threads. While wearing it, you gain resistance to poison damage, have a climbing speed equal to your walking speed, can move across and climb web surfaces freely, can't be caught in webs of any sort, and can cast the web spell (save DC 13) once per long rest.
