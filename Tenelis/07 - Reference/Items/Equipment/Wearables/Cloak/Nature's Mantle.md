# Nature's Mantle

This cloak shifts color and texture to blend with the terrain surrounding you. While wearing the cloak, you can use it as a spellcasting focus for your druid or ranger spells. While you are in an area that is lightly obscured, you can Hide as a bonus action even if you are being directly observed.
