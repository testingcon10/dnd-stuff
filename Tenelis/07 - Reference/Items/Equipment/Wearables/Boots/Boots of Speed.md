# Boots of Speed

While you wear these boots, you can use a bonus action to click the boots' heels together. The boots double your walking speed for up to 10 minutes, and any creature that makes an opportunity attack against you has disadvantage on the attack roll. The effect ends early if you click your heels together again. Once used, this property can't be used again until the next dawn.
