# Hat of Wizardry

This antiquated, cone-shaped hat is adorned with gold crescent moons and stars. While you are wearing it, you gain the following benefits: you can use the hat as a spellcasting focus for your wizard spells, and you can try to cast a cantrip that you don't know by succeeding on a DC 10 Intelligence (Arcana) check; on a failed check the spell fails with no effect. The hat has 3 charges, and you expend 1 charge each time you attempt to cast a cantrip this way. The hat regains all expended charges daily at dawn.
