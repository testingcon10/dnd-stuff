# Eyes of Charming

These crystal lenses fit over the eyes. They have 3 charges. While wearing them, you can expend 1 charge as an action to cast the [[Charm Person|charm person]] spell (save DC 13) on a humanoid within 30 feet of you. The lenses regain all expended charges daily at dawn.
