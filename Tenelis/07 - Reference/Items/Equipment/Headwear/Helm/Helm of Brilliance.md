# Helm of Brilliance

This dazzling helm is set with 1d10 diamonds, 2d10 rubies, 3d10 fire opals, and 5d10 opals. Gems can be used to cast various spells: daylight (opal), fireball (fire opal), [[Prismatic Spray|prismatic spray]] (diamond), or [[Wall of Fire|wall of fire]] (ruby). You gain resistance to fire damage, and you can use an action to cast the light cantrip from a remaining diamond. When all gems are used or destroyed, the helm loses its magic.
