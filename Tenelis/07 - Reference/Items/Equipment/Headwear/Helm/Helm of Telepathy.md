# Helm of Telepathy

While wearing this helm, you can use an action to cast the [[Detect Thoughts|detect thoughts]] spell (save DC 13) from it. As long as you maintain concentration on the spell, you can use a bonus action to send a telepathic message to a creature you are focused on, and it can reply telepathically. While focusing on a creature with [[Detect Thoughts|detect thoughts]], you can use an action to cast the suggestion spell (save DC 13) on that creature, after which the [[Detect Thoughts|detect thoughts]] spell ends.
