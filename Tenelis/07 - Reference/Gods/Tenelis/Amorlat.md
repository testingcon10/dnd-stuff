# Amorlat

*Goddess of Mana - Chaotic Good*

| Property | Value |
|----------|-------|
| **Pantheon** | Tenelis |
| **Alignment** | Chaotic Good |
| **Domains** | [[Arcana]], Life |
| **Symbol** | Spiraling thread of arcane energy |

Amorlat is the Tenelis goddess of mana, raw magical energy, and the spark that fuels all spellcasting. Her followers believe that magic is a living force that flows through all things, and that those who wield it share a sacred responsibility to the world.
