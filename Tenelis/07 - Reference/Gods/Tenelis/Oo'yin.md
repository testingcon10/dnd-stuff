# Oo'yin

*Goddess of Creation and Stars - Lawful Good*

| Property | Value |
|----------|-------|
| **Pantheon** | Tenelis |
| **Alignment** | Lawful Good |
| **Domains** | Light, Knowledge |
| **Symbol** | Radiant star cradled in open hands |

Oo'yin is the Tenelis goddess of creation, stars, and cosmic order. Her followers believe she wove the stars into the sky and that each constellation tells a story of the world's making. She is revered by astronomers, artisans, and those who seek to create something lasting.
