# Gro'vaal

*God of Decay — Neutral*

| Property | Value |
|----------|-------|
| **Pantheon** | Tenelis |
| **Alignment** | Neutral |
| **Domains** | [[Nature]], Death |
| **Symbol** | Crumbling skull entwined with vines |

Gro'vaal is the Tenelis god of decay, decomposition, and the cycle of renewal through destruction. His followers see beauty in entropy and believe that all things must break down so that new life may emerge from the remains.
