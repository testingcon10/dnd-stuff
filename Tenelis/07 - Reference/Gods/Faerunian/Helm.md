# Helm

*God of Protection — Lawful Neutral*

| Property | Value |
|----------|-------|
| **Pantheon** | Faerunian |
| **Alignment** | Lawful Neutral |
| **Domains** | Life, Light, Twilight |
| **Symbol** | Staring eye on an upright left gauntlet |

Helm the Vigilant One is the god of guardians, protectors, and sentinels, embodying unwavering duty and watchfulness. He is the eternal guardian, never abandoning his post regardless of the cost, a trait that earned both admiration and resentment when he slew a goddess during the Time of Troubles. His followers include paladins, guards, and anyone who takes an oath to protect others, and he is allied with [[Tyr]] in upholding law and order.
