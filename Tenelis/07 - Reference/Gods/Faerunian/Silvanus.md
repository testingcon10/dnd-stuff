# Silvanus

*God of Wild [[Nature]] — Neutral*

| Property | Value |
|----------|-------|
| **Pantheon** | Faerunian |
| **Alignment** | Neutral |
| **Domains** | [[Nature]] |
| **Symbol** | Oak leaf |

Silvanus the Oak Father is the god of wild nature, druids, and the balance of the natural world. He watches over forests, fields, and all growing things, demanding that his followers maintain the equilibrium between civilization and wilderness. He leads a circle of nature deities including [[Mielikki]], [[Eldath]], and [[Chauntea]], standing opposed to those who would exploit or destroy the natural order.
