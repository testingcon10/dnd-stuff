# Malar

*God of the Hunt - Chaotic Evil*

| Property | Value |
|----------|-------|
| **Pantheon** | Faerunian |
| **Alignment** | Chaotic Evil |
| **Domains** | [[Nature]] |
| **Symbol** | Clawed paw |

Malar the Beastlord is the god of the hunt, lycanthropes, and predatory beasts, reveling in the savage thrill of the chase. His followers conduct ritual hunts called the High Hunt, releasing prey into the wilderness before tracking them down. He is a fierce enemy of [[Mielikki]], [[Silvanus]], and [[Eldath]], whose reverence for nature's balance he views as weakness.
