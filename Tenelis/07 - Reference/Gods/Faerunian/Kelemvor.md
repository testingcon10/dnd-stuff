# Kelemvor

*God of the Dead - Lawful Neutral*

| Property | Value |
|----------|-------|
| **Pantheon** | Faerunian |
| **Alignment** | Lawful Neutral |
| **Domains** | Death, Grave |
| **Symbol** | Upright skeletal arm holding balanced scales |

Kelemvor the Lord of the Dead guides souls to their final judgment with fairness and compassion, despising undeath as an abomination against the natural order. Once a mortal mercenary, he ascended to replace [[Cyric]] as lord of the dead and now ensures that death is neither feared nor cheated. His clergy comfort the dying, hunt undead, and oppose the followers of [[Myrkul]] and other death gods who would corrupt the cycle of souls.
