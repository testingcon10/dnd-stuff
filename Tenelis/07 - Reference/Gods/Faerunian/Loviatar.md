# Loviatar

*Goddess of Pain - Lawful Evil*

| Property | Value |
|----------|-------|
| **Pantheon** | Faerunian |
| **Alignment** | Lawful Evil |
| **Domains** | Death |
| **Symbol** | Nine-tailed barbed scourge |

Loviatar the Maiden of Pain is the goddess of agony, torment, and suffering, teaching that pain is the purest form of experience. Her worshippers, called Loviatans, practice ritual flagellation and believe that enduring pain brings them closer to divine truth. She is allied with [[Bane]] and opposed to [[Lliira]] and [[Eldath]], whose messages of joy and peace she considers weakness.
