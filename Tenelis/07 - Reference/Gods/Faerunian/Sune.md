# Sune

*Goddess of Love and Beauty — Chaotic Good*

| Property | Value |
|----------|-------|
| **Pantheon** | Faerunian |
| **Alignment** | Chaotic Good |
| **Domains** | Life, Light |
| **Symbol** | Face of a beautiful red-haired woman |

Sune Firehair is the goddess of beauty, love, and passion, inspiring romance and the appreciation of art and aesthetics across Faerun. Her followers, the Sunites, believe that love is the most powerful force in the multiverse and that beauty should be cultivated in all its forms. She is allied with [[Lliira]], [[Milil]], and [[Lathander]], and her eternal enemy is [[Talos]], whose storms destroy the beauty she cherishes.
