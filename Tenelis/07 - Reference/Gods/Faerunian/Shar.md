# Shar

*Goddess of [[Darkness]] and Loss — Neutral Evil*

| Property | Value |
|----------|-------|
| **Pantheon** | Faerunian |
| **Alignment** | Neutral Evil |
| **Domains** | Death, Trickery |
| **Symbol** | Black disk encircled with a border |

Shar the Mistress of the Night is the goddess of darkness, loss, secrets, and the void, twin sister and eternal enemy of [[Selûne]]. She seeks to extinguish all light and return the world to the primordial darkness from which it was born. Her followers include the secretive Sharrans who infiltrate other faiths, and she created the Shadow Weave as a dark counterpart to the magical Weave.
