# Deneir

*God of Writing — Neutral Good*

| Property | Value |
|----------|-------|
| **Pantheon** | Faerunian |
| **Alignment** | Neutral Good |
| **Domains** | Knowledge, [[Arcana]] |
| **Symbol** | Lit candle above an open eye |

Deneir is the god of glyphs, images, literature, and cartography, serving as the divine patron of scribes and scholars. He believes that knowledge must be preserved in written form to endure, and his clergy maintain vast libraries across Faerun. He works closely with [[Oghma]], the god of knowledge, and together they champion the recording and dissemination of learning.
