# Myrkul

*God of Death - Neutral Evil*

| Property | Value |
|----------|-------|
| **Pantheon** | Faerunian |
| **Alignment** | Neutral Evil |
| **Domains** | Death |
| **Symbol** | White human skull |

Myrkul the Lord of Bones is the former god of the dead, embodying the terror and inevitability of death. Unlike [[Kelemvor]], who judges the dead with fairness, Myrkul revels in the fear that mortality inspires and encourages the creation of undead. He is one of the Dead Three alongside [[Bane]] and [[Bhaal]], and though he has been slain and reborn multiple times, his influence over necromancy endures.
