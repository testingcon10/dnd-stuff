# Lliira

*Goddess of Joy — Chaotic Good*

| Property | Value |
|----------|-------|
| **Pantheon** | Faerunian |
| **Alignment** | Chaotic Good |
| **Domains** | Life |
| **Symbol** | Triangle of three six-pointed stars |

Lliira Our Lady of Joy is the goddess of happiness, dance, and festivals, spreading merriment wherever her influence touches. Her clergy, known as joydancers, organize celebrations and work to lift the spirits of the downtrodden. She opposes the cruelty of [[Loviatar]] and the tyranny of [[Bane]], believing that freedom and joy are the birthright of all creatures.
