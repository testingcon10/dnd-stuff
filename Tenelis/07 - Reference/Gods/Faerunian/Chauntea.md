# Chauntea

*Goddess of Agriculture — Neutral Good*

| Property | Value |
|----------|-------|
| **Pantheon** | Faerunian |
| **Alignment** | Neutral Good |
| **Domains** | Life |
| **Symbol** | Sheaf of grain |

Chauntea the Great Mother is the goddess of agriculture, cultivated land, and the bounty of the harvest. She is one of the oldest deities of Faerun, beloved by farmers and rural folk who depend on the earth's gifts. Her faithful oppose the destructive forces of [[Talos]] and [[Auril]], working to nurture the land and ensure bountiful crops for all.
