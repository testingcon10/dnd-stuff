# Bane

*God of Tyranny - Lawful Evil*

| Property | Value |
|----------|-------|
| **Pantheon** | Faerunian |
| **Alignment** | Lawful Evil |
| **Domains** | War, Order |
| **Symbol** | Upright black right hand |

Bane the Black Lord is the god of tyranny, fear, and hatred, commanding absolute obedience from his followers. He seeks to dominate all of Faerun through his network of ruthless clergy and military orders. Together with [[Bhaal]] and [[Myrkul]], he once formed the Dead Three, mortal adventurers who rose to godhood through ambition and dark power.
