# Cyric

*God of Lies - Chaotic Evil*

| Property | Value |
|----------|-------|
| **Pantheon** | Faerunian |
| **Alignment** | Chaotic Evil |
| **Domains** | Trickery |
| **Symbol** | White jawless skull on black sunburst |

Cyric the Prince of Lies is a deity of deception, illusion, and strife who thrives on sowing conflict and mistrust. Once a mortal companion of the heroes who recovered the Tablets of Fate, he seized divine power through betrayal and murder. He is a sworn enemy of [[Kelemvor]], [[Mystra]], and many other gods, and his madness makes him unpredictable even to his own followers.
