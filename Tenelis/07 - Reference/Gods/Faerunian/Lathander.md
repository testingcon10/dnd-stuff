# Lathander

*God of Birth and Renewal - Neutral Good*

| Property | Value |
|----------|-------|
| **Pantheon** | Faerunian |
| **Alignment** | Neutral Good |
| **Domains** | Life, Light |
| **Symbol** | Road traveling into a sunrise |

Lathander the Morninglord is the god of dawn, spring, birth, and renewal, inspiring hope and new beginnings across Faerun. His followers rise with the sun and dedicate themselves to creativity, optimism, and the destruction of undead. He is a popular deity among commoners and adventurers alike, and his radiant temples serve as beacons of light against the darkness of [[Shar]] and the cold of [[Auril]].
