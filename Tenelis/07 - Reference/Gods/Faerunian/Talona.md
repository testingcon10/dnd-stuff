# Talona

*Goddess of Disease and Poison - Chaotic Evil*

| Property | Value |
|----------|-------|
| **Pantheon** | Faerunian |
| **Alignment** | Chaotic Evil |
| **Domains** | Death |
| **Symbol** | Three teardrops on a triangle |

Talona the Lady of Poison is the goddess of disease, plague, and poison, delighting in the spread of pestilence and suffering. Her followers include poisoners, plague doctors, and those who believe that suffering purifies the weak. She is a fickle and spiteful deity, often clashing with [[Chauntea]] and [[Eldath]] whose nurturing domains oppose her destructive nature.
