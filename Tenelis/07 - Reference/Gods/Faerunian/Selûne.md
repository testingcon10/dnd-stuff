# Selûne

*Goddess of the Moon — Chaotic Good*

| Property | Value |
|----------|-------|
| **Pantheon** | Faerunian |
| **Alignment** | Chaotic Good |
| **Domains** | Knowledge, Life, Twilight |
| **Symbol** | Pair of eyes surrounded by seven stars |

One of the oldest deities in the Faerunian pantheon, Selûne the Moonmaiden embodies the moon, stars, navigation, and wanderers. She is the twin sister of [[Shar]], and their eternal conflict defines the cosmic struggle between light and darkness. She created the sun and the goddess of magic to bring light and warmth to the world.
