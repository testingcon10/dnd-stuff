# Auril

*Goddess of Winter - Neutral Evil*

| Property | Value |
|----------|-------|
| **Pantheon** | Faerunian |
| **Alignment** | Neutral Evil |
| **Domains** | [[Nature]], Tempest |
| **Symbol** | Six-pointed snowflake |

Auril the Frostmaiden is the divine embodiment of winter's fury, reveling in the cold and bringing harsh blizzards upon the land. She is feared by travelers and farmers alike, and her worshippers seek to appease her wrath rather than earn her favor. She has few allies among the gods, often clashing with [[Chauntea]] and [[Lathander]], whose warmth and growth oppose her icy domain.
