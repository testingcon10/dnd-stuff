# Talos

*God of Storms — Chaotic Evil*

| Property | Value |
|----------|-------|
| **Pantheon** | Faerunian |
| **Alignment** | Chaotic Evil |
| **Domains** | Tempest |
| **Symbol** | Three lightning bolts radiating from a point |

Talos the Stormlord is the god of storms, destruction, and natural catastrophes, reveling in the raw power of nature's fury. His followers include sailors who pray for safe passage, and fanatics who summon storms to lay waste to communities. He leads a group of destructive deities including [[Auril]], [[Malar]], and [[Talona]], and is a bitter rival of [[Chauntea]], [[Lathander]], and [[Sune]].
