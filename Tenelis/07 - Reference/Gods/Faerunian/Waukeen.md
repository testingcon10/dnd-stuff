# Waukeen

*Goddess of Trade — Neutral*

| Property | Value |
|----------|-------|
| **Pantheon** | Faerunian |
| **Alignment** | Neutral |
| **Domains** | Knowledge, Trickery |
| **Symbol** | Upright coin with Waukeen's profile |

Waukeen the Merchant's Friend is the goddess of trade, money, and wealth, blessing merchants, traders, and anyone who deals in commerce. She encourages fair dealing but does not condemn cunning negotiation, valuing the flow of coin and goods as a force that strengthens civilization. Her temples serve as banks and marketplaces, and her faithful oppose those who would disrupt trade through piracy, war, or excessive taxation.
