# Eldath

*Goddess of Peace - Neutral Good*

| Property | Value |
|----------|-------|
| **Pantheon** | Faerunian |
| **Alignment** | Neutral Good |
| **Domains** | Life, [[Nature]], Peace |
| **Symbol** | Waterfall plunging into a still pool |

Eldath the Quiet One is the goddess of peace, pools, springs, and druid groves, offering sanctuary from the violence of the world. She is a pacifist deity whose followers refuse to engage in combat, instead seeking to calm conflicts and protect natural springs. She is closely allied with [[Silvanus]] and [[Mielikki]], sharing their love of the natural world.
