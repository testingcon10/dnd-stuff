# Milil

*God of Poetry and Song — Neutral Good*

| Property | Value |
|----------|-------|
| **Pantheon** | Faerunian |
| **Alignment** | Neutral Good |
| **Domains** | Light |
| **Symbol** | Five-stringed harp made of leaves |

Milil the Lord of Song is the god of poetry, eloquence, and song, inspiring bards and musicians across Faerun. He believes that the beauty of music and verse can move hearts, topple tyrants, and preserve history in ways no written word can match. He is closely allied with [[Oghma]] and [[Deneir]], sharing their devotion to the preservation and celebration of knowledge and art.
