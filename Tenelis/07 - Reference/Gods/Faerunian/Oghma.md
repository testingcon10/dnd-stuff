# Oghma

*God of Knowledge - Neutral*

| Property | Value |
|----------|-------|
| **Pantheon** | Faerunian |
| **Alignment** | Neutral |
| **Domains** | Knowledge |
| **Symbol** | Blank scroll |

Oghma the Lord of Knowledge is the god of bards, inspiration, invention, and knowledge, standing as the ultimate patron of learning in Faerun. He believes that all knowledge is valuable and should be gathered, recorded, and shared freely. His clergy maintain libraries and centers of learning, and he works alongside [[Deneir]], [[Milil]], and [[Gond]] to advance the cause of intellectual pursuits.
