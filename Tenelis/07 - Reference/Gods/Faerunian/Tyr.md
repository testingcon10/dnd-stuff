# Tyr

*God of Justice - Lawful Good*

| Property | Value |
|----------|-------|
| **Pantheon** | Faerunian |
| **Alignment** | Lawful Good |
| **Domains** | War, Order |
| **Symbol** | Balanced scales resting on a warhammer |

Tyr the Maimed God is the god of justice, law, and righteous warfare, and the patron of paladins who swear oaths of devotion. He lost his right hand to demonstrate the principle that justice requires sacrifice, and his followers uphold the law with unwavering conviction. He is allied with [[Helm]] and [[Kelemvor]], and stands as the chief opponent of [[Bane]] and all who would impose tyranny upon the innocent.
