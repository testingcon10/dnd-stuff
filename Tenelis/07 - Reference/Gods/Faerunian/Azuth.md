# Azuth

*God of Wizards — Lawful Neutral*

| Property | Value |
|----------|-------|
| **Pantheon** | Faerunian |
| **Alignment** | Lawful Neutral |
| **Domains** | Knowledge, [[Arcana]] |
| **Symbol** | Left hand pointing upward outlined in fire |

Azuth is the patron deity of wizards and spellcasters who pursue the disciplined study of arcane magic. He serves as the god of the structured and measured approach to the Art, favoring those who seek mastery through study rather than wild talent. He was once a mortal wizard who ascended to godhood, and he maintains a complex relationship with [[Mystra]], the goddess of magic.
