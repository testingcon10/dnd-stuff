# Gond

*God of Craft — Neutral*

| Property | Value |
|----------|-------|
| **Pantheon** | Faerunian |
| **Alignment** | Neutral |
| **Domains** | Knowledge, Forge |
| **Symbol** | Toothed cog with four spokes |

Gond Wonderbringer is the god of craft, smithing, and invention, inspiring artificers and tinkerers across Faerun. He encourages innovation and the creation of new devices, valuing the act of making above all else. His followers among the [[Gnome]] and [[Dwarf]] races are especially devoted, and his temples often double as workshops and forges.
