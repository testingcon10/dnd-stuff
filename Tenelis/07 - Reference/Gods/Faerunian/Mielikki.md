# Mielikki

*Goddess of Forests — Neutral Good*

| Property | Value |
|----------|-------|
| **Pantheon** | Faerunian |
| **Alignment** | Neutral Good |
| **Domains** | [[Nature]] |
| **Symbol** | Unicorn's head |

Mielikki the Forest Queen is the goddess of autumn, druids, dryads, forests, and forest creatures. She serves as the patron of rangers and those who protect the woodlands, teaching that the forest must be tended and defended. She is closely allied with [[Silvanus]] and [[Eldath]], and she opposes the destructive hunts of [[Malar]] and the deforestation wrought by civilization.
