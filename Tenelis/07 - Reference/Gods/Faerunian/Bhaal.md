# Bhaal

*God of Murder — Neutral Evil*

| Property | Value |
|----------|-------|
| **Pantheon** | Faerunian |
| **Alignment** | Neutral Evil |
| **Domains** | Death |
| **Symbol** | Skull surrounded by a ring of blood droplets |

Bhaal is the Lord of Murder, reveling in violence and slaughter. His followers carry out assassinations and ritual killings in his name, believing every murder committed strengthens his divine power. He is one of the Dead Three alongside [[Bane]] and [[Myrkul]], and his mortal offspring — the Bhaalspawn — have shaped the history of Faerun.
