# Mask

*God of Thieves - Chaotic Neutral*

| Property | Value |
|----------|-------|
| **Pantheon** | Faerunian |
| **Alignment** | Chaotic Neutral |
| **Domains** | Trickery |
| **Symbol** | Black mask |

Mask the Lord of Shadows is the god of thieves, shadows, and intrigue, patron of rogues and those who operate in the dark. He values cunning and subtlety above brute force, and his followers include thieves' guilds and spies throughout Faerun. He shares a rivalry with [[Cyric]] for influence over deception and has a complex history with [[Shar]], whose shadow domain overlaps with his own.
