# Savras

*God of [[Divination]] and Fate — Lawful Neutral*

| Property | Value |
|----------|-------|
| **Pantheon** | Faerunian |
| **Alignment** | Lawful Neutral |
| **Domains** | Knowledge, [[Arcana]] |
| **Symbol** | [[Crystal Ball|Crystal ball]] containing many kinds of eyes |

Savras the All-Seeing is the god of divination, fate, and truth, granting visions of what is, what was, and what will be. His followers include seers, prophets, and judges who seek to uncover hidden truths and expose lies. He was once imprisoned by [[Azuth]] inside a magical staff, and since his release he has maintained a wary alliance with the god of wizards while opposing the deceptions of [[Cyric]] and [[Mask]].
