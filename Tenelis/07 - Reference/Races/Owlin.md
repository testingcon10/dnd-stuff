# Owlin

Owlin are owl-like humanoids with large, expressive eyes and broad wings capable of silent flight. Originating from the Feywild, they carry an air of quiet wisdom and nocturnal grace. Their exceptional darkvision and natural stealth make them superb scouts and nighttime operatives.

- **Ability Score Increase:** Choose any +2/+1 or +1/+1/+1
- **Size:** Small or Medium (your choice)
- **Speed:** 30 ft.
- **Darkvision:** 120 ft.
- **Flight:** You have a flying speed equal to your walking speed; you cannot fly while wearing medium or heavy armor
- **Silent Feathers:** Proficiency in [[Stealth]]
- **Languages:** Common and one other language of your choice
