# Warforged

Warforged are living constructs originally built as soldiers for a great war, now seeking purpose in a world at peace. They are made of wood, metal, and stone, animated by a blend of alchemy and magic that grants them true sentience. Though they lack the biological needs of other races, they possess genuine emotions and an enduring drive to find meaning beyond the battlefield.

## Traits
- **Ability Score Increase:** Constitution +2, plus one other ability score of your choice +1
- **Size:** Medium
- **Speed:** 30 ft.
- **Constructed Resilience:** Advantage on saving throws against being poisoned; resistance to poison damage; immune to disease; you don't need to eat, drink, or breathe
- **Sentry's Rest:** You don't need to sleep; instead, you enter an inactive but conscious state for 6 hours to gain the benefits of a long rest
- **Integrated Protection:** Your AC includes a +1 bonus; you can don only armor you are proficient with, integrating it into your body over 1 hour (it can't be removed against your will)
- **Specialized Design:** Proficiency in one skill and one tool of your choice
- **Languages:** Common and one other language of your choice
