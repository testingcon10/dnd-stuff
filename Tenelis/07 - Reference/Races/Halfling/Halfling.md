# Halfling

The comforts of home are the goals of most halflings' lives. They are nimble and cheerful, with the [[Lucky]] trait that lets them reroll natural 1s on attack rolls, ability checks, and saving throws.

## Traits
- **Ability Score Increase:** Dexterity +2
- **Size:** Small
- **Speed:** 25 ft.
- **Lucky:** Reroll natural 1s on d20 rolls
- **Brave:** Advantage on saves vs. being frightened
- **Halfling Nimbleness:** Move through the space of creatures one size larger

## Subraces
- **Lightfoot** - +1 Charisma, Naturally Stealthy (hide behind Medium+ creatures)
- **Stout** - +1 Constitution, resistance to poison, advantage vs. poison saves
