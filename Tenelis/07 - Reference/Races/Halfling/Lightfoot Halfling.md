# Lightfoot Halfling

Lightfoot halflings are more prone to wanderlust than other halflings and often dwell alongside other races or take up a nomadic life. They are adept at blending into the company of others and making themselves scarce when needed.

## Subrace Traits
- **Ability Score Increase:** Charisma +1 (in addition to Dexterity +2 from [[Halfling]])
- **Naturally Stealthy:** You can attempt to hide even when you are obscured only by a creature that is at least one size larger than you (Medium or larger)

**Parent Race:** [[Halfling]]
