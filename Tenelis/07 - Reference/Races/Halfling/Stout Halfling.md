# Stout Halfling

Stout halflings are hardier than average and have some resistance to poison. Some say that stouts have dwarven blood in them, and they are sometimes called stronghearts in the Forgotten Realms or kender in Dragonlance.

## Subrace Traits
- **Ability Score Increase:** Constitution +1 (in addition to Dexterity +2 from [[Halfling]])
- **Stout Resilience:** You have advantage on saving throws against poison, and you have resistance against poison damage

**Parent Race:** [[Halfling]]
