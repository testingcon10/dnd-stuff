# Goblin

Goblins are small, cunning creatures who thrive in the margins of civilization. They are quick on their feet and vicious when cornered, capable of slipping away from danger as quickly as they strike. Their resourcefulness makes them surprisingly effective adventurers.

## Traits
- **Ability Score Increase:** Dexterity +2, Constitution +1
- **Size:** Small
- **Speed:** 30 ft.
- **Darkvision:** 60 ft.
- **Fury of the Small:** When you damage a creature that is a larger size than you, deal extra damage equal to your level (once per short or long rest)
- **Nimble Escape:** Disengage or Hide as a bonus action on each of your turns
