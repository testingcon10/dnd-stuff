# Aarakocra

Aarakocra are avian humanoids who hail from the Elemental Plane of Air. They soar through the skies on powerful wings, serving as scouts and sentinels. Their love of open skies makes them uncomfortable in enclosed spaces.

## Traits
- **Ability Score Increase:** Dexterity +2, Wisdom +1
- **Size:** Medium
- **Speed:** 25 ft. walking
- **Flight:** 50 ft. flying speed (cannot fly while wearing medium or heavy armor)
- **Talons:** Unarmed strikes deal 1d4 slashing damage
- **Languages:** Common, Aarakocra, Auran
