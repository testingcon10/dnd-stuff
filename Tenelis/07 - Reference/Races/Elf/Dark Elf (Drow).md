# Dark Elf (Drow)

Descended from an earlier subrace of dark-skinned elves, the drow were banished from the surface world for following the goddess Lolth down the path of evil and corruption. They have built their civilization in the depths of the Underdark.

## Subrace Traits
- **Ability Score Increase:** Charisma +1 (in addition to Dexterity +2 from [[Elf]])
- **Superior Darkvision:** Your darkvision has a radius of 120 feet
- **Sunlight Sensitivity:** You have disadvantage on attack rolls and on Wisdom ([[Perception]]) checks that rely on sight when you, the target of your attack, or whatever you are trying to perceive is in direct sunlight
- **Drow Magic:** You know the *[[Dancing Lights|dancing lights]]* cantrip. When you reach [[3rd Level|3rd level]], you can cast *[[Faerie Fire|faerie fire]]* once per day. When you reach [[5th Level|5th level]], you can cast *darkness* once per day. Charisma is your spellcasting ability for these spells
- **Drow Weapon Training:** You have proficiency with rapiers, shortswords, and hand crossbows

**Parent Race:** [[Elf]]
