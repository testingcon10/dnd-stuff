# Wood Elf

Wood elves have keen senses and intuition, and their fleet feet carry them quickly and stealthily through their native forests. They are reclusive and distrustful of non-elves.

## Subrace Traits
- **Ability Score Increase:** Wisdom +1 (in addition to Dexterity +2 from [[Elf]])
- **Elf Weapon Training:** You have proficiency with the longsword, shortsword, longbow, and shortbow
- **Fleet of Foot:** Your base walking speed increases to 35 feet
- **Mask of the Wild:** You can attempt to hide even when you are only lightly obscured by foliage, heavy rain, falling snow, mist, and other natural phenomena

**Parent Race:** [[Elf]]
