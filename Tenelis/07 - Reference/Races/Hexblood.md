# Hexblood

Hexbloods are individuals transformed by hag magic, whether through a curse, a bargain, or being raised by a hag. They straddle the line between fey and mortal, bearing an eldritch token that serves as a magical focus and a connection to their hag origin.

## Traits
- **Ability Score Increase:** +2 to one ability score and +1 to another, or +1 to three different scores
- **Size:** Medium or Small (your choice)
- **Speed:** 30 ft.
- **Ancestral Legacy:** Retain skill proficiencies and climbing/swimming speed from original race, or gain two skill proficiencies of your choice
- **Darkvision:** 60 ft.
- **Eerie Token:** Create a small supernatural token (such as a lock of hair or a tooth) that allows you to telepathically communicate with or use as a remote sensor for the creature holding it (within 10 miles); lasts until you finish a long rest
- **Hex Magic:** Cast [[Disguise Self]] and [[Hex]] once each per long rest without expending a spell slot (can also cast with spell slots; Intelligence, Wisdom, or Charisma-based)
