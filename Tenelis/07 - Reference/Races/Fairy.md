# Fairy

Fairies are small fey beings brimming with whimsical magic. They flutter on gossamer wings and carry the enchantment of the Feywild wherever they go. Despite their diminutive stature, they are spirited adventurers with innate magical talent.

## Traits
- **Ability Score Increase:** +2 to one ability score and +1 to another, or +1 to three different scores
- **Size:** Small
- **Speed:** 30 ft.
- **Creature Type:** Fey
- **Fairy Magic:** You know [[Druidcraft]]; at 3rd level, cast [[Faerie Fire]] once per long rest; at 5th level, cast [[Enlarge-Reduce|Enlarge/Reduce]] once per long rest (can also cast with spell slots)
- **Flight:** 30 ft. flying speed (cannot fly while wearing medium or heavy armor)
- **Languages:** Common and one other of your choice
