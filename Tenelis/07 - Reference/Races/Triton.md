# Triton

Tritons are noble aquatic guardians who hail from the Elemental Plane of Water, having migrated to the Material Plane ages ago to protect the surface world from deep-sea threats. They carry themselves with a regal bearing and a sense of duty that can sometimes come across as haughty or naive about surface customs. Their mastery over water and air makes them formidable defenders of both land and sea.

## Traits
- **Ability Score Increase:** Strength +1, Constitution +1, Charisma +1
- **Size:** Medium
- **Speed:** 30 ft., swim 30 ft.
- **Darkvision:** 60 ft.
- **Amphibious:** You can breathe air and water
- **Control Air and Water:** You can cast [[Fog Cloud]] at 1st level; [[Gust of Wind]] at 3rd level; Wall of Water at 5th level (once each per long rest, Charisma-based; also castable with spell slots)
- **Emissary of the Sea:** You can communicate simple ideas to any beast, elemental, or monstrosity that has a swimming speed
- **Guardian of the Depths:** Resistance to cold damage; you are adapted to extreme ocean depths (no ill effects from deep, underwater environments)
- **Languages:** Common, Primordial
