# Kalashtar

Kalashtar are psionic humanoids born from the merging of human souls with quori spirits from the plane of dreams. They are thoughtful, contemplative, and possess an innate connection to the world of dreams that grants them powerful mental defenses. Their dual nature gives them a serene presence and a deep drive to oppose the forces of darkness.

## Traits
- **Ability Score Increase:** Wisdom +2, Charisma +1
- **Size:** Medium
- **Speed:** 30 ft.
- **Dual Mind:** Advantage on Wisdom saving throws
- **Mental Discipline:** Resistance to psychic damage
- **Mind Link:** Telepathically speak to a creature within 10 × your level feet; no shared language needed, but the creature must understand at least one language
- **Severed from Dreams:** You are immune to spells and effects that require you to dream (such as the Dream spell), and cannot be put to sleep by magic
- **Languages:** Common, Quori, and one other language of your choice
