# Hill Dwarf

Hill dwarves have keen senses, deep intuition, and remarkable resilience. They are found across many worlds as miners, smiths, and warriors.

## Subrace Traits
- **Ability Score Increase:** Wisdom +1 (in addition to Constitution +2 from [[Dwarf]])
- **Dwarven Toughness:** Your hit point maximum increases by 1 for each level you have
