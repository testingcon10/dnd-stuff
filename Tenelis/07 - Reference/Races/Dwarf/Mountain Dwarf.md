# Mountain Dwarf

Mountain dwarves are strong and hardy, accustomed to life in rugged terrain. They tend toward the taller end of dwarven height and are trained in the use of light and medium armor.

## Subrace Traits
- **Ability Score Increase:** Strength +2 (in addition to Constitution +2 from [[Dwarf]])
- **Dwarven Armor Training:** You have proficiency with light and medium armor

**Parent Race:** [[Dwarf]]
