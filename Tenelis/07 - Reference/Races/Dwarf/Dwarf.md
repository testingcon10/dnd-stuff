# Dwarf

Bold and hardy, dwarves are known as skilled warriors, miners, and workers of stone and metal. They are resilient and tough, with darkvision, resistance to poison, and proficiency with battleaxes and hammers.

## Traits
- **Ability Score Increase:** Constitution +2
- **Size:** Medium
- **Speed:** 25 ft. (not reduced by heavy armor)
- **Darkvision:** 60 ft.
- **Dwarven Resilience:** Advantage on saves vs. poison; resistance to poison damage
- **Stonecunning:** Double proficiency bonus on [[History]] checks related to stonework

## Subraces
- **Hill Dwarf** - +1 Wisdom, +1 HP per level
- **Mountain Dwarf** - +2 Strength, medium armor proficiency
