# Kenku

Kenku are flightless crow-like humanoids cursed to be unable to produce original speech, instead communicating through uncanny mimicry of sounds and voices they have heard. They are skilled forgers and resourceful survivors, thriving in the shadows of larger civilizations. Their talent for imitation extends beyond speech to an extraordinary ability to copy craftsmanship and artistry.

## Traits
- **Ability Score Increase:** Dexterity +2, Wisdom +1
- **Size:** Medium
- **Speed:** 30 ft.
- **Expert Duplication:** Advantage on checks to produce forgeries or duplicates of existing objects
- **Kenku Training:** Proficiency in two of the following skills: [[Acrobatics]], [[Deception]], [[Stealth]], or [[Sleight of Hand]]
- **Mimicry:** You can mimic sounds and voices you have heard; a creature that hears the mimicry can tell it is an imitation with a successful Wisdom ([[Insight]]) check opposed by your Charisma ([[Deception]]) check
- **Languages:** Common, Auran
