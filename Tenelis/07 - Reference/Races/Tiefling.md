# Tiefling

Tieflings are derived from human bloodlines infused with the touch of the lower planes. They are mistrusted by many but possess innate spellcasting from their infernal heritage, along with fire resistance and darkvision.

## Traits
- **Ability Score Increase:** Charisma +2, Intelligence +1
- **Size:** Medium
- **Speed:** 30 ft.
- **Darkvision:** 60 ft.
- **Hellish Resistance:** Resistance to fire damage
- **Infernal Legacy:** [[Thaumaturgy]] cantrip; [[Hellish Rebuke]] at 3rd level; [[Darkness]] at 5th level (Charisma-based, once per long rest each)
