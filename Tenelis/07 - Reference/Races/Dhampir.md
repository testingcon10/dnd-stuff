# Dhampir

Dhampirs are poised between the living and the undead, bearing a vampiric lineage that grants them supernatural abilities. They hunger for blood or life essence, can scale walls with ease, and need not draw breath.

## Traits
- **Ability Score Increase:** +2 to one ability score and +1 to another, or +1 to three different scores
- **Size:** Medium or Small (your choice)
- **Speed:** 35 ft.
- **Ancestral Legacy:** Retain skill proficiencies and climbing/swimming speed from original race, or gain two skill proficiencies of your choice
- **Darkvision:** 60 ft.
- **Deathless Nature:** No need to breathe
- **Spider Climb:** Climbing speed equal to walking speed; can climb difficult surfaces including ceilings without needing an ability check
- **Vampiric Bite:** Constitution-based bite attack (1d4 piercing); on a hit against a willing or grappled/incapacitated/restrained creature, gain a bonus equal to the damage dealt to your next ability check or attack roll (can use a number of times equal to proficiency bonus per long rest)
