# Eladrin

Eladrin are elves of the Feywild, deeply attuned to the emotional and magical nature of that plane. Their appearance and demeanor shift with the seasons, and they can teleport short distances using Fey Step, with additional effects tied to their chosen season.

## Traits
- **Ability Score Increase:** Dexterity +2, Charisma +1
- **Size:** Medium
- **Speed:** 30 ft.
- **Darkvision:** 60 ft.
- **Fey Ancestry:** Advantage on saves vs. charm; immune to magical sleep
- **Trance:** 4 hours of meditation replaces 8 hours of sleep
- **Fey Step:** As a bonus action, teleport up to 30 ft. to an unoccupied space you can see (once per short or long rest); additional effect based on current season:
  - **Autumn:** Up to two creatures within 10 ft. of destination must save or be charmed for 1 minute
  - **Winter:** One creature within 5 ft. of destination must save or be frightened until end of your next turn
  - **Spring:** [[Teleport]] a willing creature within 5 ft. of you instead
  - **Summer:** Each creature within 5 ft. of destination takes fire damage equal to your Charisma modifier
