# Minotaur

Minotaurs are barrel-chested, bull-headed humanoids who combine raw physical power with a surprising cunning. They are driven by primal instincts and a fierce competitive spirit, charging headlong into battle with devastating force. Whether navigating labyrinthine dungeons or open battlefields, minotaurs are relentless warriors who use their natural weapons to devastating effect.

## Traits
- **Ability Score Increase:** Strength +2, Constitution +1
- **Size:** Medium
- **Speed:** 30 ft.
- **Horns:** Unarmed strikes deal 1d6 piercing damage
- **Goring Rush:** When you use the Dash action and move at least 20 feet toward a target, you can make a bonus action melee attack with your horns against that target
- **Hammering Horns:** When you hit a creature with a melee attack on your turn, you can use a bonus action to push that creature up to 10 feet away from you with your horns
- **Labyrinthine Recall:** You can perfectly recall any path you have traveled
- **Languages:** Common, Minotaur
