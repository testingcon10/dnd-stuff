# Dragonborn

Born of dragons, dragonborn walk proudly through a world that greets them with fearful incomprehension. They possess a breath weapon tied to their draconic ancestry and resistance to the corresponding damage type.

## Traits
- **Ability Score Increase:** Strength +2, Charisma +1
- **Size:** Medium
- **Speed:** 30 ft.
- **Draconic Ancestry:** Choose a dragon type for breath weapon and damage resistance
- **Breath Weapon:** Exhale destructive energy (damage type/shape based on ancestry)
- **Damage Resistance:** Resistance to the damage type of your ancestry

## Draconic Ancestry Options
Black (Acid), Blue (Lightning), Brass (Fire), Bronze (Lightning), Copper (Acid), Gold (Fire), Green (Poison), Red (Fire), Silver (Cold), White (Cold)
