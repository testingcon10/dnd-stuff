# Aasimar

Aasimar bear within their souls the light of the heavens, descended from humans touched by the power of Mount Celestia. They are champions meant to stand against evil, guided by angelic visions and empowered with celestial radiance.

## Traits
- **Ability Score Increase:** Charisma +2
- **Size:** Medium
- **Speed:** 30 ft.
- **Darkvision:** 60 ft.
- **Celestial Resistance:** Resistance to necrotic and radiant damage
- **Healing Hands:** Touch a creature to restore hit points equal to your level (once per long rest)
- **Light Bearer:** You know the Light cantrip (Charisma-based)

## Subraces
- **Protector Aasimar** — +1 Wisdom, Radiant Soul (wings and radiant damage at 3rd level)
- **Scourge Aasimar** — +1 Constitution, Radiant Consumption (radiant aura that damages all nearby, including yourself)
- **Fallen Aasimar** — +1 Strength, Necrotic Shroud (frightening skeletal wings and necrotic damage)
