# Variant Human

The variant human replaces the standard human's +1 to all [[Ability Scores|ability scores]] with a more focused set of benefits, including a feat at [[1st Level|1st level]]. This is an optional rule variant presented in the Player's Handbook.

## Subrace Traits
- **Ability Score Increase:** Two different [[Ability Scores|ability scores]] of your choice each increase by 1
- **Skills:** You gain proficiency in one skill of your choice
- **Feat:** You gain one feat of your choice at [[1st Level|1st level]]

> **Note:** This is an optional rule. Check with your DM before using the Variant [[Human]] in place of the standard [[Human]].

**Parent Race:** [[Human]]
