# Human

Humans are the most adaptable and ambitious people among the common races. They are highly versatile, gaining a +1 bonus to all ability scores — or, with the popular [[Variant Human]] option, a feat at level 1.

## Traits
- **Ability Score Increase:** All ability scores +1
- **Size:** Medium
- **Speed:** 30 ft.
- **Languages:** Common + one extra

## [[Variant Human]] (Optional)
- +1 to two ability scores of choice
- One skill proficiency
- One feat at 1st level
