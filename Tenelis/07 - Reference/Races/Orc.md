# Orc

Orcs are fierce, muscular warriors driven by an unyielding will to survive and conquer. They are forged by hardship and carry an innate vitality that pushes them beyond normal limits in the heat of battle. Whether serving as frontline soldiers or wandering as mercenaries, orcs bring unmatched aggression and tenacity to every conflict.

## Traits
- **Ability Score Increase:** Strength +2, Constitution +1
- **Size:** Medium
- **Speed:** 30 ft.
- **Darkvision:** 60 ft.
- **Adrenaline Rush:** You can use a bonus action to move up to your speed without provoking opportunity attacks, and you gain temporary hit points equal to your proficiency bonus (proficiency bonus uses per long rest)
- **Powerful Build:** You count as one size larger when determining carrying capacity and the weight you can push, drag, or lift
- **Relentless Endurance:** When you are reduced to 0 hit points but not killed outright, you can drop to 1 hit point instead (once per long rest)
- **Languages:** Common, Orc
