# Rock Gnome

Rock gnomes are naturally inventive and hardy, going beyond mere illusion to create wondrous devices and contraptions. They make up the majority of gnomes encountered in the world.

## Subrace Traits
- **Ability Score Increase:** Constitution +1 (in addition to Intelligence +2 from [[Gnome]])
- **Artificer's Lore:** Whenever you make an Intelligence (History) check related to magic items, alchemical objects, or technological devices, you can add twice your proficiency bonus, instead of any proficiency bonus you normally apply
- **Tinker:** You have proficiency with artisan's tools (tinker's tools). Using those tools, you can spend 1 hour and 10 gp worth of materials to construct a Tiny clockwork device (AC 5, 1 hp). The device ceases to function after 24 hours unless you spend 1 hour repairing it. You can have up to three such devices active at a time. Options include a clockwork toy, fire starter, or music box
