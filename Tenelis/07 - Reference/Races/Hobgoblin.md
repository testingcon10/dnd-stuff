# Hobgoblin

Hobgoblins are disciplined, militaristic goblinoids who value order, hierarchy, and martial prowess above all. They are natural tacticians who fight most effectively in organized groups, drawing strength and resolve from the presence of their allies.

## Traits
- **Ability Score Increase:** Constitution +2, Intelligence +1
- **Size:** Medium
- **Speed:** 30 ft.
- **Darkvision:** 60 ft.
- **Martial Training:** Proficiency with light armor, two martial weapons of your choice
- **Saving Face:** When you miss an attack roll, fail an ability check, or fail a saving throw, you can add a bonus equal to the number of allies you can see within 30 ft. (max +5) to the roll, potentially turning it into a success (once per short or long rest)
