# Satyr

Satyrs are merry fey creatures who revel in music, wine, and the pleasures of life. Blessed with an innate resistance to magic from their fey nature, they bound through the world with infectious joy and relentless curiosity. Their carefree demeanor belies a surprising resilience and a deep connection to the wild places of the world.

## Traits
- **Ability Score Increase:** Charisma +2, Dexterity +1
- **Size:** Medium
- **Speed:** 35 ft.
- **Creature Type:** Fey (rather than Humanoid)
- **Ram:** Unarmed strikes deal 1d4 bludgeoning damage
- **Magic Resistance:** Advantage on saving throws against spells and other magical effects
- **Mirthful Leaps:** Whenever you make a long jump or high jump, you can roll a d8 and add the result to the distance in feet
- **Reveler:** Proficiency in Performance and Persuasion
- **Languages:** Common, Sylvan
