# Reborn

Reborn are creatures who have died and returned to a state of living, whether through necromancy, divine intervention, or sheer force of will. They carry fragmented memories of their previous existence and exist in an unsettling twilight between life and death. Their bodies bear the marks of their passing, but their spirits are resilient beyond measure.

## Traits
- **Ability Score Increase:** Choose any +2/+1 or +1/+1/+1
- **Size:** Small or Medium (your choice)
- **Speed:** 30 ft.
- **Ancestral Legacy:** If you replace a race with this lineage, you can keep the following from the original race: any skill proficiencies, climbing/swimming speed, or walking speed above 30 ft.
- **Deathless Nature:** Advantage on death saving throws; advantage on saves against being poisoned; resistance to poison damage; you don't need to eat, drink, breathe, or sleep; you finish a long rest in 4 hours of inactivity while remaining conscious
- **Knowledge from a Past Life:** When you make an ability check that uses a skill, you can roll a d6 and add it to the check (proficiency bonus uses per long rest)
- **Languages:** Common and one other language of your choice
