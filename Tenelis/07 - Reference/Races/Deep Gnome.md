# Deep Gnome

Deep gnomes, also known as svirfneblin, dwell in the Underdark far beneath the surface world. They are cautious, quiet, and possess an uncanny ability to blend into their rocky surroundings. Their lives are shaped by the dangers of the deep.

## Traits
- **Ability Score Increase:** Intelligence +2, Dexterity +1
- **Size:** Small
- **Speed:** 25 ft.
- **Superior Darkvision:** 120 ft.
- **Gnome Cunning:** Advantage on Intelligence, Wisdom, and Charisma saving throws against magic
- **Stone Camouflage:** Advantage on Dexterity (Stealth) checks to hide in rocky terrain
- **Languages:** Common, Gnomish, Undercommon
