# Tabaxi

Tabaxi are cat-like humanoids driven by an insatiable curiosity and wanderlust, hailing from distant tropical lands. They collect stories, artifacts, and lore with obsessive enthusiasm, always seeking the next fascinating discovery. Their feline agility and natural climbing ability make them exceptional explorers and nimble combatants.

## Traits
- **Ability Score Increase:** Dexterity +2, Charisma +1
- **Size:** Medium
- **Speed:** 30 ft., climb 20 ft.
- **Darkvision:** 60 ft.
- **Cat's Claws:** Unarmed strikes deal 1d6 slashing damage; you have a climbing speed of 20 ft.
- **Cat's Talent:** Proficiency in Perception and Stealth
- **Feline Agility:** When you move on your turn, you can double your speed until the end of that turn; once used, you can't do so again until you spend a turn not moving
- **Languages:** Common and one other language of your choice
