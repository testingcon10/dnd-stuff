# Leonin

Leonin are proud, lion-like humanoids who dwell in the golden plains and savannas, living in close-knit prides. They are fiercely independent and distrustful of outsiders, particularly the gods, preferring to rely on their own strength and the bonds of their community. Their imposing presence and thunderous roar strike fear into the hearts of their enemies.

## Traits
- **Ability Score Increase:** Constitution +2, Strength +1
- **Size:** Medium
- **Speed:** 35 ft.
- **Darkvision:** 60 ft.
- **Claws:** Unarmed strikes deal 1d4 slashing damage
- **Hunter's Instincts:** Proficiency in one of: [[Athletics]], [[Intimidation]], [[Perception]], or [[Survival]]
- **Daunting Roar:** As a bonus action, creatures of your choice within 10 feet must succeed on a Wisdom saving throw (DC 8 + proficiency bonus + Constitution modifier) or become frightened of you until the end of your next turn (once per short or long rest)
- **Languages:** Common, Leonin
