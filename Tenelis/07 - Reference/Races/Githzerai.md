# Githzerai

Githzerai are monastic psionics who forged their civilization in the howling chaos of Limbo. Through iron discipline and mental focus, they impose order on chaos itself. They despise mind flayers and distrust their githyanki cousins.

## Traits
- **Ability Score Increase:** Wisdom +2, Intelligence +1
- **Size:** Medium
- **Speed:** 30 ft.
- **Mental Discipline:** Advantage on saving throws against the charmed and frightened conditions
- **Githzerai Psionics:** [[Mage Hand]] cantrip (invisible hand); at 3rd level, cast [[Shield]] once per long rest; at 5th level, cast [[Detect Thoughts]] once per long rest (Wisdom-based)
