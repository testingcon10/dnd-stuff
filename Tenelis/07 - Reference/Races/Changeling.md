# Changeling

Changelings are subtle shapeshifters capable of disguising their appearance and adopting different personas. Their fluid identity makes them natural spies, socialites, and tricksters, though many simply seek a place to belong.

## Traits
- **Ability Score Increase:** Charisma +2, and one other ability score +1
- **Size:** Medium
- **Speed:** 30 ft.
- **Shapechanger:** As an action, change your appearance and voice to look like a humanoid you have seen (reverts on death); your statistics remain the same
- **Changeling Instincts:** Proficiency in two of: [[Deception]], [[Insight]], [[Intimidation]], or [[Persuasion]]
- **Languages:** Common and two others of your choice
