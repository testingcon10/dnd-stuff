# Storm Sorcery

The Storm Sorcery sorcerer channels the untamed power of storms, crackling with lightning and thunder as they unleash the fury of the tempest on their enemies.

**Class:** [[Sorcerer]] | **Source:** XGtE | **Subclass Features at:** 1st, 6th, 14th, 18th level

## Key Features
- **Wind Speaker (1st):** You can speak, read, and write Primordial. Knowing Primordial allows you to understand and be understood by those who speak its dialects: Aquan, Auran, Ignan, and Terran.
- **Tempestuous Magic (1st):** When you cast a spell of [[1st Level|1st level]] or higher, you can use a bonus action to fly up to 10 feet without provoking opportunity attacks, immediately before or after casting.
- **Heart of the Storm (6th):** You gain resistance to lightning and thunder damage. Whenever you cast a spell of [[1st Level|1st level]] or higher that deals lightning or thunder damage, you deal lightning or thunder damage (your choice) equal to half your sorcerer level to creatures of your choice within 10 feet of you.
- **Storm Guide (6th):** You can subtly control the weather around you. If it is raining, you can cause the rain to stop falling in a 20-foot-radius sphere centered on you. You can also cause winds to stop in a 100-foot sphere centered on you, or choose their direction.
- **Storm's Fury (14th):** When you are hit by a melee attack, you can use your reaction to deal lightning damage to the attacker equal to your sorcerer level. The attacker must also make a STR save or be pushed 20 feet away from you.
- **Wind Soul (18th):** You gain immunity to lightning and thunder damage. You gain a magical flying speed of 60 feet. As an action, you can reduce your flying speed to 30 feet for 1 hour and grant up to 3 + CHA modifier creatures within 30 feet a flying speed of 30 feet for 1 hour.
