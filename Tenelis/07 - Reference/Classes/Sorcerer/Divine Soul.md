# Divine Soul

The Divine Soul sorcerer carries a spark of divine magic within, granting access to the full cleric spell list alongside sorcerer magic and the blessings of a higher power.

**Class:** [[Sorcerer]] | **Source:** XGtE | **Subclass Features at:** 1st, 6th, 14th, 18th level

## Key Features
- **Divine Magic (1st):** You can choose spells from the cleric spell list in addition to the sorcerer spell list. You also learn one additional spell based on your divine affinity: [[Cure Wounds|cure wounds]] (Good), [[Inflict Wounds|inflict wounds]] (Evil), bless (Law), bane (Chaos), or [[Protection from Evil and Good|protection from evil and good]] (Neutrality).
- **Favored by the Gods (1st):** When you fail a saving throw or miss with an attack roll, you can add 2d4 to the total, possibly changing the outcome. Once per short or long rest.
- **Empowered Healing (6th):** When you or an ally within 5 feet rolls dice to determine healing from a spell, you can spend 1 sorcery point to reroll any number of those dice once. You can use this feature only once per turn.
- **Otherworldly Wings (14th):** As a bonus action, you can manifest spectral wings that grant you a flying speed of 30 feet. The wings last until you are incapacitated, die, or dismiss them as a bonus action. The affinity you chose determines the wing appearance: eagle (Good), bat (Evil), dragonfly (Law), butterfly (Chaos), or hummingbird (Neutrality).
- **Unearthly Recovery (18th):** As a bonus action when you have fewer than half your hit points remaining, you can regain hit points equal to half your hit point maximum. Once per long rest.
