# Aberrant Mind

An alien influence has wrapped its tendrils around the mind of an Aberrant Mind sorcerer. This power might originate from the Far Realm, from a mind flayer, or from some other unknowable source, granting psionic spellcasting abilities.

**Class:** [[Sorcerer]] | **Source:** TCoE | **Subclass Features at:** 1st, 6th, 14th, 18th level

## Key Features
- **Psionic Spells (1st):** You learn additional spells that don't count against your spells known: [[Arms of Hadar|arms of Hadar]] and [[Dissonant Whispers|dissonant whispers]] (1st), [[Calm Emotions|calm emotions]] and [[Detect Thoughts|detect thoughts]] (3rd), [[Hunger of Hadar|hunger of Hadar]] and sending (5th), [[Evard's Black Tentacles|Evard's black tentacles]] and [[Summon Aberration|summon aberration]] (7th), telekinesis and Rary's telepathic bond (9th). When you gain a sorcerer level, you can swap one of these for a divination or enchantment spell of the same level from the sorcerer, warlock, or wizard list.
- **Telepathic Speech (1st):** As a bonus action, choose a creature within 30 feet. You and that creature can speak telepathically with each other while within a number of miles equal to your Charisma modifier. Lasts for a number of minutes equal to your sorcerer level.
- **Psionic Sorcery (6th):** You can cast any Psionic Spell by spending sorcery points equal to the spell's level instead of using a spell slot. When cast this way, the spell requires no verbal or somatic components, and no material components unless they are consumed.
- **Psychic Defenses (6th):** You gain resistance to psychic damage and advantage on saving throws against being charmed or frightened.
- **Revelation in Flesh (14th):** As a bonus action, spend 1 or more sorcery points to transform for 10 minutes. For each point, choose one: see invisible creatures within 60 feet, gain flying speed equal to walking speed (hover), gain swimming speed equal to twice walking speed and breathe underwater, or become slimy and pliable (move through 1-inch spaces, spend 5 feet per foot in tight spaces).
- **Warping Implosion (18th):** As an action, teleport up to 120 feet to an unoccupied space you can see. Each creature within 30 feet of the space you left must make a Strength save or take 3d10 force damage and be pulled toward the space you left. On a success, half damage and not pulled. Usable once per long rest (or for 5 sorcery points).
