# Draconic Bloodline

Your innate magic comes from draconic magic that was mingled with your blood or that of your ancestors. Most often, sorcerers with this origin trace their descent back to a mighty sorcerer of ancient times who made a bargain with a dragon or who might even have claimed a dragon parent.

**Class:** [[Sorcerer]] | **Source:** PHB | **Subclass Features at:** 1st, 6th, 14th, 18th level

## Key Features
- **Dragon Ancestor (1st):** Choose one type of dragon as your ancestor (black, blue, brass, bronze, copper, gold, green, red, silver, or white). This determines your associated damage type. You can speak, read, and write Draconic, and your proficiency bonus is doubled for Charisma checks when interacting with dragons.
- **Draconic Resilience (1st):** Your hit point maximum increases by 1 for each sorcerer level. Additionally, when you aren't wearing armor, your AC equals 13 + your DEX modifier.
- **Elemental Affinity (6th):** When you cast a spell that deals damage of the type associated with your draconic ancestry, you can add your CHA modifier to one damage roll of that spell. You can also spend 1 sorcery point to gain resistance to that damage type for 1 hour.
- **Dragon Wings (14th):** You gain the ability to sprout a pair of dragon wings from your back as a bonus action, gaining a flying speed equal to your current speed. The wings last until you dismiss them as a bonus action. You can't manifest your wings while wearing armor unless the armor is made to accommodate them.
- **Draconic Presence (18th):** You can channel the dread presence of your dragon ancestor as an action, creating an aura of awe or fear in a 60-foot radius for up to 1 minute (concentration). Each hostile creature that starts its turn in the aura must succeed on a WIS save or be charmed (awe) or frightened (fear) until the aura ends. Costs 5 sorcery points.
