# Shadow Magic

The Shadow Magic sorcerer draws power from the Shadowfell, commanding darkness itself to obscure, terrify, and destroy with the cold touch of shadow.

**Class:** [[Sorcerer]] | **Source:** XGtE | **Subclass Features at:** 1st, 3rd, 6th, 14th, 18th level

## Key Features
- **Eyes of the Dark (1st):** You gain darkvision out to 120 feet. At [[3rd Level|3rd level]], you can cast darkness by spending 2 sorcery points, and when you do, you can see through the darkness created by that casting.
- **Strength of the Grave (1st):** When damage reduces you to 0 hit points, you can make a CHA saving throw (DC 5 + damage taken). On a success, you instead drop to 1 hit point. You can't use this feature if you are reduced to 0 HP by radiant damage or a critical hit. Once per long rest.
- **Hound of Ill Omen (6th):** As a bonus action, you spend 3 sorcery points to summon a dire wolf made of shadow that targets one creature you can see within 120 feet. The hound pursues the target and has advantage on attacks against it. The target has disadvantage on saving throws against your spells while the hound is within 5 feet of it. The hound lasts for 5 minutes.
- **Shadow Walk (14th):** As a bonus action when you are in dim light or darkness, you can teleport up to 120 feet to an unoccupied space you can see that is also in dim light or darkness.
- **Umbral Form (18th):** As a bonus action, you spend 6 sorcery points to transform into a shadowy form for 1 minute. You have resistance to all damage except force and radiant, and you can move through other creatures and objects as if they were difficult terrain (5 force damage if you end your turn inside an object).
