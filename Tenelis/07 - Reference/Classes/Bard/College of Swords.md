# College of Swords

The College of Swords bard entertains through daring feats of weapon prowess, blending swordplay with performance in a deadly dance of blades.

**Class:** [[Bard]] | **Source:** XGtE | **Subclass Features at:** 3rd, 6th, 14th level

## Key Features
- **Bonus Proficiencies (3rd):** You gain proficiency with medium armor and the scimitar. You can use a weapon you are proficient with as a spellcasting focus.
- **Fighting Style (3rd):** Choose Dueling (+2 damage with one-handed weapon) or [[Two-Weapon Fighting]] (add ability modifier to off-hand damage).
- **Blade Flourish (3rd):** When you take the Attack action, your walking speed increases by 10 feet. You can expend a Bardic Inspiration die to add to the damage roll and choose one flourish: Defensive (add the die to your AC until next turn), Slashing (deal the die as damage to another adjacent creature), or [[Mobile]] (add the die to a Shove attempt and move the target).
- **Extra Attack (6th):** You can attack twice when you take the Attack action.
- **Master's Flourish (14th):** When you use a Blade Flourish, you can roll a d6 instead of expending a Bardic Inspiration die.
