# College of Creation

Bards of the College of Creation believe the cosmos is a work of art — the creation of the first combineers. They draw on that cosmic power of creation to craft wonders with their performances.

**Class:** [[Bard]] | **Source:** TCoE | **Subclass Features at:** 3rd, 6th, 14th level

## Key Features
- **Mote of Potential (3rd):** When you give a creature a Bardic Inspiration die, a mote orbits them providing an extra effect depending on how the die is used. Ability check: roll the die twice and choose. Attack roll: deal thunder damage equal to the die roll to each creature within 5 feet. Saving throw: gain temporary HP equal to the roll + your Charisma modifier.
- **Performance of Creation (3rd):** As an action, create a nonmagical item of your choice that is no more than Large size and worth no more than 20 x your bard level in gold. It lasts a number of hours equal to your proficiency bonus. Usable once per long rest (or for a 2nd-level spell slot).
- **Animating Performance (6th):** As an action, animate a Large or smaller nonmagical item within 30 feet as a dancing item. It is a construct that uses your bonus action to command, can attack (force damage), and can carry objects. Lasts 1 hour or until reduced to 0 HP.
- **Creative Crescendo (14th):** When you use [[Performance]] of Creation, you can create a number of items equal to your Charisma modifier (minimum two). One item can be Large; the rest must be Small or Tiny. You are no longer limited by gold piece value.
