# Inquisitive

The Inquisitive rogue excels at rooting out secrets and unraveling mysteries, using keen observation and insight to expose hidden truths and exploit enemy weaknesses.

**Class:** [[Rogue]] | **Source:** XGtE | **Subclass Features at:** 3rd, 9th, 13th, 17th level

## Key Features
- **Ear for Deceit (3rd):** Whenever you make a Wisdom ([[Insight]]) check to determine if a creature is lying, treat a roll of 7 or lower on the d20 as an 8.
- **Eye for Detail (3rd):** You can use a bonus action to make a Wisdom ([[Perception]]) check to spot a hidden creature or object, or an Intelligence ([[Investigation]]) check to uncover or decipher clues.
- **Insightful Fighting (3rd):** As a bonus action, make a Wisdom ([[Insight]]) check contested by a target's Charisma ([[Deception]]). On success, you can use Sneak Attack against that target even without advantage for 1 minute, provided you don't have disadvantage on the attack roll.
- **Steady Eye (9th):** You have advantage on Wisdom ([[Perception]]) and Intelligence ([[Investigation]]) checks if you move no more than half your speed on the same turn.
- **Unerring Eye (13th):** As an action, you can sense the presence of illusions, shapechangers, and other magic designed to deceive the senses within 30 feet, though you don't learn what is hidden or its true nature. Uses equal to WIS modifier per long rest.
- **Eye for Weakness (17th):** While your Insightful Fighting feature applies to a creature, your Sneak Attack damage against that creature increases by 3d6.
