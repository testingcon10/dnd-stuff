# Swashbuckler

The Swashbuckler rogue combines agility, charm, and panache in equal measure, excelling in single combat and using wit and daring to outmaneuver foes.

**Class:** [[Rogue]] | **Source:** XGtE | **Subclass Features at:** 3rd, 9th, 13th, 17th level

## Key Features
- **Fancy Footwork (3rd):** During your turn, if you make a melee attack against a creature, that creature can't make opportunity attacks against you for the rest of your turn.
- **Rakish Audacity (3rd):** You add your CHA modifier to initiative rolls. You also don't need advantage on the attack roll to use Sneak Attack if no creature other than your target is within 5 feet of you (you still can't have disadvantage).
- **Panache (9th):** As an action, make a Persuasion check contested by the target's Insight. In combat, on a success the target has disadvantage attacking creatures other than you and can't make opportunity attacks against others for 1 minute (it ends if your allies attack it or affect it with a spell, or if you move more than 60 feet away). Outside combat, on a success the creature is charmed by you for 1 minute.
- **Elegant Maneuver (13th):** You can use a bonus action to gain advantage on the next Acrobatics or Athletics check you make during the same turn.
- **Master Duelist (17th):** If you miss with an attack roll, you can roll it again with advantage. Once per short or long rest.
