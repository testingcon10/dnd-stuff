# Phantom

Phantom rogues walk a fine line between life and death. They have discovered how to channel the power of the dead, gaining abilities fueled by departed souls that drift in their wake.

**Class:** [[Rogue]] | **Source:** TCoE | **Subclass Features at:** 3rd, 9th, 13th, 17th level

## Key Features
- **Whispers of the Dead (3rd):** Whenever you finish a short or long rest, you can gain proficiency in one skill or tool of your choice, as a ghostly presence shares its knowledge with you. This proficiency lasts until you use this feature again.
- **Wails from the Grave (3rd):** When you deal Sneak Attack damage, you can target a second creature within 30 feet of the first. The second creature takes necrotic damage equal to half the number of Sneak Attack dice rolled (rounded up). Usable proficiency bonus times per long rest.
- **Tokens of the Departed (9th):** When a creature you can see dies within 30 feet of you, you can use your reaction to create a soul trinket (tiny trinket, max equal to proficiency bonus). While a trinket is on your person, you have advantage on death saves and Constitution saves. You can destroy a trinket to ask one question of the spirit associated with it, or to use Wails from the Grave without expending a use.
- **Ghost Walk (13th):** As a bonus action, assume a spectral form for 10 minutes. You gain a flying speed of 10 feet (hover), and you can move through creatures and objects as if they were difficult terrain (1d10 force damage if you end your turn inside). Usable once per long rest (or by destroying a soul trinket).
- **Death's Friend (17th):** Your Wails from the Grave deals damage equal to the full number of Sneak Attack dice (not half). At the end of a long rest, a soul trinket appears in your hand if you have none.
