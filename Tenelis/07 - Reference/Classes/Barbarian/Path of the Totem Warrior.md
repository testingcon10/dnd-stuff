# Path of the Totem Warrior

The Path of the Totem Warrior is a spiritual journey, as you accept a spirit animal as guide, protector, and inspiration. In battle, your totem spirit fills you with supernatural might, adding magical fuel to your barbarian rage.

**Class:** [[Barbarian]] | **Source:** PHB | **Subclass Features at:** 3rd, 6th, 10th, 14th level

## Key Features
- **Spirit Seeker (3rd):** You gain the ability to cast *[[Beast Sense|beast sense]]* and *[[Speak with Animals|speak with animals]]* as rituals.
- **Totem Spirit (3rd):** Choose a totem animal that grants a benefit while raging. **Bear:** You have resistance to all damage except psychic. **Eagle:** Opportunity attacks against you have disadvantage, and you can Dash as a bonus action. **Wolf:** Your allies have advantage on melee attack rolls against any hostile creature within 5 feet of you.
- **Aspect of the Beast (6th):** Choose a totem animal for a passive benefit. **Bear:** Your carrying capacity doubles and you have advantage on STR checks to push, pull, lift, or break objects. **Eagle:** You can see up to 1 mile away with no difficulty and dim light doesn't impose disadvantage on [[Perception]] checks. **Wolf:** You can track creatures at a fast pace and move stealthily at a normal pace.
- **Spirit Walker (10th):** You can cast *[[Commune with Nature|commune with nature]]* as a ritual. The spell's information comes from the spiritual forces of nature connected to your totem.
- **Totemic Attunement (14th):** Choose a totem animal for a combat benefit. **Bear:** While raging, hostile creatures within 5 feet of you have disadvantage on attack rolls against targets other than you. **Eagle:** While raging, you gain a flying speed equal to your walking speed (you fall if you end your turn in the air). **Wolf:** While raging, you can use a bonus action to knock a Large or smaller creature prone when you hit it with a melee weapon attack.
