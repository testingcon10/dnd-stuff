# Path of the Zealot

The Path of the Zealot barbarian is a warrior fueled by divine fury, fighting as a champion of the gods with a fervor that borders on the supernatural.

**Class:** [[Barbarian]] | **Source:** XGtE | **Subclass Features at:** 3rd, 6th, 10th, 14th level

## Key Features
- **Divine Fury (3rd):** While raging, the first creature you hit on each of your turns takes an extra 1d6+half barbarian level radiant or necrotic damage (your choice when you select this path).
- **Warrior of the Gods (3rd):** Spells that restore you to life (but not undeath) don't require material components when cast on you.
- **Fanatical Focus (6th):** While raging, if you fail a saving throw you can reroll it and must use the new roll. Once per rage.
- **Zealous Presence (10th):** As a bonus action, you unleash a battle cry and up to 10 creatures of your choice within 60 feet gain advantage on attack rolls and saving throws until your next turn. Once per long rest.
- **Rage Beyond Death (14th):** While raging, dropping to 0 hit points doesn't knock you unconscious. You still make death saving throws, and you only die if you fail three death saves or your rage ends while at 0 HP.
