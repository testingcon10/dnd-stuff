# Path of the Beast

Barbarians who walk the Path of the Beast draw their rage from a bestial spark burning within their soul. When they rage, the beast within physically transforms them, granting natural weapons.

**Class:** [[Barbarian]] | **Source:** TCoE | **Subclass Features at:** 3rd, 6th, 10th, 14th level

## Key Features
- **Form of the Beast (3rd):** When you rage, you manifest a natural weapon. Choose Bite (1d8 piercing, heals you equal to proficiency bonus if below half HP), Claws (1d6 slashing each, one extra claw attack when you use the Attack action), or Tail (1d8 piercing, reach 10ft, reaction to add +d8 to AC when hit).
- **Bestial Soul (6th):** Your natural weapons count as magical. When you finish a short or long rest, choose one adaptation: swimming speed equal to walking speed, climb speed with free hands, or extend long jump by [[Athletics]] check feet.
- **Infectious Fury (10th):** When you hit with your natural weapons, you can curse the target (Wisdom save). On a failure, the target must either use its reaction to make a melee attack against a creature you choose, or take 2d12 psychic damage.
- **Call the Hunt (14th):** When you enter your rage, choose a number of willing creatures equal to your Constitution modifier within 30 feet. Each gains extra damage equal to 1d6 once per turn. You gain 5 temporary HP per creature that accepts.
