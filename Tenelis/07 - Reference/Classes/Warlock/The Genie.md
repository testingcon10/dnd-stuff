# The Genie

You have made a pact with a noble genie, a creature of elemental power. Your patron is one of four types: Dao (earth), Djinni (air), Efreeti (fire), or Marid (water). Each type grants a different expanded spell list and damage type.

**Class:** [[Warlock]] | **Source:** TCoE | **Subclass Features at:** 1st, 6th, 10th, 14th level

## Key Features
- **Expanded Spell List (1st):** Varies by genie type. Dao: sanctuary, [[Spike Growth|spike growth]], [[Create Food and Water|create food and water]], [[Stone Shape|stone shape]], [[Wall of Stone|wall of stone]]. Djinni: thunderwave, [[Gust of Wind|gust of wind]], [[Wind Wall|wind wall]], [[Greater Invisibility|greater invisibility]], seeming. Efreeti: [[Burning Hands|burning hands]], [[Scorching Ray|scorching ray]], fireball, [[Fire Shield|fire shield]], [[Flame Strike|flame strike]]. Marid: [[Fog Cloud|fog cloud]], blur, [[Sleet Storm|sleet storm]], [[Control Water|control water]], [[Cone of Cold|cone of cold]]. All types also share: [[Detect Evil and Good|detect evil and good]], [[Phantasmal Force|phantasmal force]], [[Create Food and Water|create food and water]], [[Phantasmal Killer|phantasmal killer]], creation, wish (9th-level shared spell).
- **Genie's Vessel (1st):** You have a Tiny object (ring, lamp, etc.) that serves as your vessel. As a bonus action, you can enter the vessel's extradimensional interior (a 20-foot cylinder), remaining inside for up to twice your proficiency bonus in hours. You can see and hear outside through the vessel. Usable once per long rest.
- **Genie's Wrath (1st):** Once per turn when you hit with an attack roll, deal extra damage equal to your proficiency bonus. The damage type depends on your patron: bludgeoning (Dao), thunder (Djinni), fire (Efreeti), or cold (Marid).
- **Elemental Gift (6th):** You gain resistance to a damage type determined by your patron (bludgeoning/thunder/fire/cold). Additionally, as a bonus action, you can gain a flying speed of 30 feet (hover) for 10 minutes. Usable proficiency bonus times per long rest.
- **Sanctuary Vessel (10th):** When you enter your vessel, you can choose up to 5 willing creatures within 30 feet to enter with you. Anyone who remains inside for at least 10 minutes gains the benefit of a short rest, and they can add your proficiency bonus to HP regained from Hit Dice.
- **Limited Wish (14th):** As an action, you contact your patron and request the effect of any spell of [[6th Level|6th level]] or lower with a casting time of 1 action. You don't need to meet any requirements, provide material components, or have the spell on your list. Usable once per 1d4 long rests.
