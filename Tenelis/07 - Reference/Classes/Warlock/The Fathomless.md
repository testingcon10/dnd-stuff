# The Fathomless

You have plighted your pact with an entity of the deep ocean -- a kraken, an elder elemental, or some other unfathomable being. The Fathomless patron grants power drawn from the crushing depths of the sea.

**Class:** [[Warlock]] | **Source:** TCoE | **Subclass Features at:** 1st, 6th, 10th, 14th level

## Key Features
- **Expanded Spell List (1st):** Your patron adds spells to your warlock spell list: [[Create or Destroy Water|create or destroy water]] and thunderwave (1st), [[Gust of Wind|gust of wind]] and silence (2nd), [[Lightning Bolt|lightning bolt]] and [[Sleet Storm|sleet storm]] (3rd), [[Control Water|control water]] and [[Summon Elemental|summon elemental]] (4th), [[Bigby's Hand|Bigby's hand]] (appearing as a tentacle) and [[Cone of Cold|cone of cold]] (5th).
- **Tentacle of the Deeps (1st):** As a bonus action, create a spectral tentacle at a point within 60 feet for 1 minute. When you create it and as a bonus action on subsequent turns, you can make a melee spell attack through the tentacle dealing 1d8 cold damage and reducing the target's speed by 10 feet until the start of your next turn. Usable proficiency bonus times per long rest.
- **Gift of the Sea (1st):** You gain a swimming speed of 40 feet and can breathe underwater.
- **Oceanic Soul (6th):** You gain resistance to cold damage. While fully submerged in water, you and any creature also fully submerged can understand each other's speech.
- **Guardian Coil (6th):** When you or a creature you can see within 10 feet of your tentacle takes damage, you can use your reaction to reduce the damage by 1d8 (increases with warlock level).
- **Grasping Tentacles (10th):** You learn [[Evard's Black Tentacles|Evard's black tentacles]] (it doesn't count against your spells known). You can cast it once without a spell slot per long rest. When you cast it, you gain temporary HP equal to your warlock level, and damage from the spell can't break your concentration.
- **Fathomless Plunge (14th):** As an action, teleport yourself and up to 5 willing creatures you can see within 30 feet to a body of water you've visited. Each creature appears within 30 feet of each other at the destination. Usable once per short or long rest.
