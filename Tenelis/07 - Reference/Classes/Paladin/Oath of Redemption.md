# Oath of Redemption

The Oath of Redemption paladin believes that any creature can find salvation and seeks peace above all, using violence only as a last resort and absorbing harm meant for others.

**Class:** [[Paladin]] | **Source:** XGtE | **Subclass Features at:** 3rd, 7th, 15th, 20th level

## Key Features
- **Oath Spells (3rd):** You gain oath spells at the listed paladin levels: sanctuary and sleep (3rd), [[Calm Emotions|calm emotions]] and [[Hold Person|hold person]] (5th), counterspell and [[Hypnotic Pattern|hypnotic pattern]] (9th), [[Otiluke's Resilient Sphere|Otiluke's resilient sphere]] and stoneskin (13th), [[Hold Monster|hold monster]] and [[Wall of Force|wall of force]] (17th).
- **Channel Divinity: Emissary of Peace (3rd):** As a bonus action, you grant yourself a +5 bonus to Persuasion checks for the next 10 minutes.
- **Channel Divinity: Rebuke the Violent (3rd):** As a reaction, when a creature within 30 feet deals damage with an attack against a creature other than you, the attacker must make a WIS save or take radiant damage equal to the damage it just dealt (half on a success).
- **Aura of the Guardian (7th):** When a creature within 10 feet (20 feet at 18th level) takes damage, you can use your reaction to magically take that damage instead. This damage can't be reduced in any way.
- **Protective Spirit (15th):** You regain hit points equal to 1d6 + half your paladin level at the end of your turn in combat if you have fewer than half your hit points remaining and aren't incapacitated.
- **Emissary of Redemption (20th):** You gain resistance to all damage dealt by other creatures. Whenever a creature damages you, it takes radiant damage equal to half the amount it dealt. If you attack a creature, cast a spell on it, or deal damage to it, this feature stops working against that creature until you finish a long rest.
