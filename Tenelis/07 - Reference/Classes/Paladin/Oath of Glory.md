# Oath of Glory

Paladins who take the Oath of Glory believe they and their companions are destined to achieve glory through deeds of heroism. They train diligently and encourage their companions to strive for greatness.

**Class:** [[Paladin]] | **Source:** TCoE | **Subclass Features at:** 3rd, 7th, 15th, 20th level

## Key Features
- **Oath Spells (3rd):** You gain oath spells at the listed paladin levels: [[Guiding Bolt|guiding bolt]] and heroism (3rd), [[Enhance Ability|enhance ability]] and [[Magic Weapon|magic weapon]] (5th), haste and [[Protection from Energy|protection from energy]] (9th), compulsion and [[Freedom of Movement|freedom of movement]] (13th), commune and [[Flame Strike|flame strike]] (17th).
- **Channel Divinity: Peerless Athlete (3rd):** As a bonus action, for 10 minutes you gain advantage on [[Athletics]] and [[Acrobatics]] checks, your carrying capacity and push/drag/lift weight is doubled, and the distance of your long and high jumps increases by 10 feet.
- **Channel Divinity: Inspiring Smite (3rd):** Immediately after you deal damage with [[Divine Smite]], you can use your Channel Divinity as a bonus action to distribute temporary HP equal to 2d8 + your paladin level among creatures within 30 feet (including yourself).
- **Aura of Alacrity (7th):** Your walking speed increases by 10 feet. Allies within 5 feet of you also gain a 10-foot speed increase (10 feet increases to 10 feet of allies at 18th level).
- **Glorious Defense (15th):** When a creature you can see within 10 feet is hit by an attack, you can use your reaction to add your Charisma modifier to the target's AC against that attack. If the attack still misses, you can make one weapon attack against the attacker. Usable Charisma modifier times per long rest.
- **Living Legend (20th):** As a bonus action, for 1 minute you gain: advantage on Charisma checks, once per turn you can make a missed weapon attack hit instead, and you emanate an aura of awe (creatures within 10 feet on a failed Charisma save are frightened). Usable once per long rest (or for a 5th-level spell slot).
