# Bladesinging

Bladesingers master a tradition of wizardry that incorporates swordplay and dance. Originally created by elves, this tradition has been adopted by non-elf practitioners in Faerun and beyond.

**Class:** [[Wizard]] | **Source:** TCoE | **Subclass Features at:** 2nd, 6th, 10th, 14th level

## Key Features
- **Training in War and Song (2nd):** You gain proficiency with light armor and one type of one-handed melee weapon of your choice.
- **Bladesong (2nd):** As a bonus action, start a Bladesong that lasts for 1 minute (ends early if you are incapacitated, don medium/heavy armor, a shield, or use two hands with a weapon). While active: add Intelligence modifier to AC, add 10 feet to walking speed, advantage on [[Acrobatics]] checks, and add Intelligence modifier to Constitution saves for concentration. Usable proficiency bonus times per long rest.
- **Extra Attack (6th):** You can attack twice when you take the Attack action. Moreover, you can replace one of those attacks with casting a cantrip.
- **Song of Defense (10th):** While your Bladesong is active, when you take damage, you can use your reaction to expend a spell slot and reduce the damage by 5 x the spell slot's level.
- **Song of Victory (14th):** While your Bladesong is active, you add your Intelligence modifier to the damage of your melee weapon attacks.
