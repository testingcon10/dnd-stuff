# Circle of Dreams

The Circle of Dreams druid draws on the magic of the Feywild, using their connection to the realm of dreams to mend wounds and transport allies.

**Class:** [[Druid]] | **Source:** XGtE | **Subclass Features at:** 2nd, 6th, 10th, 14th level

## Key Features
- **Balm of the Summer Court (2nd):** You have a pool of d6s equal to your druid level. As a bonus action, you can spend up to half your druid level in d6s to heal a creature you can see within 120 feet. The target also gains 1 temporary hit point per die spent.
- **Hearth of Moonlight and Shadow (6th):** During a short or long rest, you can create a 30-foot-radius invisible ward. You and your allies gain a +5 bonus to Stealth and Perception checks while resting there, and light from open flames is not visible outside the sphere.
- **Hidden Paths (10th):** As a bonus action, you can teleport up to 60 feet to an unoccupied space you can see. Alternatively, as an action, you can teleport a willing creature you touch to an unoccupied space within 30 feet. Uses equal to WIS modifier per long rest.
- **Walker in Dreams (14th):** After a short rest, you can cast dream, scrying, or [[Teleportation Circle|teleportation circle]] (connecting to the last place you finished a long rest) without expending a spell slot. Once per long rest.
