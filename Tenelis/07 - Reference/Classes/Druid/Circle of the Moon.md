# Circle of the Moon

Druids of the Circle of the Moon are fierce guardians of the wilds. Their order gathers under the full moon to share news and trade warnings. They haunt the deepest parts of the wilderness, where they might go for weeks on end before crossing paths with another humanoid creature.

**Class:** [[Druid]] | **Source:** PHB | **Subclass Features at:** 2nd, 6th, 10th, 14th level

## Key Features
- **Combat Wild Shape (2nd):** You can use Wild Shape as a bonus action rather than an action. While in beast form, you can expend a spell slot to regain 1d8 hit points per level of the spell slot expended.
- **Circle Forms (2nd):** You can use Wild Shape to transform into beasts with a CR as high as 1 (no walking speed limitation). Starting at [[6th Level|6th level]], you can transform into a beast with a CR as high as your druid level divided by 3, rounded down.
- **Primal Strike (6th):** Your attacks in beast form count as magical for the purpose of overcoming resistance and immunity to nonmagical attacks and damage.
- **Elemental Wild Shape (10th):** You can expend two uses of Wild Shape at the same time to transform into an air elemental, earth elemental, fire elemental, or water elemental.
- **Thousand Forms (14th):** You can cast *[[Alter Self|alter self]]* at will.
