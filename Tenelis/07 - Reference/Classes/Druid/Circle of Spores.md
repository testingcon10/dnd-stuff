# Circle of Spores

Druids of the Circle of Spores find beauty in decay. They see mold and other fungi as symbols of life's endless cycle and draw power from the process of death and rebirth.

**Class:** [[Druid]] | **Source:** TCoE | **Subclass Features at:** 2nd, 6th, 10th, 14th level

## Key Features
- **Circle Spells (2nd):** You learn the [[Chill Touch|chill touch]] cantrip. At certain druid levels, you gain circle spells: blindness/deafness and [[Gentle Repose|gentle repose]] (3rd), [[Animate Dead|animate dead]] and [[Gaseous Form|gaseous form]] (5th), blight and confusion (7th), cloudkill and contagion (9th).
- **Halo of Spores (2nd):** As a reaction when a creature you can see moves within 10 feet of you or starts its turn there, you deal 1d4 necrotic damage to it (Constitution save negates). Damage increases to 1d6 (6th), 1d8 (10th), 1d10 (14th).
- **Symbiotic Entity (2nd):** As an action, expend a use of Wild Shape to gain 4 x druid level temporary HP, double Halo of Spores damage, and an extra 1d6 necrotic damage on melee weapon attacks. Lasts 10 minutes or until you lose all the temporary HP.
- **Fungal Infestation (6th):** When a Small or Medium beast or humanoid dies within 10 feet of you, you can use your reaction to animate it as a zombie with 1 HP. It acts on your initiative, can take only the Attack action, and lasts for 1 hour. Usable Wisdom modifier times per long rest.
- **Spreading Spores (10th):** As a bonus action while Symbiotic Entity is active, you can hurl your spores up to 30 feet to create a 10-foot cube of Halo of Spores. You can't use your reaction Halo of Spores while the cube is active.
- **Fungal Body (14th):** You can't be blinded, deafened, frightened, or poisoned, and any critical hit against you counts as a normal hit unless you're incapacitated.
