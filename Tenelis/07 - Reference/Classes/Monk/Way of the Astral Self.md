# Way of the Astral Self

Monks of the Way of the Astral Self see their body as an illusion. Their true self is a multilimbed astral form that they can manifest to devastating effect in combat and interaction.

**Class:** [[Monk]] | **Source:** TCoE | **Subclass Features at:** 3rd, 6th, 11th, 17th level

## Key Features
- **Arms of the Astral Self (3rd):** As a bonus action, spend 1 ki point to summon spectral arms for 10 minutes. Each creature of your choice within 10 feet must make a Dexterity save or take force damage. While active, you use Wisdom instead of Strength for unarmed strikes with the arms, they have 5-foot extra reach, and you deal extra force damage equal to your Martial Arts die.
- **Visage of the Astral Self (6th):** Spend 1 ki point (or activate when summoning arms) to manifest a visage for 10 minutes. Grants: darkvision 120 feet (Astral Sight), advantage on Insight and Intimidation checks (Wisdom of the Spirit), and the ability to speak so only chosen creatures hear you plus understanding of all languages (Word of the Spirit).
- **Body of the Astral Self (11th):** When you have both your arms and visage active, spend 1 ki point to manifest the body for the remaining duration. Deflect Energy: reduce acid, cold, fire, force, lightning, or thunder damage by 1d10 + your Wisdom modifier (like Deflect Missiles but for energy). Empowered Arms: once per turn, deal extra damage equal to your Martial Arts die with your astral arms.
- **Awakened Astral Self (17th):** As a bonus action, spend 5 ki points to summon arms, visage, and body simultaneously for 10 minutes. While all three are active, you gain +2 to AC, and when you use Extra Attack, you can make three attacks with the astral arms instead of two.
