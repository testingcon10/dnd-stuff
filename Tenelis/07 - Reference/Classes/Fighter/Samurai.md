# Samurai

The Samurai fighter combines martial prowess with an unbreakable fighting spirit, gaining advantage on attacks at will and refusing to fall even at death's door.

**Class:** [[Fighter]] | **Source:** XGtE | **Subclass Features at:** 3rd, 7th, 10th, 15th, 18th level

## Key Features
- **Bonus Proficiency (3rd):** You gain proficiency in [[History]], [[Insight]], [[Performance]], or [[Persuasion]]. Alternatively, you learn one language.
- **Fighting Spirit (3rd):** As a bonus action, you gain advantage on all weapon attack rolls and 5 temporary hit points until the end of your current turn. Temp HP increases to 10 at 10th and 15 at 15th level. Three uses per long rest.
- **Elegant Courtier (7th):** You can add your WIS modifier to [[Persuasion]] checks. You also gain proficiency in WIS saving throws. If you already have this proficiency, you gain INT or CHA save proficiency instead.
- **Tireless Spirit (10th):** When you roll initiative and have no uses of Fighting Spirit remaining, you regain one use.
- **Rapid Strike (15th):** If you have advantage on a weapon attack against a target, you can forgo the advantage on one attack to make an additional weapon attack against that target as part of the same action. Once per turn.
- **Strength Before Death (18th):** When you are reduced to 0 hit points but not killed outright, you can use your reaction to take an entire extra turn, interrupting the current turn. You still fall unconscious if you are still at 0 HP after the extra turn. Once per long rest.
