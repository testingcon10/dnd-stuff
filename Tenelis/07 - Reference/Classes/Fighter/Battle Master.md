# Battle Master

Those who emulate the Battle Master archetype employ martial techniques passed down through generations. A Battle Master uses superiority dice to fuel special maneuvers, granting tactical advantages in combat.

**Class:** [[Fighter]] | **Source:** PHB | **Subclass Features at:** 3rd, 7th, 10th, 15th, 18th level

## Key Features
- **Combat Superiority (3rd):** You learn three maneuvers of your choice from a list of 16 (such as Trip Attack, Riposte, Precision Attack, etc.). You gain four superiority dice (d8s), which are expended when you use a maneuver and regained on a short or long rest. Your save DC equals 8 + proficiency bonus + STR or DEX modifier. You learn two additional maneuvers at 7th, 10th, and 15th level. Your superiority dice increase to five at [[7th Level|7th level]] and six at 15th level.
- **Student of War (3rd):** You gain proficiency with one type of [[Artisan's Tools|artisan's tools]] of your choice.
- **Know Your Enemy (7th):** If you spend at least 1 minute observing or interacting with a creature outside combat, the DM tells you if the creature is your equal, superior, or inferior with regard to two characteristics of your choice (such as STR score, AC, current HP, class levels, fighter levels, etc.).
- **Improved Combat Superiority (10th):** Your superiority dice become d10s. At 18th level, they become d12s.
- **Relentless (15th):** When you roll initiative and have no superiority dice remaining, you regain one superiority die.
