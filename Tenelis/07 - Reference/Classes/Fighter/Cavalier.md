# Cavalier

The Cavalier is a master of mounted combat and battlefield control, excelling at protecting allies and marking enemies.

**Class:** [[Fighter]] | **Source:** XGtE | **Subclass Features at:** 3rd, 7th, 10th, 15th, 18th level

## Key Features
- **Bonus Proficiency (3rd):** Proficiency in Animal Handling, History, Insight, Performance, or Persuasion.
- **Born to the Saddle (3rd):** Advantage on saves to avoid falling off mount, land on feet if fall less than 10 feet, and mounting/dismounting costs only 5 feet of movement.
- **Unwavering Mark (3rd):** When you hit a creature with a melee weapon attack, you mark it until the end of your next turn. It has disadvantage on attack rolls against targets other than you, and you can make a bonus action melee attack against it if it damages anyone else (with extra damage equal to half fighter level).
- **Warding Maneuver (7th):** When you or a creature within 5 feet is hit by an attack, you can use your reaction to roll 1d8 and add it to the target's AC. If the attack still hits, the target gains resistance to the attack's damage. Uses equal to CON modifier per long rest.
- **Hold the Line (10th):** Creatures provoke an opportunity attack from you when they move 5 feet or more while within your reach, not just when they leave your reach. On a hit, the target's speed becomes 0 until end of turn.
- **Ferocious Charger (15th):** If you move at least 10 feet in a straight line before attacking a creature and hit, the target must succeed on a STR save or be knocked prone.
- **Vigilant Defender (18th):** You gain a special reaction on every other creature's turn (not your own) that can only be used for opportunity attacks. This is in addition to your normal reaction.
