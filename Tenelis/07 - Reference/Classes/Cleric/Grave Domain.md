# Grave Domain

The Grave Domain cleric watches over the line between life and death, ensuring the dead rest in peace and that the living reach their destined end naturally.

**Class:** [[Cleric]] | **Source:** XGtE | **Subclass Features at:** 1st, 2nd, 6th, 8th, 17th level

## Key Features
- **Circle of Mortality (1st):** When you use a healing spell on a creature at 0 hit points, treat any dice rolled to determine healing as having rolled their maximum value. Additionally, you learn [[Spare the Dying|spare the dying]], which you can cast as a bonus action with a range of 30 feet.
- **Eyes of the Grave (1st):** As an action, you can detect the location of any undead within 60 feet that isn't behind total cover. Uses equal to WIS modifier per long rest.
- **Channel Divinity: Path to the Grave (2nd):** As an action, choose one creature within 30 feet. The next time that creature takes damage from an attack, it is vulnerable to all of that attack's damage. Lasts until the end of your next turn.
- **Sentinel at Death's Door (6th):** As a reaction, when you or a creature within 30 feet suffers a critical hit, you can turn it into a normal hit. Uses equal to WIS modifier per long rest.
- **Potent Spellcasting (8th):** Add your WIS modifier to the damage of your cleric cantrips.
- **Keeper of Souls (17th):** When an enemy you can see dies within 60 feet, you or one ally within 60 feet regains hit points equal to the enemy's number of Hit Dice. Once per round.
