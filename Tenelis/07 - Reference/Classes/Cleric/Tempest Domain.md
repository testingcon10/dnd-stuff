# Tempest Domain

Gods whose portfolios include the Tempest domain govern storms, sea, and sky. Tempest clerics inspire fear in common folk by wielding the power of lightning, thunder, and devastating storms.

**Class:** [[Cleric]] | **Source:** PHB | **Subclass Features at:** 1st, 2nd, 6th, 8th, 17th level

## Key Features
- **Bonus Proficiencies (1st):** You gain proficiency with martial weapons and heavy armor.
- **Wrath of the Storm (1st):** When a creature within 5 feet of you that you can see hits you with an attack, you can use your reaction to force the creature to make a DEX save. On a failure, the creature takes 2d8 lightning or thunder damage (your choice), or half on a success. You can use this a number of times equal to your Wisdom modifier.
- **Channel Divinity: Destructive Wrath (2nd):** When you roll lightning or thunder damage, you can use your Channel Divinity to deal maximum damage instead of rolling.
- **Thunderbolt Strike (6th):** When you deal lightning damage to a Large or smaller creature, you can push it up to 10 feet away from you.
- **Divine Strike (8th):** Once on each of your turns when you hit a creature with a weapon attack, you can deal an extra 1d8 thunder damage. This increases to 2d8 at 14th level.
- **Stormborn (17th):** You have a flying speed equal to your current walking speed whenever you are outdoors and not underground.
