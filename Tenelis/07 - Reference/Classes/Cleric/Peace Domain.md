# Peace Domain

The Peace Domain encourages reconciliation and understanding. Clerics of this domain preside over the signing of treaties and are often asked to arbitrate disputes.

**Class:** [[Cleric]] | **Source:** TCoE | **Subclass Features at:** 1st, 2nd, 6th, 8th, 17th level

## Key Features
- **Implement of Peace (1st):** You gain proficiency in one of the following skills of your choice: [[Insight]], [[Performance]], or [[Persuasion]].
- **Emboldening Bond (1st):** As an action, bond a number of creatures equal to your proficiency bonus together for 10 minutes. While bonded and within 30 feet of each other, a bonded creature can add 1d4 to an attack roll, ability check, or saving throw once per turn. Usable proficiency bonus times per long rest.
- **Channel Divinity: Balm of Peace (2nd):** As an action, move up to your speed without provoking opportunity attacks. When you move within 5 feet of any creature, you can restore hit points equal to 2d6 + your Wisdom modifier (each creature only once).
- **Protective Bond (6th):** When a bonded creature is about to take damage, another bonded creature within 30 feet can use their reaction to teleport to an unoccupied space within 5 feet and take all the damage instead.
- **Potent Spellcasting (8th):** Add your Wisdom modifier to the damage you deal with cleric cantrips.
- **Expansive Bond (17th):** The range of your Emboldening Bond and Protective Bond increases to 60 feet. When a creature uses Protective Bond to take damage, they gain resistance to that damage.
