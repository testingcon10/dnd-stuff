# Life Domain

The Life domain focuses on the vibrant positive energy that sustains all life. Clerics who tap into this domain are among the most accomplished healers in the multiverse, and their magic grants vitality and health.

**Class:** [[Cleric]] | **Source:** PHB | **Subclass Features at:** 1st, 2nd, 6th, 8th, 17th level

## Key Features
- **Bonus Proficiency (1st):** You gain proficiency with heavy armor.
- **Disciple of Life (1st):** Whenever you use a spell of [[1st Level|1st level]] or higher to restore hit points, the creature regains additional hit points equal to 2 + the spell's level.
- **Channel Divinity: Preserve Life (2nd):** As an action, you present your [[Holy Symbol|holy symbol]] and restore a total number of hit points equal to five times your cleric level, divided among creatures within 30 feet. This can't raise a creature above half its hit point maximum.
- **Blessed Healer (6th):** When you cast a spell of [[1st Level|1st level]] or higher that restores hit points to another creature, you also regain hit points equal to 2 + the spell's level.
- **Divine Strike (8th):** Once on each of your turns when you hit a creature with a weapon attack, you can deal an extra 1d8 radiant damage (increases to 2d8 at 14th level).
- **Supreme Healing (17th):** When you roll dice to restore hit points with a healing spell, you instead use the highest number possible for each die.
