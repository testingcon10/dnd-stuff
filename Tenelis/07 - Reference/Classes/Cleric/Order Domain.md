# Order Domain

The Order Domain represents discipline, devotion to law, and the conviction that a well-ordered society requires strong leadership. Clerics of order believe in obedience and the rule of the wise.

**Class:** [[Cleric]] | **Source:** TCoE | **Subclass Features at:** 1st, 2nd, 6th, 8th, 17th level

## Key Features
- **Bonus Proficiencies (1st):** You gain proficiency with heavy armor and your choice of [[Intimidation]] or [[Persuasion]].
- **Voice of Authority (1st):** When you cast a spell ([[1st Level|1st level]] or higher) targeting an ally, that ally can use their reaction to make one weapon attack against a creature you can see.
- **Channel Divinity: Order's Demand (2nd):** As an action, each creature of your choice within 30 feet must make a Wisdom save or be charmed by you until the end of your next turn or until they take damage. You can also force them to drop what they're holding.
- **Embodiment of the Law (6th):** When you cast an enchantment spell of [[1st Level|1st level]] or higher using a spell slot, you can change the casting time to 1 bonus action (if it was 1 action). Usable a number of times equal to your Wisdom modifier per long rest.
- **Divine Strike (8th):** Once per turn, deal an extra 1d8 psychic damage with a weapon attack (2d8 at 14th level).
- **Order's Wrath (17th):** When you use Divine Strike to deal psychic damage, the cursed target takes an extra 2d8 psychic damage the next time an ally hits it with an attack on each of the ally's turns.
