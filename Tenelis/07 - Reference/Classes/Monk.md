# Monk

A master of martial arts, harnessing the power of the body in pursuit of physical and spiritual perfection. Monks use Ki points to fuel special abilities like Flurry of Blows, Patient Defense, and Step of the Wind, and their Unarmored Defense lets them fight without armor.
