# Oath of the Watchers

The Oath of the Watchers binds paladins to protect the mortal realm from the predations of extraplanar creatures. Paladins who follow this oath are ever vigilant against otherworldly threats.

**Class:** [[Paladin]] | **Source:** TCoE | **Subclass Features at:** 3rd, 7th, 15th, 20th level

## Key Features
- **Oath Spells (3rd):** You gain oath spells at the listed paladin levels: alarm and [[Detect Magic|detect magic]] (3rd), moonbeam and [[See Invisibility|see invisibility]] (5th), counterspell and nondetection (9th), [[Aura of Purity|aura of purity]] and banishment (13th), [[Hold Monster|hold monster]] and scrying (17th).
- **Channel Divinity: Watcher's Will (3rd):** As an action, choose a number of creatures you can see within 30 feet (up to your Charisma modifier). For 1 minute, each chosen creature has advantage on Intelligence, Wisdom, and Charisma saving throws.
- **Channel Divinity: Abjure the Extraplanar (3rd):** As an action, each aberration, celestial, elemental, fey, or fiend within 30 feet must make a Wisdom save or be turned for 1 minute (or until it takes damage).
- **Aura of the Sentinel (7th):** You and allies within 10 feet of you add your proficiency bonus to initiative rolls. At 18th level, the aura extends to 30 feet.
- **Vigilant Rebuke (15th):** When you or a creature within 30 feet succeeds on an Intelligence, Wisdom, or Charisma saving throw, you can use your reaction to deal 2d8 + your Charisma modifier force damage to the creature that forced the save.
- **Mortal Bulwark (20th):** As a bonus action, for 1 minute you gain: truesight out to 120 feet, advantage on attack rolls against aberrations, celestials, elementals, fey, and fiends, and when you hit such a creature, you can force it to make a Charisma save or be banished to its native plane (as banishment). Usable once per long rest (or for a 5th-level spell slot).
