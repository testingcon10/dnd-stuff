# Oath of the Ancients

The Oath of the Ancients is as old as the race of elves and the rituals of the druids. Paladins who swear this oath cast their lot with the side of light in the cosmic struggle against darkness, loving the beautiful and life-giving things of the world more than any principle of honor or courage.

## Key Features
- **Channel Divinity: Nature's Wrath (3rd):** Use your Channel Divinity to cause spectral vines to restrain a creature within 10 feet. The target must succeed on a STR or DEX save or be restrained until freed or until you lose concentration.
- **Channel Divinity: Turn the Faithless (3rd):** Use your Channel Divinity to turn fey and fiends within 30 feet, causing them to flee for 1 minute on a failed WIS save.
- **Aura of Warding (7th):** You and friendly creatures within 10 feet have resistance to damage from spells. At 18th level, the range increases to 30 feet.
- **Undying Sentinel (15th):** When you are reduced to 0 hit points and not killed outright, you can choose to drop to 1 hit point instead. Once you use this ability, you can't use it again until you finish a long rest. Additionally, you suffer none of the drawbacks of old age and can't be aged magically.
- **Elder Champion (20th):** You can assume the form of an ancient force of nature for 1 minute, regaining 10 HP at the start of each turn, casting paladin spells as a bonus action, and causing enemy creatures within 10 feet to have disadvantage on saves against your paladin spells and Channel Divinity.
