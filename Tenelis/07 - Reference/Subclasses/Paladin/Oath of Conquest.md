# Oath of Conquest

The Oath of Conquest paladin seeks to crush chaos and evil beneath an iron fist, ruling through fear and strength to impose order on the world.

**Class:** [[Paladin]] | **Source:** XGtE | **Subclass Features at:** 3rd, 7th, 15th, 20th level

## Key Features
- **Oath Spells (3rd):** You gain oath spells at the listed paladin levels: [[Armor of Agathys|armor of agathys]] and command (3rd), [[Hold Person|hold person]] and [[Spiritual Weapon|spiritual weapon]] (5th), [[Bestow Curse|bestow curse]] and fear (9th), [[Dominate Beast|dominate beast]] and stoneskin (13th), cloudkill and [[Dominate Person|dominate person]] (17th).
- **Channel Divinity: Conquering Presence (3rd):** As an action, each creature of your choice within 30 feet must make a WIS save or become frightened of you for 1 minute (save at end of each turn to end).
- **Channel Divinity: Guided Strike (3rd):** When you make an attack roll, you can add +10 to the roll. You make this choice after seeing the roll but before knowing if it hits.
- **Aura of Conquest (7th):** Creatures frightened of you within 10 feet (20 feet at 18th level) have their speed reduced to 0 and take psychic damage equal to half your paladin level at the start of their turns.
- **Scornful Rebuke (15th):** Whenever a creature hits you with an attack, it takes psychic damage equal to your CHA modifier (minimum 1) if you are not incapacitated.
- **Invincible Conqueror (20th):** As an action, you transform for 1 minute: you have resistance to all damage, you can make an additional attack as part of the Attack action, and your melee weapon attacks score a critical hit on a 19 or 20. Once per long rest.
