# Path of Wild Magic

Many places in the multiverse abound with beauty, intense emotion, and rampant magic. Barbarians exposed to this environment may walk the Path of [[Wild Magic]], feeling the magic that infuses them and erupting in uncontrollable surges when they rage.

**Class:** [[Barbarian]] | **Source:** TCoE | **Subclass Features at:** 3rd, 6th, 10th, 14th level

## Key Features
- **Magic Awareness (3rd):** As an action, you can detect the presence of spells and magic items within 60 feet until the end of your next turn. This functions like [[Detect Magic|detect magic]] but is not itself magical. You can use this a number of times equal to your proficiency bonus per long rest.
- **Wild Surge (3rd):** When you enter your rage, roll on the [[Wild Magic]] table (d8) for a random magical effect, such as summoning a spirit, teleporting, creating an aura of magical energy, or releasing a bolt of light.
- **Bolstering Magic (6th):** As a bonus action, touch a creature to grant one of two benefits: add a d3 to their attack rolls and ability checks for 10 minutes, or roll a d3 and restore an expended spell slot of that level or lower. Usable proficiency bonus times per long rest.
- **Unstable Backlash (10th):** When you take damage or fail a saving throw while raging, you can use your reaction to roll on the Wild Surge table, replacing your current Wild Surge effect with the new one.
- **Controlled Surge (14th):** When you roll on the Wild Surge table, you can roll twice and choose which effect to use.
