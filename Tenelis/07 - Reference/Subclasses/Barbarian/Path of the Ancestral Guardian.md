# Path of the Ancestral Guardian

The Path of the Ancestral Guardian calls upon the spirits of honored ancestors to shield allies and punish enemies who dare to ignore the barbarian on the battlefield.

**Class:** [[Barbarian]] | **Source:** XGtE | **Subclass Features at:** 3rd, 6th, 10th, 14th level

## Key Features
- **Ancestral Protectors (3rd):** While raging, the first creature you hit with an attack on your turn has disadvantage on attack rolls against targets other than you, and those targets have resistance to the damage it deals, until your next turn.
- **Spirit Shield (6th):** As a reaction, when a creature you can see within 30 feet takes damage, you can reduce that damage by 2d6 (increases to 3d6 at 10th, 4d6 at 14th). Requires you to be raging.
- **Consult the Spirits (10th):** You can cast clairvoyance as a ritual, and you can cast augury without material components. You use Wisdom as your spellcasting ability for these spells.
- **Vengeful Ancestors (14th):** When you use Spirit [[Shield]] to reduce damage, the attacker takes an amount of force damage equal to the damage your Spirit [[Shield]] prevented.
