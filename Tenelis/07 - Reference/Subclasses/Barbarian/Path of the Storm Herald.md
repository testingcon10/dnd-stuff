# Path of the Storm Herald

The Path of the Storm Herald barbarian channels the primal magic of the storm, emanating a powerful aura that mirrors the fury of the natural world.

**Class:** [[Barbarian]] | **Source:** XGtE | **Subclass Features at:** 3rd, 6th, 10th, 14th level

## Key Features
- **Storm Aura (3rd):** While raging, you emanate a 10-foot aura. Choose an environment (Desert, Sea, or Tundra) that determines the effect: Desert deals fire damage to nearby enemies, Sea lets you deal lightning damage to one creature, Tundra grants temporary hit points to allies. You can change your environment choice on level up.
- **Storm Soul (6th):** You gain resistance to fire (Desert), lightning (Sea), or cold (Tundra) damage based on your environment. Desert lets you set things on fire, Sea lets you breathe underwater and gain a swim speed, Tundra lets you freeze water.
- **Shielding Storm (10th):** Creatures of your choice in your Storm Aura gain the same damage resistance that your Storm Soul grants you.
- **Raging Storm (14th):** Enhanced aura effect based on environment: Desert forces a DEX save or catch fire, Sea forces a STR save or be knocked prone when you hit, Tundra reduces speed to 0 on a failed STR save when aura damage is dealt.
