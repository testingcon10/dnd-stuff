# Clockwork Soul

The cosmic force of order has suffused a Clockwork Soul sorcerer with magic. This power arises from Mechanus or a similar realm of clockwork law, granting the sorcerer the ability to restore balance and impose order.

**Class:** [[Sorcerer]] | **Source:** TCoE | **Subclass Features at:** 1st, 6th, 14th, 18th level

## Key Features
- **Clockwork Magic (1st):** You learn additional spells that don't count against your spells known: alarm and [[Protection from Evil and Good|protection from evil and good]] (1st), aid and [[Lesser Restoration|lesser restoration]] (3rd), [[Dispel Magic|dispel magic]] and [[Protection from Energy|protection from energy]] (5th), [[Freedom of Movement|freedom of movement]] and summon construct (7th), [[Greater Restoration|greater restoration]] and [[Wall of Force|wall of force]] (9th). When you gain a sorcerer level, you can swap one of these for an abjuration or transmutation spell of the same level from the sorcerer, warlock, or wizard list.
- **Restore Balance (1st):** When a creature you can see within 60 feet is about to make a d20 roll with advantage or disadvantage, you can use your reaction to prevent the advantage or disadvantage. Usable proficiency bonus times per long rest.
- **Bastion of Law (6th):** As an action, spend 1 to 5 sorcery points to create a ward on yourself or a creature you touch. The ward has a number of d8s equal to the sorcery points spent. When the warded creature takes damage, it can spend any number of those dice, rolling them and reducing the damage by the total. The ward lasts until you finish a long rest or use this feature again.
- **Trance of Order (14th):** As a bonus action, enter a state of clockwork order for 1 minute. Attack rolls against you can't benefit from advantage, and whenever you make an attack roll, ability check, or saving throw, you can treat a d20 roll of 9 or lower as a 10. Usable once per long rest (or for 7 sorcery points).
- **Clockwork Cavalcade (18th):** As an action, summon spirits of order in a 30-foot cube originating from you. Each creature of your choice in the area gains one benefit: restore 100 HP, end all conditions from certain spell effects, or repair all damage to objects and constructs. Usable once per long rest (or for 7 sorcery points).
