# Way of the Open Hand

Monks of the Way of the Open Hand are the ultimate masters of martial arts combat, whether armed or unarmed. They learn techniques to push and trip their foes, manipulate ki to heal damage to their bodies, and practice advanced meditation that can protect them from harm.

**Class:** [[Monk]] | **Source:** PHB | **Subclass Features at:** 3rd, 6th, 11th, 17th level

## Key Features
- **Open Hand Technique (3rd):** Whenever you hit a creature with one of the attacks granted by your Flurry of Blows, you can impose one of the following effects: it must succeed on a DEX save or be knocked prone; it must make a STR save or be pushed up to 15 feet away from you; or it can't take reactions until the end of your next turn.
- **Wholeness of Body (6th):** As an action, you can regain hit points equal to three times your monk level. You must finish a long rest before you can use this feature again.
- **Tranquility (11th):** At the end of a long rest, you gain the effect of a *sanctuary* spell that lasts until the start of your next long rest (the spell can end early as normal). The saving throw DC equals 8 + your Wisdom modifier + your proficiency bonus.
- **Quivering Palm (17th):** When you hit a creature with an unarmed strike, you can spend 3 ki points to start imperceptible vibrations in the creature's body, which last for a number of days equal to your monk level. You can then use an action to end the vibrations, forcing the creature to make a CON save. On a failure, the creature is reduced to 0 hit points. On a success, the creature takes 10d10 necrotic damage.
