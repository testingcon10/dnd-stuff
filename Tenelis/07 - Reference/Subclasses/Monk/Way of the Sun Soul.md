# Way of the Sun Soul

The Way of the Sun Soul monk channels their inner light into searing bolts of radiant energy, transforming ki into devastating ranged attacks and protective radiance.

**Class:** [[Monk]] | **Source:** XGtE | **Subclass Features at:** 3rd, 6th, 11th, 17th level

## Key Features
- **Radiant Sun Bolt (3rd):** You gain a ranged spell attack with a range of 30 feet that deals 1d4 + DEX modifier radiant damage. You can use this in place of any attack in your Attack action. When you use the Attack action with this bolt, you can spend 1 ki point to make two additional bolts as a bonus action. The damage die scales with your Martial Arts die.
- **Searing Arc Strike (6th):** After you take the Attack action on your turn, you can spend 2 ki points to cast [[Burning Hands|burning hands]] as a bonus action. You can spend additional ki points to cast it at a higher level (1 ki per additional spell level, max additional ki equal to half monk level).
- **Searing Sunburst (11th):** As an action, you create a 20-foot-radius sphere of light at a point within 150 feet. Each creature in the area must make a CON save or take 2d6 radiant damage. You can spend 1 to 3 ki points to deal an additional 2d6 per ki point spent.
- **Sun Shield (17th):** You shed bright light in a 30-foot radius and dim light for an additional 30 feet (toggled as a bonus action). When a creature hits you with a melee attack while the light shines, you can deal radiant damage to it equal to 5 + your WIS modifier.
