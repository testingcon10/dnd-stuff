# Way of the Four Elements

Monks who follow the Way of the Four Elements learn to harness the power of the elements, channeling their ki to produce magical effects mimicking elemental spells. Some members of this tradition dedicate themselves to a single element, but most weave the elements together.

**Class:** [[Monk]] | **Source:** PHB | **Subclass Features at:** 3rd, 6th, 11th, 17th level

## Key Features
- **Disciple of the Elements (3rd):** You learn elemental disciplines, which are magical options that allow you to spend ki to produce spell-like effects. You learn two disciplines at 3rd level and gain additional disciplines at 6th, 11th, and 17th level. You can also replace one discipline you know when you gain a new one. Some disciplines require a minimum monk level.
- **Elemental Attunement (3rd):** Available at 3rd level, this discipline allows you to briefly manipulate elemental forces without spending ki, creating minor harmless sensory effects, lighting or snuffing small flames, chilling or warming material, or causing earth or stone to shape.

## Notable Elemental Disciplines
- **Fangs of the Fire Snake (3rd):** Spend 1 ki; your unarmed strikes gain 10-foot reach and deal fire damage for the turn, plus an extra 1d10 fire damage on a hit.
- **Fist of Four Thunders (3rd):** Spend 2 ki to cast *thunderwave*.
- **Fist of Unbroken Air (3rd):** Spend 2 ki to create a blast of compressed air (3d10 bludgeoning, push 20 feet, knock prone on failed STR save).
- **Sweeping Cinder Strike (3rd):** Spend 2 ki to cast *[[Burning Hands|burning hands]]*.
- **Water Whip (3rd):** Spend 2 ki to create a whip of water (3d10 bludgeoning, pull 25 feet or knock prone on failed DEX save).
- **Clench of the North Wind (6th):** Spend 3 ki to cast *[[Hold Person|hold person]]*.
- **Gong of the Summit (6th):** Spend 3 ki to cast *shatter*.
- **Flames of the Phoenix (11th):** Spend 4 ki to cast *fireball*.
- **Ride the Wind (11th):** Spend 4 ki to cast *fly* on yourself.
- **River of Hungry Flame (17th):** Spend 5 ki to cast *[[Wall of Fire|wall of fire]]*.
- **Breath of Winter (17th):** Spend 6 ki to cast *[[Cone of Cold|cone of cold]]*.
- **Wave of Rolling Earth (17th):** Spend 6 ki to cast *[[Wall of Stone|wall of stone]]*.

*You can spend additional ki points to cast discipline spells at a higher level (1 ki per level, up to a maximum equal to your proficiency bonus).*
