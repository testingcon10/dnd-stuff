# Way of Shadow

Monks of the Way of Shadow follow a tradition that values stealth and subterfuge. These monks serve as spies and assassins, though the discipline of their order demands they use their shadow arts in service of their ideals.

**Class:** [[Monk]] | **Source:** PHB | **Subclass Features at:** 3rd, 6th, 11th, 17th level

## Key Features
- **Shadow Arts (3rd):** You can spend 2 ki points to cast *darkness*, *darkvision*, *[[Pass Without Trace|pass without trace]]*, or *silence* without providing material components. You also learn the *[[Minor Illusion|minor illusion]]* cantrip if you don't already know it.
- **Shadow Step (6th):** When you are in dim light or darkness, as a bonus action you can teleport up to 60 feet to an unoccupied space you can see that is also in dim light or darkness. You then have advantage on the first melee attack you make before the end of the turn.
- **Cloak of Shadows (11th):** You can use your action to become invisible in an area of dim light or darkness. You remain invisible until you make an attack, cast a spell, or are in an area of bright light.
- **Opportunist (17th):** When a creature within 5 feet of you is hit by an attack made by a creature other than you, you can use your reaction to make a melee attack against that creature.
