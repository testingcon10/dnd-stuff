# College of Whispers

The College of Whispers bard uses knowledge as a weapon, appearing to be an ordinary entertainer while secretly wielding fear, manipulation, and stolen identities.

**Class:** [[Bard]] | **Source:** XGtE | **Subclass Features at:** 3rd, 6th, 14th level

## Key Features
- **Psychic Blades (3rd):** When you hit a creature with a weapon attack, you can expend one Bardic Inspiration die to deal extra psychic damage equal to the die roll. Scales from 2d6 at 3rd to 8d6 at 15th level. Once per turn.
- **Words of Terror (3rd):** After speaking privately with a humanoid for at least 1 minute, it must succeed on a WIS save or be frightened of you or a creature of your choice for 1 hour. The target doesn't know you used magic. Once per short rest.
- **Mantle of Whispers (6th):** When a humanoid dies within 30 feet of you, you can capture its shadow using your reaction. You can then magically disguise yourself as that creature, gaining access to surface-level information like its name, general memories, and mannerisms. Lasts for 1 hour or until you dismiss it. Once per short rest.
- **Shadow Lore (14th):** You magically whisper to a creature within 30 feet, which must succeed on a WIS save or be charmed by you for 8 hours. It believes you know its darkest secret and obeys you out of fear. Once per long rest.
