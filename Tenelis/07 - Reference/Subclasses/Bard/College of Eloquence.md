# College of Eloquence

Adherents of the College of Eloquence master the art of oratory. They use a blend of logic and theatrical wordplay to convince even the most skeptical audiences.

**Class:** [[Bard]] | **Source:** TCoE | **Subclass Features at:** 3rd, 6th, 14th level

## Key Features
- **Silver Tongue (3rd):** When you make a Charisma (Persuasion) or Charisma (Deception) check, you can treat a d20 roll of 9 or lower as a 10.
- **Unsettling Words (3rd):** As a bonus action, expend one use of Bardic Inspiration and choose one creature within 60 feet. The creature subtracts the Bardic Inspiration die from its next saving throw before the start of your next turn.
- **Universal Speech (6th):** As an action, choose one or more creatures (up to your Charisma modifier) within 60 feet. They magically understand you for 1 hour regardless of language. Usable once per long rest (or for a spell slot).
- **Unfailing Inspiration (6th):** When a creature uses your Bardic Inspiration die and the roll fails, the Inspiration die is not expended and the creature keeps it.
- **Infectious Inspiration (14th):** When a creature within 60 feet uses your Bardic Inspiration die and succeeds, you can use your reaction to give a Bardic Inspiration die to a different creature within 60 feet without expending a use. Usable a number of times equal to your Charisma modifier per long rest.
