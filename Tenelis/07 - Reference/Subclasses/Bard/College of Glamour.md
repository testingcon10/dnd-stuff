# College of Glamour

The College of Glamour bard has learned to weave fey magic into their performances, commanding admiration and bending the emotions of their audience.

**Class:** [[Bard]] | **Source:** XGtE | **Subclass Features at:** 3rd, 6th, 14th level

## Key Features
- **Mantle of Inspiration (3rd):** As a bonus action, expend one Bardic Inspiration die to grant up to 5 creatures within 60 feet temporary hit points equal to the roll + CHA modifier. Each creature can also immediately use its reaction to move up to its speed without provoking opportunity attacks.
- **Enthralling Performance (3rd):** After performing for at least 1 minute, choose up to a number of humanoids equal to your CHA modifier within 60 feet. Each must succeed on a WIS save or be charmed for 1 hour, regarding you as a trusted friend. Once per short rest.
- **Mantle of Majesty (6th):** As a bonus action, you take on an unearthly appearance for 1 minute, and can cast command as a bonus action on each turn without expending a spell slot. Creatures charmed by you automatically fail the save. Once per long rest.
- **Unbreakable Majesty (14th):** As a bonus action, you gain a magisterial presence for 1 minute. Any creature that attacks you or targets you with a damaging spell must first make a CHA save; on a failure, it must choose a different target or waste the attack/spell. Once per short rest.
