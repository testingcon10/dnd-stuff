# College of Lore

Bards of the College of Lore know something about most things, collecting bits of knowledge from sources as diverse as scholarly tomes and peasant tales. They use their gifts to hold audiences spellbound and to undermine their foes.

**Class:** [[Bard]] | **Source:** PHB | **Subclass Features at:** 3rd, 6th, 14th level

## Key Features
- **Bonus Proficiencies (3rd):** You gain proficiency with three skills of your choice.
- **Cutting Words (3rd):** When a creature you can see within 60 feet makes an attack roll, ability check, or damage roll, you can use your reaction to expend a Bardic Inspiration die and subtract the number rolled from the creature's roll.
- **Additional Magical Secrets (6th):** You learn two spells of your choice from any class's spell list. These spells count as bard spells for you. This is in addition to the Magical Secrets feature at 10th level.
- **Peerless Skill (14th):** When you make an ability check, you can expend one use of Bardic Inspiration and add the die roll to your ability check. You can do so after rolling but before knowing whether you succeed or fail.
