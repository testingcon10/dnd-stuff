# Forge Domain

The Forge Domain cleric serves deities of smithing and creation, channeling divine power into the crafting of weapons, armor, and other works of metal.

**Class:** [[Cleric]] | **Source:** XGtE | **Subclass Features at:** 1st, 2nd, 6th, 8th, 17th level

## Key Features
- **Bonus Proficiencies (1st):** You gain proficiency with heavy armor and smith's tools.
- **Blessing of the Forge (1st):** At the end of a long rest, you can imbue one nonmagical suit of armor or weapon with a +1 bonus until your next long rest or until you use this feature again.
- **Channel Divinity: Artisan's Blessing (2nd):** You conduct a ritual to create a nonmagical metal item (weapon, armor, up to 10 items, or a single object worth no more than 100 gp) by placing metal of equal value on your holy symbol during a short rest.
- **Soul of the Forge (6th):** You gain +1 AC while wearing heavy armor and resistance to fire damage.
- **Divine Strike (8th):** Once per turn, you can deal an extra 1d8 fire damage with a weapon attack. Increases to 2d8 at 14th level.
- **Saint of Forge and Fire (17th):** You gain immunity to fire damage. While wearing heavy armor, you have resistance to bludgeoning, piercing, and slashing damage from nonmagical attacks.
