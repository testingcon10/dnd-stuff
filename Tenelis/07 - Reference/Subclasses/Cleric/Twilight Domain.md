# Twilight Domain

The Twilight Domain governs the transition and blending of light into darkness. Clerics of this domain bring comfort to those who seek rest and protect them from the horrors of the night.

**Class:** [[Cleric]] | **Source:** TCoE | **Subclass Features at:** 1st, 2nd, 6th, 8th, 17th level

## Key Features
- **Bonus Proficiencies (1st):** You gain proficiency with heavy armor and martial weapons.
- **Eyes of Night (1st):** You have darkvision out to 300 feet. As an action, you can share this darkvision with a number of willing creatures equal to your Wisdom modifier within 10 feet. Shared darkvision lasts 1 hour. Usable once per long rest (or for a spell slot).
- **Vigilant Blessing (1st):** As an action, give one creature (including yourself) advantage on the next initiative roll it makes. The benefit ends after the roll or if you use this feature again.
- **Channel Divinity: Twilight Sanctuary (2nd):** As an action, create a 30-foot radius sphere of twilight centered on you for 1 minute. At the end of each of your turns, you and each creature you choose in the sphere gains either 1d6 + cleric level temporary HP or the end of one effect causing charmed or frightened.
- **Steps of Night (6th):** As a bonus action when you are in dim light or darkness, you gain a flying speed equal to your walking speed for 1 minute. Usable proficiency bonus times per long rest.
- **Divine Strike (8th):** Once per turn, deal an extra 1d8 radiant damage with a weapon attack (2d8 at 14th level).
- **Twilight Shroud (17th):** You and your allies in your Twilight [[Sanctuary]] have half cover (granting +2 to AC and Dexterity saving throws).
