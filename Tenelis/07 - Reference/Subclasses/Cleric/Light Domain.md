# Light Domain

Gods of light promote the ideals of rebirth and renewal, truth, vigilance, and beauty. Clerics of a god of light are enlightened souls infused with radiance and the power of their gods' discerning vision.

## Key Features
- **Bonus Cantrip (1st):** You gain the *light* cantrip if you don't already know it. It doesn't count against your number of cantrips known.
- **Warding Flare (1st):** When you are attacked by a creature within 30 feet that you can see, you can use your reaction to impose disadvantage on the attack roll. You can use this feature a number of times equal to your Wisdom modifier.
- **Channel Divinity: Radiance of the Dawn (2nd):** As an action, you dispel any magical darkness within 30 feet. Each hostile creature within 30 feet must make a CON save, taking 2d10 + cleric level radiant damage on a failure, or half on a success.
- **Improved Flare (6th):** You can use your Warding Flare feature when a creature you can see within 30 feet attacks a creature other than you.
- **Potent Spellcasting (8th):** You add your Wisdom modifier to the damage you deal with any cleric cantrip.
- **Corona of Light (17th):** As an action, you activate an aura of sunlight that lasts for 1 minute. You emit bright light in a 60-foot radius and dim light 30 feet beyond that. Enemies in the bright light have disadvantage on saving throws against spells that deal fire or radiant damage.
