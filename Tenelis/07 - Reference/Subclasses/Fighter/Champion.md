# Champion

The Champion archetype focuses on raw physical power refined to deadly perfection. Champions are rewarded with increased critical hit range and remarkable athleticism.

**Class:** [[Fighter]] | **Source:** PHB | **Subclass Features at:** 3rd, 7th, 10th, 15th, 18th level

## Key Features
- **Improved Critical (3rd):** Weapon attacks score a critical hit on a 19 or 20.
- **Remarkable Athlete (7th):** Add half proficiency bonus (rounded up) to STR, DEX, and CON checks you aren't proficient in. Running long jump distance increases by your Strength modifier in feet.
- **Additional Fighting Style (10th):** Choose a second Fighting Style option.
- **Superior Critical (15th):** Weapon attacks score a critical hit on an 18, 19, or 20.
- **Survivor (18th):** At the start of each of your turns, you regain hit points equal to 5 + your Constitution modifier if you have no more than half your hit points and more than 0 HP.
