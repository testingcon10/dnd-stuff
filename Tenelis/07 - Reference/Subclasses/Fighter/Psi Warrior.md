# Psi Warrior

Psi Warriors are fighters who augment their physical might with psi-infused weapon strikes, telekinetic lashes, and barriers of mental force. They harness psionic energy to devastating effect on the battlefield.

**Class:** [[Fighter]] | **Source:** TCoE | **Subclass Features at:** 3rd, 7th, 10th, 15th, 18th level

## Key Features
- **Psionic Power (3rd):** You gain a number of Psionic Energy dice (d6) equal to twice your proficiency bonus. These fuel your psionic abilities and recharge on a long rest (one die recoverable as a bonus action per short rest).
- **Protective Field (3rd):** When you or a creature within 30 feet takes damage, use your reaction to expend a Psionic Energy die and reduce the damage by the roll + your Intelligence modifier.
- **Psionic Strike (3rd):** Once per turn when you hit with a weapon, expend a Psionic Energy die to deal extra force damage equal to the die roll + your Intelligence modifier.
- **Telekinetic Movement (3rd):** As an action, move a Large or smaller loose object or willing creature up to 30 feet to an unoccupied space you can see. If it's a Tiny object, you can move it to or from your hand. Usable free once per short rest, or by expending a Psionic Energy die.
- **Telekinetic Adept (7th):** Psi-Powered Leap: as a bonus action, gain a fly speed equal to twice your walking speed until end of turn. Telekinetic Thrust: when you deal damage with Psionic Strike, force a Strength save or knock the target prone or push it up to 10 feet.
- **Guarded Mind (10th):** You gain resistance to psychic damage. If you start your turn charmed or frightened, you can expend a Psionic Energy die to end every effect causing those conditions.
- **Bulwark of Force (15th):** As a bonus action, choose a number of creatures up to your Intelligence modifier within 30 feet (including yourself). Each gains half cover (+2 AC and Dexterity saves) for 1 minute. Usable free once per long rest, or by expending a Psionic Energy die.
- **Telekinetic Master (18th):** You can cast telekinesis, requiring no components, and your spellcasting ability is Intelligence. Usable free once per long rest, or by expending a Psionic Energy die. While concentrating on telekinesis, you can make one weapon attack as a bonus action.
