# Rune Knight

Rune Knights enhance their martial prowess using the supernatural power of runes. They learn to inscribe magical runes into their equipment, unlocking ancient giant magic with each engraving.

**Class:** [[Fighter]] | **Source:** TCoE | **Subclass Features at:** 3rd, 7th, 10th, 15th, 18th level

## Key Features
- **Bonus Proficiencies (3rd):** You gain proficiency with smith's tools.
- **Rune Carver (3rd):** You learn 2 runes (3 at 7th, 4 at 10th, 5 at 15th). Each rune has a passive benefit and an activated effect (once per short/long rest). Cloud Rune: advantage on Deception and Sleight of Hand; activate to redirect an attack to a different creature. Fire Rune: double proficiency with tools; activate to restrain a creature and deal 2d6 fire per turn. Frost Rune: advantage on Animal Handling and Intimidation; activate to incapacitate a creature for 1 turn (Strength save). Stone Rune: darkvision 120ft and advantage on Insight; activate to charm a creature into an incapacitated stupor (Wisdom save). Hill Rune (7th+): resistance to poison damage; activate to gain resistance to bludgeoning, piercing, and slashing damage for 1 minute. Storm Rune (7th+): advantage on Arcana checks and can't be surprised; activate for 1 minute of using a reaction to grant advantage or disadvantage on an attack/save/ability check within 60ft.
- **Giant's Might (3rd):** As a bonus action, become Large for 1 minute (if space allows), gain advantage on Strength checks and saves, and deal an extra 1d6 damage once per turn on a weapon attack. Usable proficiency bonus times per long rest.
- **Runic Shield (7th):** When a creature you can see within 60 feet is hit by an attack, use your reaction to force the attacker to reroll and use the new roll.
- **Great Stature (10th):** Your height increases by 3d4 inches. Additionally, Giant's Might extra damage increases to 1d8.
- **Master of Runes (15th):** You can invoke each rune you know twice per short or long rest instead of once.
- **Runic Juggernaut (18th):** Giant's Might can make you Huge (if space allows), and the extra damage increases to 1d10.
