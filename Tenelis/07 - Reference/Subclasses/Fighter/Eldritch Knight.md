# Eldritch Knight

The Eldritch Knight archetype combines the martial mastery common to all fighters with a careful study of magic. Eldritch Knights use magical techniques similar to those practiced by wizards, focusing on the schools of abjuration and evocation.

**Class:** [[Fighter]] | **Source:** PHB | **Subclass Features at:** 3rd, 7th, 10th, 15th, 18th level

## Key Features
- **Spellcasting (3rd):** You learn cantrips and spells from the wizard spell list, using Intelligence as your spellcasting ability. Your spells must come from the abjuration or evocation schools, except at 3rd, 8th, 14th, and 20th level where you can choose from any school. You follow the one-third caster progression for spell slots.
- **Weapon Bond (3rd):** You can bond with up to two weapons over a 1-hour ritual each. You can't be disarmed of a bonded weapon unless incapacitated, and you can summon a bonded weapon to your hand as a bonus action if it is on the same plane of existence.
- **War Magic (7th):** When you use your action to cast a cantrip, you can make one weapon attack as a bonus action.
- **Eldritch Strike (10th):** When you hit a creature with a weapon attack, that creature has disadvantage on its next saving throw against a spell you cast before the end of your next turn.
- **Arcane Charge (15th):** When you use Action Surge, you can teleport up to 30 feet to an unoccupied space you can see. You can teleport before or after the additional action.
- **Improved War Magic (18th):** When you use your action to cast a spell, you can make one weapon attack as a bonus action.
