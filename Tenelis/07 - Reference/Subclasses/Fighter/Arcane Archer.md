# Arcane Archer

The Arcane Archer infuses arrows with powerful magic, choosing from a variety of arcane shots to control the battlefield and devastate foes at range.

**Class:** [[Fighter]] | **Source:** XGtE | **Subclass Features at:** 3rd, 7th, 10th, 15th, 18th level

## Key Features
- **Arcane Archer Lore (3rd):** You learn either the prestidigitation or druidcraft cantrip, and you gain proficiency in either Arcana or Nature.
- **Arcane Shot (3rd):** You learn two Arcane Shot options (from 8 total: Banishing, Beguiling, Bursting, Enfeebling, Grasping, Piercing, Seeking, Shadow Arrow). When you hit with a shortbow or longbow attack, you can apply one option for additional effects. Two uses per short or long rest. You learn additional options at 7th, 10th, 15th, and 18th level.
- **Magic Arrow (7th):** Whenever you fire a nonmagical arrow from a shortbow or longbow, you can make it magical for the purpose of overcoming resistance and immunity.
- **Curving Shot (7th):** When you miss with a magic arrow, you can use a bonus action to redirect it at a different target within 60 feet of the original, making a new attack roll.
- **Ever-Ready Shot (15th):** If you roll initiative and have no Arcane Shot uses remaining, you regain one use.
