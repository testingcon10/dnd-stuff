# School of Transmutation

You are a student of spells that modify energy and matter. To you, the world is not a fixed thing, but eminently mutable, and you delight in being an agent of change. You wield raw creation and learn to alter both physical forms and mental qualities.

**Class:** [[Wizard]] | **Source:** PHB | **Subclass Features at:** 2nd, 6th, 10th, 14th level

## Key Features
- **Transmutation Savant (2nd):** The gold and time you must spend to copy a transmutation spell into your spellbook is halved.
- **Minor Alchemy (2nd):** You can temporarily alter the physical properties of one nonmagical object, changing it from one substance to another. You perform a special alchemical procedure on one object composed entirely of wood, stone, iron, copper, or silver, transforming it into a different one of those materials. For each 10 minutes you spend performing the procedure, you can transform up to 1 cubic foot of material. The transformation lasts for 1 hour or until you lose concentration (as if concentrating on a spell).
- **Transmuter's Stone (6th):** You can spend 8 hours creating a transmuter's stone that stores transmutation magic. You can benefit from the stone yourself or give it to another creature. A creature gains a benefit of your choice as long as it possesses the stone. Choose from: darkvision out to 60 feet, an increase to speed of 10 feet, proficiency in CON saving throws, or resistance to acid, cold, fire, lightning, or thunder damage. Each time you cast a transmutation spell of 1st level or higher, you can change the stone's effect.
- **Shapechanger (10th):** You add the *polymorph* spell to your spellbook if it is not there already. You can cast *polymorph* without expending a spell slot. When you do so, you can target only yourself and transform into a beast whose challenge rating is 1 or lower. Once you cast *polymorph* in this way, you can't do so again until you finish a short or long rest.
- **Master Transmuter (14th):** You can use your action to consume the reserve of transmutation magic stored within your transmuter's stone in a single burst. You choose one of the following effects: *Major Transformation* (transmute one nonmagical object into another nonmagical object of similar size and mass, up to 5-foot cube), *Panacea* (remove all curses, diseases, and poisons and heal 1 creature for all its hit points), *Restore Life* (cast *[[Raise Dead|raise dead]]* without expending a spell slot or needing the spell in your spellbook), or *Restore Youth* (reduce a creature's apparent age by 3d10 years, minimum 13 years old). You must create a new transmuter's stone to use this feature again.
