# School of Conjuration

As a conjurer, you favor spells that produce objects and creatures out of thin air. You can conjure billowing clouds of killing fog or summon creatures from elsewhere to fight on your behalf. As your mastery grows, you learn spells of transportation and can teleport yourself across vast distances in the blink of an eye.

**Class:** [[Wizard]] | **Source:** PHB | **Subclass Features at:** 2nd, 6th, 10th, 14th level

## Key Features
- **Conjuration Savant (2nd):** The gold and time you must spend to copy a conjuration spell into your spellbook is halved.
- **Minor Conjuration (2nd):** As an action, you can conjure up an inanimate object in your hand or on the ground in an unoccupied space within 10 feet of you. The object can be no larger than 3 feet on a side and weigh no more than 10 pounds, and its form must be that of a nonmagical object you have seen. It is visibly magical, radiating dim light out to 5 feet. The object disappears after 1 hour, when you use this feature again, or if it takes or deals any damage.
- **Benign Transposition (6th):** As an action, you can teleport up to 30 feet to an unoccupied space you can see. Alternatively, you can choose a space within range that is occupied by a Small or Medium willing creature; you both teleport, swapping places. Once you use this feature, you can't use it again until you finish a long rest or you cast a conjuration spell of 1st level or higher.
- **Focused Conjuration (10th):** While you are concentrating on a conjuration spell, your concentration can't be broken as a result of taking damage.
- **Durable Summons (14th):** Any creature that you summon or create with a conjuration spell has 30 temporary hit points.
