# School of Necromancy

The School of Necromancy explores the cosmic forces of life, death, and undeath. As you focus your studies in this tradition, you learn to manipulate the energy that animates all living things. As you progress, you learn to sap the life force from a creature as your magic destroys its body.

**Class:** [[Wizard]] | **Source:** PHB | **Subclass Features at:** 2nd, 6th, 10th, 14th level

## Key Features
- **Necromancy Savant (2nd):** The gold and time you must spend to copy a necromancy spell into your spellbook is halved.
- **Grim Harvest (2nd):** Once per turn when you kill one or more creatures with a spell of 1st level or higher, you regain hit points equal to twice the spell's level, or three times its level if the spell belongs to the School of Necromancy. You don't gain this benefit for killing constructs or undead.
- **Undead Thralls (6th):** You add the *[[Animate Dead|animate dead]]* spell to your spellbook if it is not there already. When you cast *[[Animate Dead|animate dead]]*, you can target one additional corpse or pile of bones, creating another zombie or skeleton. Whenever you create an undead using a necromancy spell, it has additional benefits: its hit point maximum is increased by an amount equal to your wizard level, and it adds your proficiency bonus to its weapon damage rolls.
- **Inured to Undeath (10th):** You have resistance to necrotic damage, and your hit point maximum can't be reduced. You have spent so much time dealing with undead and the forces that animate them that you have become inured to some of their worst effects.
- **Command Undead (14th):** As an action, you can choose one undead that you can see within 60 feet. That creature must make a CHA saving throw against your wizard spell save DC. If it succeeds, you can't use this feature on it again. If it fails, it becomes friendly to you and obeys your commands until you use this feature again. Intelligent undead (INT 8 or higher) have advantage on the save, and those with INT 12 or higher can repeat the save at the end of every hour.
