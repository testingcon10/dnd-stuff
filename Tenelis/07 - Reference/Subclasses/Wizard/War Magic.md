# War Magic

The War Magic wizard blends evocation and abjuration into a deadly martial discipline, bolstering defenses with arcane power while maintaining an aggressive edge on the battlefield.

**Class:** [[Wizard]] | **Source:** XGtE | **Subclass Features at:** 2nd, 6th, 10th, 14th level

## Key Features
- **Arcane Deflection (2nd):** When you are hit by an attack or fail a saving throw, you can use your reaction to gain +2 to AC against that attack or +4 to that saving throw. When you use this feature, you can only cast cantrips (not leveled spells) until the end of your next turn.
- **Tactical Wit (2nd):** You add your INT modifier to initiative rolls.
- **Power Surge (6th):** You can store magical energy in yourself to boost your damaging spells. You start with a number of power surges equal to your INT modifier (minimum 1). You gain a surge when you successfully counterspell or [[Dispel Magic|dispel magic]], or at the end of a short rest if you have none. When you deal damage with a wizard spell, you can spend one power surge to deal extra force damage equal to half your wizard level to one target.
- **Durable Magic (10th):** While you are maintaining concentration on a spell, you gain +2 to AC and to all saving throws.
- **Deflecting Shroud (14th):** When you use Arcane Deflection, you can deal force damage equal to half your wizard level to up to three creatures of your choice within 60 feet of you.
