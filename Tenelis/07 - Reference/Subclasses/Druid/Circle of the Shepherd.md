# Circle of the Shepherd

The Circle of the Shepherd druid communes with the spirits of nature, summoning spectral totems and bolstering beast and fey allies with the power of the wild.

**Class:** [[Druid]] | **Source:** XGtE | **Subclass Features at:** 2nd, 6th, 10th, 14th level

## Key Features
- **Speech of the Woods (2nd):** You gain the ability to speak Sylvan. You also gain the ability to speak with beasts; they can understand your speech and you can decipher their noises and motions.
- **Spirit Totem (2nd):** As a bonus action, you summon an incorporeal spirit to a point within 60 feet that creates a 30-foot-radius aura for 1 minute. Choose Bear (temp HP equal to 5 + druid level to creatures in aura), Hawk (advantage on attack rolls against enemies in aura using your reaction to grant it), or Unicorn (when you cast a healing spell, each creature of your choice in aura also regains HP equal to your druid level). Once per short rest.
- **Mighty Summoner (6th):** Beasts and fey summoned or created by your spells gain +2 hit points per Hit Die and their natural weapon attacks count as magical.
- **Guardian Spirit (10th):** Beasts and fey you conjure with spells regain hit points equal to half your druid level at the end of each of their turns.
- **Faithful Summons (14th):** When you are reduced to 0 hit points or incapacitated, you can immediately gain the effect of [[Conjure Animals|conjure animals]] as if cast with a 9th-level spell slot, summoning four beasts of CR 2 or lower within 20 feet. They last for 1 hour. Once per long rest.
