# Circle of Stars

Druids of the Circle of Stars draw on the power of starlight to guide others and unleash cosmic power. They map the stars to unlock cosmic secrets.

**Class:** [[Druid]] | **Source:** TCoE | **Subclass Features at:** 2nd, 6th, 10th, 14th level

## Key Features
- **Star Map (2nd):** You learn guidance and [[Guiding Bolt|guiding bolt]]. You have a star map focus that grants free castings of [[Guiding Bolt|guiding bolt]].
- **Starry Form (2nd):** Expend Wild Shape to take a starry form. Choose Archer (ranged radiant attack as bonus action), Chalice (healing bonus when you cast healing spells), or Dragon (minimum 10 on concentration checks and Intelligence/Wisdom checks).
- **Cosmic Omen (6th):** Roll a die after long rest. Use reaction to add or subtract d6 from nearby creature's roll.
- **Twinkling Constellations (10th):** Change starry form each turn + improved constellation effects.
- **Full of Stars (14th):** Starry form grants resistance to bludgeoning, piercing, and slashing damage.
