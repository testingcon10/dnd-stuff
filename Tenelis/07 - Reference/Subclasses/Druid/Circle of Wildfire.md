# Circle of Wildfire

Druids of the Circle of Wildfire understand that destruction is sometimes the precursor to creation. These druids bond with a primal spirit that harbors both destructive and creative power, allowing them to burn away the old and bring forth the new.

**Class:** [[Druid]] | **Source:** TCoE | **Subclass Features at:** 2nd, 6th, 10th, 14th level

## Key Features
- **Circle Spells (2nd):** At certain druid levels, you gain circle spells: [[Burning Hands|burning hands]] and [[Cure Wounds|cure wounds]] (3rd), [[Flaming Sphere|flaming sphere]] and [[Scorching Ray|scorching ray]] (5th), [[Plant Growth|plant growth]] and revivify (7th), [[Aura of Life|aura of life]] and [[Fire Shield|fire shield]] (9th).
- **Summon Wildfire Spirit (2nd):** As an action, expend a use of Wild Shape to summon a wildfire spirit in an unoccupied space within 30 feet. Each creature within 10 feet of the spirit when it appears must make a Dexterity save or take 2d6 fire damage. The spirit obeys your commands, can teleport itself and allies, and deals fire damage.
- **Enhanced Bond (6th):** While your wildfire spirit is summoned, your fire damage spells and healing spells gain a +1d8 bonus to one damage or healing roll when cast through you or the spirit's space.
- **Cauterizing Flames (10th):** When a Small or larger creature dies within 30 feet of you or your wildfire spirit, you can use your reaction to cause spectral flames to appear. A creature of your choice within the flames regains HP equal to 2d10 + your Wisdom modifier, or must make a Dexterity save or take fire damage equal to the same amount.
- **Blazing Revival (14th):** If your wildfire spirit is within 120 feet of you when you drop to 0 HP, the spirit can drop to 0 HP instead. You then regain half your hit points and rise to your feet. Each creature within 30 feet of you takes 2d10 + your druid level fire damage (Dexterity save for half).
