# Circle of the Land

The Circle of the Land is made up of mystics and sages who safeguard ancient knowledge and rites through a vast oral tradition. Membership in this circle is open to any druid who has been initiated into the circle's secret knowledge.

**Class:** [[Druid]] | **Source:** PHB | **Subclass Features at:** 2nd, 6th, 10th, 14th level

## Circle Spells (by Land Type)
You choose a land type when you join this circle at 2nd level, gaining access to additional spells:
- **Arctic:** *[[Hold Person|hold person]], [[Spike Growth|spike growth]], [[Sleet Storm|sleet storm]], slow, [[Commune with Nature|commune with nature]], [[Cone of Cold|cone of cold]]*
- **Coast:** *[[Mirror Image|mirror image]], [[Misty Step|misty step]], [[Water Breathing|water breathing]], [[Water Walk|water walk]], [[Control Water|control water]], [[Freedom of Movement|freedom of movement]]*
- **Desert:** *blur, silence, [[Create Food and Water|create food and water]], [[Protection from Energy|protection from energy]], blight, [[Hallucinatory Terrain|hallucinatory terrain]]*
- **Forest:** *barkskin, [[Spider Climb|spider climb]], [[Call Lightning|call lightning]], [[Plant Growth|plant growth]], divination, [[Freedom of Movement|freedom of movement]]*
- **Grassland:** *invisibility, [[Pass Without Trace|pass without trace]], daylight, haste, divination, [[Freedom of Movement|freedom of movement]]*
- **Mountain:** *[[Spider Climb|spider climb]], [[Spike Growth|spike growth]], [[Lightning Bolt|lightning bolt]], [[Meld into Stone|meld into stone]], passwall, [[Stone Shape|stone shape]]*
- **Swamp:** *darkness, [[Melf's Acid Arrow|Melf's acid arrow]], [[Water Walk|water walk]], [[Stinking Cloud|stinking cloud]], [[Freedom of Movement|freedom of movement]], [[Locate Creature|locate creature]]*
- **Underdark:** *[[Spider Climb|spider climb]], web, [[Gaseous Form|gaseous form]], [[Stinking Cloud|stinking cloud]], cloudkill, [[Greater Invisibility|greater invisibility]]*

## Key Features
- **Bonus Cantrip (2nd):** You learn one additional druid cantrip of your choice.
- **Natural Recovery (2nd):** During a short rest, you can recover expended spell slots with a combined level equal to or less than half your druid level (rounded up). You can't recover slots of 6th level or higher. Once used, you can't use this feature again until you finish a long rest.
- **Circle Spells (2nd):** You gain access to circle spells connected to the land where you became a druid (see above). These are always prepared and don't count against the number of spells you can prepare.
- **Land's Stride (6th):** Moving through nonmagical difficult terrain costs you no extra movement. You can also pass through nonmagical plants without being slowed or taking damage. You also have advantage on saves against plants that are magically created or manipulated to impede movement.
- **Nature's Ward (10th):** You can't be charmed or frightened by elementals or fey, and you are immune to poison and disease.
- **Nature's Sanctuary (14th):** Creatures of the natural world sense your connection to nature and hesitate to attack you. When a beast or plant creature attacks you, it must make a WIS save against your druid spell save DC. On a failure, it must choose a different target or the attack misses. On a success, the creature is immune to this effect for 24 hours.
