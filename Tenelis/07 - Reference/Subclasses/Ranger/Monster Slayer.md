# Monster Slayer

The Monster Slayer ranger is trained to hunt and destroy supernatural threats, using specialized knowledge and techniques to exploit the weaknesses of their quarry.

**Class:** [[Ranger]] | **Source:** XGtE | **Subclass Features at:** 3rd, 7th, 11th, 15th level

## Key Features
- **Hunter's Sense (3rd):** As an action, choose a creature you can see within 60 feet. You learn whether it has any damage immunities, resistances, or vulnerabilities, and what they are. Alternatively, you learn if it has no such traits. Uses equal to WIS modifier per long rest.
- **Slayer's Prey (3rd):** As a bonus action, designate one creature you can see within 60 feet as your prey. The first time each turn that you hit your prey with a weapon attack, it takes an extra 1d6 damage. This lasts until you finish a short or long rest or designate a different creature.
- **Supernatural Defense (7th):** When your Slayer's Prey target forces you to make a saving throw, or when you make an ability check to escape its grapple, you add 1d6 to your roll.
- **Magic-User's Nemesis (11th):** When you see a creature within 60 feet casting a spell or teleporting, you can use your reaction to force a WIS save. On a failure, the creature's spell or teleport fails and is wasted. Once per short or long rest.
- **Slayer's Counter (15th):** If your Slayer's Prey target forces you to make a saving throw, you can use your reaction to make one weapon attack against it. If the attack hits, you automatically succeed on the saving throw (in addition to dealing damage).
