# Horizon Walker

The Horizon Walker ranger guards the world against threats from other planes, wielding planar magic to teleport across the battlefield and convert attacks into force damage.

**Class:** [[Ranger]] | **Source:** XGtE | **Subclass Features at:** 3rd, 7th, 11th, 15th level

## Key Features
- **Detect Portal (3rd):** As an action, you can detect the distance and direction to the closest planar portal within 1 mile. Once per short or long rest.
- **Planar Warrior (3rd):** As a bonus action, choose one creature you can see within 30 feet. The next time you hit that creature with a weapon attack on this turn, all of the attack's damage becomes force damage, and the creature takes an extra 1d8 force damage. The extra damage increases to 2d8 at 11th level.
- **Ethereal Step (7th):** As a bonus action, you can cast etherealness, but the spell ends at the end of your current turn. Once per short or long rest.
- **Distant Strike (11th):** When you take the Attack action, you can teleport up to 10 feet before each attack to an unoccupied space you can see. If you attack at least two different creatures with the action, you can make one additional attack against a third creature.
- **Spectral Defense (15th):** When you take damage from an attack, you can use your reaction to give yourself resistance to all of that attack's damage.
