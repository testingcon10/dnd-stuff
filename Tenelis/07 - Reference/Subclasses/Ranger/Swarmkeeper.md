# Swarmkeeper

A Swarmkeeper ranger has bonded with a swarm of nature spirits that aid them in battle and exploration. The swarm might be insects, birds, tiny twig blights, or pixies -- whatever suits your character's story.

**Class:** [[Ranger]] | **Source:** TCoE | **Subclass Features at:** 3rd, 7th, 11th, 15th level

## Key Features
- **Gathered Swarm (3rd):** Once per turn when you hit a creature with an attack, the swarm assists with one of three effects (your choice): move the target 15 feet horizontally (Strength save negates), move yourself 5 feet (no opportunity attack), or deal 1d6 piercing damage to the target.
- **Swarmkeeper Magic (3rd):** You learn additional spells that don't count against your spells known: [[Faerie Fire|faerie fire]] (3rd), web (5th), [[Gaseous Form|gaseous form]] (9th), [[Arcane Eye|arcane eye]] (13th), [[Insect Plague|insect plague]] (17th).
- **Writhing Tide (7th):** As a bonus action, you gain a flying speed of 10 feet for 1 minute (hovering, carried by the swarm). Usable proficiency bonus times per long rest.
- **Mighty Swarm (11th):** Your Gathered Swarm improves. Push: the creature must also succeed on a Strength save or be knocked prone. Move: you gain half cover until the start of your next turn. Damage: the extra damage increases to 1d8.
- **Swarming Dispersal (15th):** When you take damage, you can use your reaction to become a swarm. You gain resistance to the triggering damage, and you teleport up to 30 feet to an unoccupied space you can see (appearing with the swarm reforming around you). Usable proficiency bonus times per long rest.
