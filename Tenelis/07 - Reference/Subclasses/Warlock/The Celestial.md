# The Celestial

The Celestial warlock has forged a pact with a being of the Upper Planes, gaining access to healing light and radiant power normally reserved for the most devout of the divine casters.

**Class:** [[Warlock]] | **Source:** XGtE | **Subclass Features at:** 1st, 6th, 10th, 14th level

## Key Features
- **Expanded Spell List (1st):** You gain additional spell options: [[Cure Wounds|cure wounds]] and [[Guiding Bolt|guiding bolt]] (1st), [[Flaming Sphere|flaming sphere]] and [[Lesser Restoration|lesser restoration]] (2nd), daylight and revivify (3rd), [[Guardian of Faith|guardian of faith]] and [[Wall of Fire|wall of fire]] (4th), [[Flame Strike|flame strike]] and [[Greater Restoration|greater restoration]] (5th).
- **Bonus Cantrips (1st):** You learn the light and [[Sacred Flame|sacred flame]] cantrips, which count as warlock cantrips for you and don't count against your number of cantrips known.
- **Healing Light (1st):** You have a pool of d6s equal to 1 + your warlock level. As a bonus action, you can heal a creature you can see within 60 feet by spending dice from the pool (maximum number of dice equal to your CHA modifier per use). You regain all expended dice on a long rest.
- **Radiant Soul (6th):** You gain resistance to radiant damage. When you cast a spell that deals fire or radiant damage, you can add your CHA modifier to one damage roll of that spell against one of its targets.
- **Celestial Resilience (10th):** You gain temporary hit points equal to your warlock level + CHA modifier whenever you finish a short or long rest. Additionally, up to five creatures of your choice also gain temporary hit points equal to half your warlock level + your CHA modifier.
- **Searing Vengeance (14th):** When you must make a death saving throw at the start of your turn, you can instead spring to your feet with a burst of radiant energy. You regain HP equal to half your HP maximum, then stand up. Each creature of your choice within 30 feet takes 2d8 + CHA modifier radiant damage and is blinded until the end of your current turn. Once per long rest.
