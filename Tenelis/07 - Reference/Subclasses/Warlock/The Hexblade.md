# The Hexblade

The Hexblade warlock has made a pact with a mysterious entity from the Shadowfell that manifests through sentient weapons, granting the power to curse enemies and wield weapons with supernatural skill.

**Class:** [[Warlock]] | **Source:** XGtE | **Subclass Features at:** 1st, 6th, 10th, 14th level

## Key Features
- **Expanded Spell List (1st):** You gain additional spell options: shield and [[Wrathful Smite|wrathful smite]] (1st), blur and [[Branding Smite|branding smite]] (2nd), blink and [[Elemental Weapon|elemental weapon]] (3rd), [[Phantasmal Killer|phantasmal killer]] and [[Staggering Smite|staggering smite]] (4th), [[Banishing Smite|banishing smite]] and [[Cone of Cold|cone of cold]] (5th).
- **Hexblade's Curse (1st):** As a bonus action, curse a creature within 30 feet for 1 minute. You gain a bonus to damage rolls against it equal to your proficiency bonus, your attacks against it score a critical hit on a 19 or 20, and if it dies, you regain hit points equal to your warlock level + CHA modifier. Once per short or long rest.
- **Hex Warrior (1st):** You gain proficiency with medium armor, shields, and martial weapons. When you attack with a weapon you're proficient with that lacks the two-handed property, you can use CHA instead of STR or DEX for attack and damage rolls. At each level up, this benefit extends to your pact weapon regardless of type.
- **Accursed Specter (6th):** When you slay a humanoid, you can cause its spirit to rise as a specter that gains temporary hit points equal to half your warlock level. It obeys your commands, acts on its own initiative, and lasts until the end of your next long rest. Once per long rest.
- **Armor of Hexes (10th):** When the target of your Hexblade's Curse hits you with an attack, you can roll a d6. On a 4 or higher, the attack misses you instead, regardless of the attack roll.
- **Master of Hexes (14th):** When the creature cursed by your Hexblade's Curse dies, you can apply the curse to a different creature you can see within 30 feet. You don't regain hit points from the previous target's death when transferring the curse.
