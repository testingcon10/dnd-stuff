# Scout

The Scout rogue is a skilled explorer and ambusher, excelling at skirmishing ahead of companions to gather information and strike swiftly before fading away.

**Class:** [[Rogue]] | **Source:** XGtE | **Subclass Features at:** 3rd, 9th, 13th, 17th level

## Key Features
- **Skirmisher (3rd):** When an enemy ends its turn within 5 feet of you, you can use your reaction to move up to half your speed without provoking opportunity attacks.
- **Survivalist (3rd):** You gain proficiency in Nature and Survival. Your proficiency bonus is doubled for any ability check you make that uses either of those proficiencies (expertise).
- **Superior Mobility (9th):** Your walking speed increases by 10 feet. If you have a climbing or swimming speed, it also increases by 10 feet.
- **Ambush Master (13th):** You have advantage on initiative rolls. The first creature you hit during the first round of combat becomes easier for your allies to strike: attack rolls against it have advantage until the start of your next turn.
- **Sudden Strike (17th):** If you take the Attack action on your turn, you can make one additional attack as a bonus action. This attack can benefit from Sneak Attack even if you already dealt Sneak Attack damage this turn, but only if the bonus attack targets a different creature.
