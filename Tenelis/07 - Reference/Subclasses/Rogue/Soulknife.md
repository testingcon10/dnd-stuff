# Soulknife

A Soulknife rogue strikes with the mind, cutting through physical and psychic barriers with blades formed of pure psychic energy. Their psionic talents make them versatile operatives.

**Class:** [[Rogue]] | **Source:** TCoE | **Subclass Features at:** 3rd, 9th, 13th, 17th level

## Key Features
- **Psionic Power (3rd):** You gain a number of Psionic Energy dice (d6) equal to twice your proficiency bonus. These recharge on a long rest (one die recoverable as a bonus action per short rest). The die size increases to d8 (5th), d10 (11th), d12 (17th).
- **Psi-Bolstered Knack (3rd):** When you fail an ability check using a skill or tool you're proficient with, you can roll a Psionic Energy die and add it to the result (possibly turning failure into success). The die is only expended if the check then succeeds.
- **Psychic Whispers (3rd):** As an action, choose a number of creatures up to your proficiency bonus within 1 mile. You establish telepathic communication with each for a number of hours equal to the Psionic Energy die rolled. Usable free once per long rest, or by expending a die.
- **Psychic Blades (3rd):** When you take the Attack action, you manifest a psychic blade (1d6 psychic, finesse, thrown 60ft). If you attack with it, you can make a bonus action attack with a second blade (1d4 psychic). Both blades vanish after the attack.
- **Soul Blades (9th):** Homing Strikes: when you miss with a Psychic Blade, roll a Psionic Energy die and add it to the attack roll (die only expended if you then hit). Psychic Teleportation: as a bonus action, throw one blade up to 10x the Psionic Energy die roll in feet and teleport to it.
- **Psychic Veil (13th):** As an action, become invisible for 1 hour or until you deal damage or force a save. Usable free once per long rest, or by expending a Psionic Energy die.
- **Rend Mind (17th):** When you deal Sneak Attack damage with your Psychic Blades, the target must make a Wisdom save (DC 8 + proficiency + Dexterity modifier) or be stunned for 1 minute (save at end of each turn). Usable free once per long rest, or by expending three Psionic Energy dice.
