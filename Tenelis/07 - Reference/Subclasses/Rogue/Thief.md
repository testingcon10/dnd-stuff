# Thief

The Thief archetype hones skills in the larcenous arts. Thieves are among the most agile and stealthy rogues, excelling at infiltration and burglary.

**Class:** [[Rogue]] | **Source:** PHB | **Subclass Features at:** 3rd, 9th, 13th, 17th level

## Key Features
- **Fast Hands (3rd):** Use Cunning Action to make Sleight of Hand checks, use thieves' tools to disarm a trap or open a lock, or take the Use an Object action.
- **Second-Story Work (3rd):** Climbing no longer costs you extra movement. In addition, when you make a running jump, the distance you cover increases by a number of feet equal to your DEX modifier.
- **Supreme Sneak (9th):** You have advantage on Stealth checks if you move no more than half your speed on the same turn.
- **Use Magic Device (13th):** You ignore all class, race, and level requirements on the use of magic items.
- **Thief's Reflexes (17th):** You can take two turns during the first round of any combat. You take your first turn at your normal initiative and your second turn at your initiative minus 10.
