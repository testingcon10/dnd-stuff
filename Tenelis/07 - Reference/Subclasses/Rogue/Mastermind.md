# Mastermind

The Mastermind rogue focuses on people and influence, expertly manipulating others from behind the scenes while maintaining an impenetrable web of deception.

**Class:** [[Rogue]] | **Source:** XGtE | **Subclass Features at:** 3rd, 9th, 13th, 17th level

## Key Features
- **Master of Intrigue (3rd):** You gain proficiency with the disguise kit, the forgery kit, and one gaming set of your choice. You also learn two languages. You can unerringly mimic the speech patterns and accent of a creature you hear speak for at least 1 minute.
- **Master of Tactics (3rd):** You can use the Help action as a bonus action. Additionally, when you use the Help action to aid an ally in attacking a creature, the target of that attack can be within 30 feet of you rather than within 5 feet.
- **Insightful Manipulator (9th):** After spending at least 1 minute observing or interacting with a creature outside of combat, you learn whether it is your equal, superior, or inferior with regard to two of the following characteristics: Intelligence score, Wisdom score, Charisma score, or class levels (if any).
- **Misdirection (13th):** When you are targeted by an attack while a creature is providing you cover, you can use your reaction to redirect the attack to that creature instead.
- **Soul of Deceit (17th):** Your thoughts can't be read by telepathy or other means unless you allow it. You can present false thoughts. No matter what you say, magic that determines if you are telling the truth indicates you are being truthful. You are immune to being compelled to tell the truth by magic.
